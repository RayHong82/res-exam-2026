# P1.1 The Real Estate Agency Industry & Regulatory Framework (CEA, Estate Agents Act)

## P1.1 The Real Estate Agency Industry & Regulatory Framework (CEA, Estate Agents Act)
### RES Paper 1 — Revision Notes / 复习笔记
**Exam sitting: 18 July 2026 | All figures verified against CEA primary sources (current as of June 2026)**

> **Currency rule / 时效规则:** You will **NOT** be tested on policy changes implemented within **~1 month** before the exam (anything after ~18 June 2026). Use the figures below.

---

## (a) Key Concepts / 核心概念

This topic is about **WHO regulates the industry, WHAT law governs it, WHO the players are, and HOW misconduct is punished**. It is heavily knowledge/comprehension-based — expect definition swaps, "EXCEPT" inversions, and figure-matching distractors.

| Concept | One-line definition |
|---|---|
| **CEA** (Council for Estate Agencies) | The statutory regulator of the real estate agency industry; a statutory board under **MND**. |
| **EAA 2010** (Estate Agents Act) | The principal statute that created CEA and the licensing/registration regime. |
| **EA** (Estate Agent) | The **firm/company** — must be **licensed**. |
| **RES / Salesperson** | The **individual** — must be **registered** (one EA only). |
| **KEO** (Key Executive Officer) | The person who manages and is responsible for the EA's conduct. |
| **CEPCC** | Code of Ethics & Professional Client Care — **individual-level** conduct rules (toward clients/public). |
| **COPEA** | Code of Practice for Estate Agencies — **firm-level** management/supervision rules. |
| **CPD** | Continuing Professional Development — mandatory annual training, condition of renewal. |

**中文要点:** CEA 是行业监管机构（隶属国家发展部 MND）；EAA 2010 是主法。记牢 **EA = 公司（持牌 licensed）、RES = 个人（注册 registered）**——这是最常考的混淆点。CEPCC 管个人，COPEA 管公司。

---

## (b) Must-Know Rules & Exact Figures / 必背规则与精确数字

### B1. CEA — establishment, status, functions

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| Established | **22 October 2010** |
| Licensing regime began | **1 January 2011** |
| Parent ministry | **MND** (Ministry of National Development) — statutory board |
| Administers | **Estate Agents Act 2010**, CEPCC, COPEA, exams, CPD, discipline |
| **3 strategic thrusts** | (1) Effective **Regulation** (2) Industry **Development** (3) Consumer **Education** |

*中文note:* CEA 成立于 2010 年 10 月 22 日，发牌制度 2011 年 1 月 1 日开始；三大支柱 = 监管、发展、教育。

### B2. The legislation

| Statute | Role |
|---|---|
| **Estate Agents Act 2010 (EAA)** | Principal statute |
| **Estate Agents (Estate Agency Work) Regulations 2010** | Subsidiary; prescribes CEPCC & COPEA (Second Schedule) |
| **Estate Agents (Dispute Resolution Schemes) Regulations 2011** | Mediation + adjudication scheme |
| **AML and Other Matters (Estate Agents and Developers) Act 2025** | Passed **8 Apr 2025**, **operational 1 Jul 2025**; strengthens AML/CFT; raised max penalties to "**per breach**" basis |

*中文note:* 主法是 EAA 2010；2025 年新增反洗钱法（2025 年 7 月 1 日生效），把 AML 罚款改为「每项违规 per breach」计算。

### B3. The players — eligibility

| Role | Key eligibility |
|---|---|
| **EA (firm)** | Must hold a valid **licence** from CEA. |
| **RES (individual)** | ≥21 yrs; 4 GCE 'O' Level passes **OR** WPLN Level 5+ in all 5 components; pass RES exam; **single-agency rule** (only 1 EA); no moneylending/moonlighting. |
| **KEO** | ≥21 yrs; **≥3 yrs** as salesperson; **≥30 transactions in past 3 yrs (≥10 private + ≥10 HDB)**; 4 'O' Levels + valid REA exam; fit-and-proper; cannot concurrently be RES / sole proprietor / director of another EA. |

| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| RES application fee | **$60** |
| RES registration (on/before 30 Jun) | **$280** |
| RES registration (after 30 Jun) | **$140** (pro-rated) |

*中文note:* EA 持牌、RES 注册。RES「单一中介原则」——只能挂靠一家公司。KEO 门槛高：3 年经验 + 过去 3 年 ≥30 宗交易（私宅 ≥10、组屋 ≥10）。

### B4. RES Exam format (P1.1 often tests this directly)

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| Papers | **2** (Paper 1 & Paper 2), each passed independently |
| Per paper | Section A: **60 MCQs = 60 marks**; Section B: **20 MCQs (case study) = 40 marks** → **80 MCQs = 100 marks** |
| Format | **100% multiple-choice**, single-best-answer (short-answer removed since 2025) |
| Duration | **2.5 hours per paper** |
| **Pass mark** | **60 / 100 per paper (60%)** — "subject to review by CEA" |
| Attendance (course) | **75%** course attendance required — *this is NOT the pass mark* |
| Administrator | **NTUC LearningHub** |
| Cert of Course Completion validity | **2 years** |
| Validity of a pass | Must register as RES within **2 years**, else retake course + exam |

> **TRAP:** Pass mark = **60%**, NOT 75%. The **75%** figure is **course attendance**.

*中文note:* 每卷 100 分，**及格线 60 分（60%）**；75% 是**上课出勤率**，不是及格线——这是最经典的陷阱。两份卷各自独立通过；考试通过后须在 **2 年内**注册成为 RES。

### B5. CEPCC vs COPEA

| | **CEPCC** | **COPEA** |
|---|---|---|
| Full name | Code of Ethics & Professional Client Care | Code of Practice for Estate Agencies |
| Level | **Individual (RES)** | **Firm (EA)** |
| Source | EAW Regulations 2010 | **Second Schedule**, EAW Regulations 2010 |
| Coverage | **12 areas** — legal compliance, due diligence, duties to clients/public, document execution, prescribed agreements, offer/counter-offer, translation, advertising, **conflict-of-interest avoidance**, referral to professional advice, confidentiality | **4 areas** — management & supervision, Estate Agent Card, claims/complaints procedures, **document & records retention** |
| Record retention | — | **≥5 years** (COPEA Para 7(1); raised from 3 yrs to align with AML/CFT) |

*中文note:* CEPCC = 个人（12 项）；COPEA = 公司（4 项）。文件保存期 **≥5 年**（与反洗钱对齐，原为 3 年）。

### B6. Conflict of interest & dual representation

| Scenario | Rule |
|---|---|
| **Dual representation** | **An OFFENCE** — one RES collecting/sharing fees from **BOTH** sides of the same transaction. (Real case: **$9,000 fine**.) |
| Two RES from the **same EA** on opposite sides | **NOT** dual rep, **provided** neither collects from both sides; both must **declare in writing** they are from the same EA AND obtain **written consent**. |
| Overriding/referral fees | Must be **disclosed in writing** as a potential conflict. |

*中文note:* 「双重代理」= 同一名经纪从交易**双方**收钱 = 违法。但同一公司的**两名不同**经纪分别代表买卖双方可以，前提是各收各的、并书面披露 + 取得书面同意。

### B7. CPD — 2026 framework (HIGHLY EXAMINABLE / 高频考点)

| Cycle | Requirement |
|---|---|
| **2025 (outgoing)** | 6 credits, Oct–Sep cycle |
| **2026 onwards (Project ADEPT, eff. 1 Jan 2026)** | **16 training hours/year** = **12 h Structured Learning (SL)** + **4 h Self-Directed Learning (SDL)** |
| Within the 12 h SL | **≥4 h must be Prescribed Essentials (PE)** |
| **2026 PE topic** | **PMLPFTF** (Prevention of Money Laundering, Proliferation Financing & Terrorism Financing) |
| Cycle period | Permanently **Jan–Dec** |
| New RES | Exempt in 1st cycle |
| Consequence | CPD is a **condition of annual registration renewal** |

> **Memory hook:** **16 = 12 + 4**, and inside the 12, **≥4 is Prescribed Essentials**. So the smallest mandated topic block = **4 h PE on AML (PMLPFTF)**.

*中文note:* 2026 起从「学分」改为「学时」：**全年 16 小时 = 结构化学习 12 小时（其中 ≥4 小时为「必修要点 PE」）+ 自主学习 4 小时**。2026 年 PE 主题是反洗钱 PMLPFTF。周期改为每年 1 月–12 月。

### B8. Disciplinary process & penalties (HIGH-FREQUENCY FIGURES)

| Track | Body | Max financial penalty |
|---|---|---|
| **Less serious** (non-criminal) | **Letter of Censure (LOC)** | **$5,000** — **per case** (general) / **per breach** (AML, on/after 1 Jul 2025) |
| **Serious** | **Disciplinary Committee (DC)** — ≥3 members from CEA Disciplinary Panel | **RES: $100,000**; **EA: $200,000** — *per case* (other breaches) / *per breach* (AML, on/after 1 Jul 2025) |

**Enforcement ladder:** LOW (Letter of Warning) → **LOC** → **DC**.

**DC powers:** revoke/suspend licence or registration, vary conditions, impose financial penalty, reprimand.

**DC procedural timeline (all 14-day steps):**
Statement of Case → respondent **Admission/Defence (14 days)** → **Reply (14 days)** → **Rejoinder (14 days)** → hearing → sentencing → **Appeal to MND Appeals Board within 14 days**.

*中文note:* 轻微违规走「谴责信 LOC」，上限 **$5,000**；严重违规走「纪律委员会 DC」，**RES 上限 $10 万、EA 上限 $20 万**。AML 违规（2025/7/1 起）按「每项 per breach」算，其余按「每宗 per case」。上诉去**国家发展部上诉委员会**，限 **14 天**。

### B9. Complaints & dispute resolution

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| Complaint to CEA hotline | **1800-643-2555** |
| What CEA does | Handles **discipline only** — **does NOT recover money** for consumers |
| First step (EA's duty) | EA must meet complainant to seek **amicable resolution** (COPEA) |
| **CEA Dispute Resolution Scheme** (Regs 2011) | **Mediation** (via Singapore Mediation Centre) + **Adjudication/arbitration** |
| **Small Claims Tribunals** | Up to **$20,000**; up to **$30,000** if both parties sign a **Memorandum of Consent** |

> **TRAP:** CEA disciplines errant agents but **cannot order compensation / recover money** for a consumer — that goes to mediation/adjudication or the courts/SCT.

*中文note:* CEA 只管纪律处分，**不替消费者追讨金钱**。索赔走调解/仲裁或小额索偿庭（**上限 $2 万，双方签署同意书（Memorandum of Consent）可到 $3 万**）。

### B10. Estate agency agreements (8 prescribed forms) — overlaps with Paper 2 but commonly tested

| Form | Type | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| 1 / 2 | Non-exclusive | Sell / Buy residential |
| 3 / 4 | Non-exclusive | Lease — landlord / tenant |
| 5 / 6 | Exclusive | Sell / Buy residential |
| 7 / 8 | Exclusive | Lease — landlord / tenant |

| Exclusive Estate Agency Agreement (EAA) | Figure |
|---|---|
| Max validity | **3 months** (no auto-renew) |
| Post-expiry commission "tail" | Commission still payable if sold within **3 months** after expiry to a buyer the agent **introduced during** the validity period |

**Commission:** **NOT fixed/regulated by CEA — fully negotiable** and documented in the EAA.

*中文note:* 共 **8 份**规定表格（1–4 非独家、5–8 独家）；独家协议最长 **3 个月**，到期后 **3 个月**内卖给协议期内引荐的买家仍要付佣金。**佣金不由 CEA 规定，完全可协商**。

## (c) Common Exam Traps / 易错点

1. **Pass mark = 60%, NOT 75%.** 75% = course attendance. **及格 60 分，出勤 75%，别混。**
2. **EA = licensed firm; RES = registered individual.** Don't swap "licensed" and "registered". **公司持牌、个人注册。**
3. **CEPCC (individual, 12 areas) vs COPEA (firm, 4 areas).** Watch swapped numbers/scope. **CEPCC 个人 12 项、COPEA 公司 4 项。**
4. **CEA is under MND, NOT MAS / MinLaw.** **CEA 隶属国家发展部 MND。**
5. **CPD 2026 = 16 h (12 SL + 4 SDL), ≥4 h of the SL is PE.** Distractors will show "6 credits" (that's 2025) or "16 h all structured". **16 = 12+4，PE ≥4 在 SL 内。**
6. **Dual representation = collecting from BOTH sides = offence.** Two agents from same firm on opposite sides is allowed (with disclosure + consent). **双重代理 = 一人两头收钱 = 违法。**
7. **LOC max $5,000; DC max RES $100k / EA $200k.** Don't swap RES and EA caps. **LOC 5 千；DC：RES 10 万、EA 20 万。**
8. **CEA does NOT recover money for consumers.** **CEA 不追讨金钱。**
9. **AML penalties = "per breach" from 1 Jul 2025**; older breaches = "per case". **反洗钱罚款 2025/7/1 起按每项算。**
10. **Record retention = 5 years (raised from 3).** **文件保存 5 年。**
11. **Exclusive EAA = 3 months max + 3-month tail.** Don't confuse the two 3-month periods. **独家 3 个月 + 到期后 3 个月佣金尾巴。**
12. **"EXCEPT / NOT / EXCLUDING" stems** — read carefully; the correct answer is the odd-one-out. **看清「除了/不是」的反向问法。**

## (d) Worked Examples / 计算例题

P1.1 is mostly conceptual, but two calculation-style items can appear:

**Example 1 — CPD hours allocation (2026)**
*Q: An RES completes 12 h structured learning (of which 5 h is Prescribed Essentials) and 3 h self-directed learning. Has she met the 2026 CPD requirement?*

- Required: **16 h total** = **12 h SL** (≥4 h PE) + **4 h SDL**.
- Her SL = 12 h ✔ (PE 5 h ≥ 4 h ✔), but **SDL = 3 h < 4 h ✘**, and **total = 15 h < 16 h ✘**.
- **Answer: NO** — she is short by **1 h of SDL**.

*中文:* 结构化达标（含 PE 5h≥4h），但自主学习只有 3h（需 4h），总数 15h（需 16h）——**不达标，差 1 小时自主学习**。

**Example 2 — Disciplinary penalty cap (per breach, AML)**
*Q: An EA firm commits 3 separate AML/CFT breaches after 1 Jul 2025 and is referred to the DC. What is the theoretical maximum financial penalty?*

- AML breaches on/after 1 Jul 2025 are penalised **per breach**.
- EA cap = **$200,000 per breach** × 3 = **$600,000**.
- **Answer: $600,000.**

*中文:* 2025/7/1 起 AML 按「每项」计：EA 每项上限 $20 万 × 3 = **$60 万**。（若是 RES 则 $10 万 × 3 = $30 万。）

## P1.2 — Code of Ethics & Professional Client Care (CEPCC) + Code of Practice (COPEA)
**RES Paper 1 | Exam: 18 July 2026 | Verified against CEA primary sources (current as of June 2026)**

> **Currency rule:** You will NOT be tested on government/CEA policy changes implemented within ~1 month of the exam (after ~18 June 2026). Use the figures below.

## (a) Key Concepts / 核心概念

### The two regulatory instruments — know the difference cold

| | **CEPCC** | **COPEA** |
|---|---|---|
| **Full name** | Code of Ethics & Professional Client Care | Code of Practice for Estate Agents |
| **Who it binds** | **Individuals** — Estate Agents (EAs as licensees) **and** RES (salespersons) | **The firm (EA)** — operational/management standards |
| **Source of law** | Prescribed in the **Estate Agents (Estate Agency Work) Regulations 2010** | **Second Schedule** of the same Regulations |
| **Nature** | Standard of **conduct / ethics** toward clients & public | Standard of **business management / supervision** |
| **Focus** | How you behave (honesty, conflict, confidentiality, due diligence) | How the firm runs (supervision, complaints, records, EA card) |
| **Breach consequence** | Disciplinary action (LOC / DC) | Disciplinary action against the **firm** |

**中文说明：** CEPCC 管"人"（个人销售员和持牌经纪的职业操守），COPEA 管"公司"（房产中介行号的管理与监督）。两者都源自《2010 年房地产经纪（房地产经纪工作）条例》——CEPCC 是条例正文规定，COPEA 是该条例的**第二附表**。考试最常考的陷阱就是把两者对调。

### The legal hierarchy / 法律层级
1. **Estate Agents Act 2010 (EAA)** — the parent statute. CEA established 22 Oct 2010; licensing from 1 Jan 2011; regulator is a statutory board under **MND**.
2. **Estate Agents (Estate Agency Work) Regulations 2010** — subsidiary legislation → prescribes **CEPCC** (body) + **COPEA** (Second Schedule).
3. **Practice Circulars / Guidelines** issued by CEA (e.g. advertising, AML).

> **AML and Other Matters (Estate Agents and Developers) Act 2025** — passed 8 Apr 2025, **operational 1 Jul 2025**; strengthens AML/CFT, raised maximum penalties, and is why record retention is now **5 years**.

**中文note：** EAA 2010 是母法 → 2010 条例是子法 → CEPCC 和 COPEA 都在子法里。

## (b) Must-Know Rules & Exact Figures / 必背规则与数字

### CEPCC — the 12 conduct areas (个人操守 12 项)

| # | Area | 中文 |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Knowledge of & compliance with relevant laws, circulars, guidelines | 知法守法 |
| 2 | Due diligence / verification of material facts | 尽职调查 |
| 3 | Duties to clients (act in client's best interest, competently) | 对客户的义务 |
| 4 | Duties to the public (honesty, no misrepresentation) | 对公众的义务 |
| 5 | Proper execution of documents | 正确签署文件 |
| 6 | Use of **prescribed Estate Agency Agreements (EAA forms 1–8)** | 使用规定协议表格 |
| 7 | Handling **offer & counter-offer** properly / promptly | 处理报价与还价 |
| 8 | **Translation** of documents where client doesn't understand | 文件翻译 |
| 9 | Advertising — accurate, with required particulars, owner's consent | 广告规范 |
| 10 | **Avoidance of conflict of interest** | 避免利益冲突 |
| 11 | Referral of clients to independent professional advice (legal/financial) | 转介专业意见 |
| 12 | **Confidentiality** of client information | 保密 |

**中文note：** 这 12 项的核心三关键词：诚信 (honesty)、尽职 (due diligence)、避免利益冲突 (conflict of interest)。

### COPEA — the 4 firm-level areas (公司层面 4 项)

| # | Area | Key figure / 中文 |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Management & supervision** of salespersons | KEO supervises; firm responsible for RES conduct |
| 2 | **Estate Agent Card** issuance & control | Every RES must carry/display valid card |
| 3 | **Claims & complaints procedure** | Firm must meet complainant first for amicable resolution |
| 4 | **Document & records retention — Para 7(1)** | **≥5 years** (raised from 3yr to align with AML/CFT); electronic OK |

**中文note：** COPEA 四大块：管理监督、经纪卡、投诉处理、文件保存（**5 年**）。记住"5 年"是高频考点。

### Conflict of Interest & Dual Representation (利益冲突与双重代理)

| Scenario | Allowed? | Rule |
|---|---|---|
| **One RES collects/shares fees from BOTH sides** of same transaction | **NO — OFFENCE** (双重代理违法) | Real disciplinary case: **$9,000 fine** |
| **Two RES from the SAME EA** on opposite sides | **YES, conditionally** | Neither may collect from both sides; **both must declare in writing they are from the same EA + obtain written consent** |
| Receiving **overriding/referral fees** | Allowed | Must be **disclosed in writing** as potential conflict |

**中文说明：** **双重代理 (dual representation) 是违法行为**——同一个 RES 不能同时向买卖双方收费。但同一家公司的**两个不同 RES** 分别代理买卖双方是**允许的**，前提是：(1) 双方书面声明来自同一家公司，(2) 取得双方书面同意，(3) 任一 RES 不得向双方同时收费。这是经典易错点——别把"同公司两个 RES"误判为违法。

### CPD — Continuing Professional Development (2026 framework, 高度可考)

| Cycle | Total | Breakdown | 中文 |
|---|---|---|---|
| **2025 (outgoing)** | 6 credits | Oct–Sep cycle | 旧制 6 学分 |
| **2026 (eff. 1 Jan 2026, "Project ADEPT")** | **16 training hours/year** | **12h Structured Learning (incl. ≥4h Prescribed Essentials) + 4h Self-Directed Learning** | 新制 16 小时 |

- **Cycle now permanently Jan–Dec** (calendar year).
- **2026 Prescribed Essentials (PE) topic = PMLPFTF** (Prevention of Money Laundering, Proliferation Financing & Terrorism Financing). *(Note: a CEPCC ethics course can earn 4h under Professional Competencies, but the mandatory PE topic for 2026 is PMLPFTF.)*
- **CPD is a condition of annual registration renewal.**
- **New RES exempt in their first cycle.**

**中文note：** 2026 起 CPD = 16 小时 = 12 小时结构化学习（其中至少 4 小时是强制的 PE，2026 年 PE 主题是反洗钱 PMLPFTF）+ 4 小时自主学习。周期改为 1–12 月。完不成 = 无法续牌。

### Disciplinary Framework & Penalties (纪律处分与罚则)

| Mechanism | Who decides | Max penalty | 中文 |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Letter of Warning (LOW)** | CEA | — (least serious) | 警告信 |
| **Letter of Censure (LOC)** | CEA | **$5,000 per case** (general) / **per breach** for AML (on/after 1 Jul 2025) | 谴责信 |
| **Disciplinary Committee (DC)** | ≥3 from CEA Disciplinary Panel | **RES $100,000 · EA $200,000** (per case general; per breach for AML) | 纪律委员会 |

- **Disciplinary ladder: LOW → LOC → DC** (escalating severity).
- **DC powers:** revoke / suspend registration or licence, vary conditions, impose financial penalty, reprimand.
- **DC process:** Statement of Case → respondent Admission/Defence (**14 days**) → Reply (14d) → Rejoinder (14d) → hearing → sentencing.
- **Appeal to the MND Appeals Board within 14 days.**

**中文note：** 处分由轻到重：LOW → LOC（最高 \$5,000）→ DC（RES 最高 \$10 万，EA 最高 \$20 万）。AML 违规按"每项"计罚（更重）。不服可在 **14 天**内向 **MND 上诉委员会**上诉。

### Complaints & Dispute Resolution (投诉与争议解决)

| Item | Detail | 中文 |
|---|---|---|
| Complaint hotline to CEA | **1800-643-2555** | 投诉热线 |
| CEA's role | Handles **discipline only** — **does NOT recover money** for consumers | CEA 只管纪律，不替消费者追讨金钱 |
| First step (COPEA) | EA must meet complainant for **amicable resolution** | 先尝试和解 |
| **CEA Dispute Resolution Scheme** (Regs 2011) | **Mediation** (via Singapore Mediation Centre) + **Adjudication/arbitration** | 调解 + 仲裁 |
| **Small Claims Tribunals** | Up to **$20,000** (up to **$30,000** if both parties sign a Memorandum of Consent) | 小额索偿 |

**中文note：** 关键陷阱——**CEA 不替消费者讨钱**！金钱纠纷走调解/仲裁/小额索偿庭。

### Exam Format anchors (与本主题相关的考试格式)

| Item | Value |
|---|---|
| **Pass mark** | **60 / 100 per paper (60%)**, subject to CEA review |
| **75%** | = RES **course attendance** requirement, **NOT** the pass mark (常见陷阱) |
| Per paper | 80 MCQs = 100 marks (Section A: 60 MCQ/60 marks; Section B: 20 case-study MCQ/40 marks); 2.5 hours |
| Validity of a pass | Register as RES within **2 years** of passing, else retake |

## (c) Common Exam Traps / 易错点

1. **CEPCC vs COPEA 对调** — CEPCC = individuals/conduct; COPEA = firm/management. COPEA is the **Second Schedule**.
2. **Pass mark = 60%, NOT 75%.** 75% is **course attendance**. The single most-set distractor.
3. **Dual representation (one RES, both sides) = OFFENCE**, but **two RES from the same EA** on opposite sides **is allowed** with written declaration + written consent. Don't conflate.
4. **CEA does NOT recover money** for consumers — it only disciplines. Money disputes → mediation/Small Claims Tribunal.
5. **Record retention = 5 years** (raised from 3), per COPEA Para 7(1) + AML alignment. Old "3 years" is now a wrong answer.
6. **CPD 2026 = 16 hours** (not the old 6 credits); within that, **≥4h must be Prescribed Essentials**, and 2026 PE topic = **PMLPFTF (AML)**. Cycle = Jan–Dec.
7. **LOC cap = $5,000; DC caps = $100k (RES) / $200k (EA).** For **AML breaches** these are **per breach** (post 1 Jul 2025), not per case — heavier.
8. **Appeal window & each DC pleading stage = 14 days.** Appeals go to the **MND Appeals Board**, not to CEA itself.
9. **Source of law:** CEPCC/COPEA are prescribed under the **Regulations 2010**, which are subsidiary to the **EAA 2010** (the Act). Don't say the Act itself "contains" the Code text.
10. **"EXCEPT / NOT" inversions** — read the stem carefully; conduct questions love negative phrasing.
11. **Overriding/referral fees are legal IF disclosed in writing** — not automatically a breach.
12. **New RES are exempt from CPD in their first cycle** — a common "must complete 16h immediately" trap.

## (d) Worked Examples / 计算示例

This topic is largely qualitative, but two computational/threshold scenarios commonly appear:

**Example 1 — CPD compliance check (2026):**
An RES completes 10h Structured Learning (of which 3h is Prescribed Essentials) + 5h Self-Directed Learning in 2026. Compliant?
- Total = 15h < required **16h** → **fail on total**.
- SL = 10h < required **12h** → **fail on SL**.
- PE = 3h < required **4h** → **fail on PE**.
- SDL = 5h ≥ required **4h** → **SDL met** (the surplus 1h CANNOT offset the SL/PE shortfall — each component is a separate minimum).
- **Result: NON-compliant on three counts** (total, SL, PE) → registration renewal blocked.
- 中文：三项全不达标——总时数、结构化学习、强制 PE 都不够。

**Example 2 — Penalty routing:**
An RES collects commission from **both** buyer and seller in one transaction (dual representation). Which penalty regime?
- Dual representation = **offence** → typically referred to **DC** (serious). DC max for RES = **$100,000**; can also suspend/revoke. (Illustrative real case: **$9,000 fine**.) Not a mere LOC if egregious.
- 中文：双重代理属严重违规 → 交纪律委员会 (DC)，RES 最高罚 \$10 万，并可吊销/暂停注册。

**Example 3 — Pass/fail:**
Candidate scores 59/100 on Paper 1. Pass?
- Pass mark = **60/100**. 59 < 60 → **fail** (must re-sit that paper). Course attendance (75%) is irrelevant to the score.
- 中文：59 分 < 60 分 = 不及格，需补考该卷。出席率 75% 与分数无关。

- **CEPCC = 人/操守** (individuals' ethics) · **COPEA = 公司/管理** (firm's practice, **Second Schedule**). Both from **EAW Regulations 2010**, under **EAA 2010**.
- **CEPCC 12 areas** core trio: honesty · due diligence · conflict-of-interest avoidance. Also: prescribed agreements, offer/counter-offer, translation, advertising, confidentiality, refer to pro advice.

## P1.3 Licensing, Registration, CPD, Disciplinary Action & Dispute Resolution
**RES Paper 1 — Exam-Focused Revision Notes | Sitting: 18 July 2026**
*All figures verified against CEA primary sources (current as of June 2026). Currency rule: you will NOT be tested on changes implemented within ~1 month before the exam (after ~18 June 2026).*

## (a) Key Concepts / 核心概念

This topic governs **who may legally do estate agency work, how they stay qualified, and what happens when things go wrong**. The whole regime sits under one statute and one regulator.

| Concept | Meaning | 中文说明 |
|---|---|---|
| **Estate Agency Work** | Doing/offering to do work introducing/negotiating a property transaction for a fee. Anyone doing this must be licensed (EA) or registered (RES). | 任何为收取报酬而介绍、协商房产交易的工作，都必须持牌或注册。 |
| **EA (Estate Agent)** | The **firm/company** — it is **LICENSED**. | 公司层面，"持牌"。 |
| **RES (Real Estate Salesperson)** | The **individual** — he/she is **REGISTERED** under exactly one EA. | 个人层面，"注册"，且只能挂靠一家公司。 |
| **KEO (Key Executive Officer)** | The senior person who runs and is responsible for the EA's day-to-day estate agency work and compliance. | 公司的主要负责人，对公司合规负责。 |
| **Single-Agency Rule** | An RES may be registered with **only ONE EA at a time**. | RES只能注册在一家中介公司，不能同时挂两家。 |
| **CPD** | Continuing Professional Development — annual training to keep registration alive. | 持续专业进修，是续牌的强制条件。 |
| **Fit-and-Proper** | Character/integrity test applied at registration and renewal (no relevant convictions, undischarged bankruptcy, etc.). | "适当人选"标准：品行端正，无相关犯罪记录、未破产。 |
| **Disciplinary ladder** | LOW (warning) → LOC (censure + small penalty) → DC (serious cases, large penalty/suspension/revocation). | 纪律阶梯：警告→谴责信→纪律委员会。 |

**Critical legal distinction to lock in:** **EA = LICENSED (firm); RES = REGISTERED (individual).** Exam distractors swap these constantly. 公司"持牌(licensed)"，个人"注册(registered)"——千万别搞反。

## (b) Must-Know Rules & Exact Figures / 必背规则与数字

### 1. The Regulator & Statute

| Item | Fact |
|---|---|
| Regulator | **CEA (Council for Estate Agencies)** — established **22 Oct 2010**; statutory board under **MND**. |
| Licensing regime started | **1 Jan 2011** |
| Principal statute | **Estate Agents Act 2010 (EAA)** |
| Key subsidiary legislation | Estate Agents (Estate Agency Work) Regulations 2010 → prescribes **CEPCC** & **COPEA**; Estate Agents (Dispute Resolution Schemes) Regulations 2011 |
| Major 2025 update | **AML and Other Matters (Estate Agents and Developers) Act 2025** — passed 8 Apr 2025, **operational 1 Jul 2025**; strengthens AML/CFT, raises max penalties to **per-breach** basis |
| CEA's 3 strategic thrusts | **Effective Regulation · Industry Development · Consumer Education** |

中文note：CEA是房地产中介行业的唯一监管机构，2010年10月22日成立，归MND管，执行《2010年地产代理法》。

### 2. RES Registration — Eligibility & Fees

| Item | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Minimum age | **≥ 21 years** |
| Academic | **4 GCE 'O' Level passes** (within max 2 sittings) **OR WPLN Level 5+** in all 5 components |
| Exam | Pass the **RES Examination** |
| Course | Valid **Certificate of RES Course Completion** (75% attendance; valid **2 years**) |
| Character | Pass **fit-and-proper** test |
| Single-agency | Registered with **ONE EA only**; no moneylending / moonlighting |
| Validity of an exam pass | Must register as RES within **2 years** of passing, else retake course + exam |

**RES Fees (paid by the EA, not the salesperson):**

| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| Application fee (new) | **~S$53.50** (incl. GST) |
| Annual registration fee | **S$230** (full year, or part-year ending 31 Dec if started **on/before 30 Jun**); **half (~S$140)** if started **after 30 Jun** |

中文note：RES注册门槛——满21岁、4科O水准(或WPLN 5级)、通过考试、75%出勤的课程证书(有效2年)、品行合格、只挂一家公司。考试通过后必须**2年内**注册，否则作废。

### 3. KEO Eligibility

| Requirement | Detail |
|---|---|
| Age | ≥ 21 years |
| Experience | **≥ 3 years** as a salesperson **AND ≥ 30 transactions** in past 3 years (**≥10 private + ≥10 HDB**) |
| Academic | 4 GCE 'O' Levels |
| Exam | Valid **REA (Real Estate Agency) exam** pass (within 2 years) |
| Character | Fit-and-proper |
| Exclusivity | **Cannot** concurrently be an RES, sole proprietor, or director of **another** EA |

中文note：KEO要求3年经验+30宗交易(私宅、组屋各≥10宗)，且不能同时是别家中介的RES/老板/董事。

### 4. Codes of Conduct — CEPCC vs COPEA (high-yield distinction)

| | **CEPCC** | **COPEA** |
|---|---|---|
| Full name | Code of Ethics & Professional Client Care | Code of Practice for Estate Agents |
| Targets | The **individual / conduct toward clients & public** | The **firm (EA) — management level** |
| Scope | **12 areas**: legal compliance, due diligence, duties to clients/public, document execution, prescribed agreements, offer/counter-offer, translation, advertising, **conflict-of-interest avoidance**, referral to professional advice, confidentiality | **4 areas**: management & supervision, Estate Agent Card, claims/complaints procedures, **document & records retention** |
| Key number | — | **Record retention ≥ 5 years** (Para 7(1); raised from 3 to align with AML/CFT) |

中文note：**CEPCC管"人"(对客户/公众的职业操守，12项)；COPEA管"公司"(管理层面，4项)。** 文件保存**≥5年**(COPEA第7(1)条)。

### 5. CPD — 2026 Framework (HIGHLY EXAMINABLE / Project ADEPT)

| | **2025 (outgoing)** | **2026 (eff. 1 Jan 2026) — USE THIS** |
|---|---|---|
| Total requirement | 6 credits | **16 training hours / year** |
| Breakdown | — | **12h Structured Learning (SL)** + **4h Self-Directed Learning (SDL)** |
| Within Structured Learning | — | **≥ 4h Prescribed Essentials (PE)** + remaining 8h Professional Competencies |
| 2026 PE topic | — | **PMLPFTF** (Prevention of Money Laundering, Proliferation Financing & Terrorism Financing) |
| Cycle | Oct–Sep | **Jan–Dec (permanent)** |
| New RES | — | **Exempt in 1st registration cycle** |
| Consequence | CPD is a **condition of annual registration renewal** | same |

> **MEMORY HOOK: 16 = 12 + 4, and within the 12 → ≥4 PE.** Think **"16/12/4 + 4PE"**.

中文note：2026年起CPD改为每年**16小时 = 12小时结构化学习(其中≥4小时"指定必修"PE) + 4小时自主学习(SDL)**；周期改为**1–12月**；2026年PE主题是**反洗钱(PMLPFTF)**；新RES第一个周期豁免。完不成CPD就不能续牌。

### 6. Disciplinary Action — Penalties (must memorise exact dollars)

| Forum | Used for | Max financial penalty | Other powers |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Letter of Warning (LOW)** | Minor/first issues | — | Warning only |
| **Letter of Censure (LOC)** | Less serious, **non-criminal** breaches | **$5,000 per case** (general); **$5,000 per breach** for AML breaches committed **on/after 1 Jul 2025** | Censure |
| **Disciplinary Committee (DC)** | Serious breaches; ≥3 members from CEA Disciplinary Panel | **RES: $100,000** · **EA: $200,000** (per **case** for general breaches; per **breach** for AML on/after 1 Jul 2025) | **Revoke / suspend** registration/licence, vary conditions, reprimand, financial penalty |

**DC process & timeline:** Statement of Case → respondent **Admission/Defence (14 days)** → **Reply (14d)** → **Rejoinder (14d)** → hearing → sentencing. **Appeal to the MND Appeals Board within 14 days.**

中文note：纪律阶梯 **LOW → LOC → DC**。谴责信(LOC)上限**$5,000**；纪律委员会(DC)上限 **RES $10万 / EA $20万**。一般违规按"每案(per case)"计，**反洗钱违规(2025年7月1日起)按"每项(per breach)"计** ——所以反洗钱罚款可叠加，远超上限。上诉14天内向**MND上诉委员会**提出。

### 7. Conflict of Interest & Dual Representation

| Rule | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Dual representation** | **An OFFENCE** — one RES collecting/sharing fees from **BOTH** sides of the **same** transaction (real case: **$9,000 fine**) |
| Two RES, **same EA**, opposite sides | **NOT** dual rep — **provided** neither collects from both sides; both must **declare in writing** they are from the same EA **AND** obtain the parties' **written consent** |
| Overriding/referral fees | Must be **disclosed in writing** as a potential conflict |

中文note：**同一个RES从交易双方都收钱 = 违法(双重代理)。** 同公司两个RES代理买卖双方是允许的——只要各收各的一方报酬、书面声明同属一家公司并取得双方书面同意。

### 8. Complaints & Dispute Resolution

| Item | Fact |
|---|---|
| Complaint to CEA | Hotline **1800-643-2555** |
| **CEA's role** | Handles **discipline only** — **CEA does NOT recover money** for consumers |
| First step (COPEA) | EA must first meet complainant to attempt **amicable resolution** |
| **CEA Dispute Resolution Scheme** (Regs 2011) | Two sub-schemes: **(1) Mediation** (via Singapore Mediation Centre) → **(2) Arbitration** (binding). Max disputed amount **$60,000** |
| **Small Claims Tribunals (SCT)** | Claims up to **$20,000**; up to **$30,000** if both parties sign a **Memorandum of Consent** |

中文note：投诉CEA → CEA只管**纪律处分**，**不替消费者追讨金钱**。CEA争议解决机制 = **先调解(Mediation, 由SMC)、不成再仲裁(Arbitration, 有约束力)**，金额上限**$60,000**。小额索偿庭(SCT)上限**$20,000**(双方签署同意书 Memorandum of Consent 可到**$30,000**)。

> **TRAP:** Mediation = facilitated, **non-binding** (mediator does NOT impose a decision). Arbitration = arbitrator makes a **binding** decision. 调解不强制、仲裁强制——别弄反。

## (c) Common Exam Traps / 易错点

1. **EA vs RES wording** — EA is **LICENSED** (firm); RES is **REGISTERED** (individual). The single most common swap. 公司持牌、个人注册。
2. **Pass mark = 60/100 (60%) per paper.** The **75% is course ATTENDANCE**, NOT the exam pass mark. Distractors offer "75%". 及格是**60分**，75%是**上课出勤率**。
3. **CPD 2026 = 16h, broken 12 SL + 4 SDL, with ≥4 PE inside the 12.** Wrong options will say "16h all structured", "6 credits" (that's 2025), or "4h PE + 12h SDL" (inverted). 别把SL和SDL/PE数字搞混。
4. **DC max penalty: RES $100,000 / EA $200,000.** Distractors swap the two or offer $5,000 (that's the **LOC**, not DC). RES十万、EA二十万；五千是LOC。
5. **"Per case" vs "per breach"** — AML breaches on/after 1 Jul 2025 are penalised **per breach** (penalties stack); other breaches are **per case**. 反洗钱按"每项"，可叠加。
6. **CEA does NOT recover money** for consumers — it only disciplines. Money recovery = SCT / civil court / arbitration. CEA不追钱。
7. **Dual representation is an OFFENCE**, but two RES from the **same firm** on opposite sides is allowed (with written declaration + consent). Don't over-generalise that "same firm = always illegal". 同一RES双收=违法；同公司两RES≠违法。
8. **Exam pass validity = 2 years** to register; **Course completion certificate validity = 2 years**; **RES exam pass / REA pass for KEO validity = 2 years.** Several "2-year" rules — read which one the stem asks. 多个"2年"，看清问哪个。
9. **Mediation (non-binding) vs Arbitration (binding)** — see trap box above.
10. **Appeal window = 14 days to MND Appeals Board** (not to CEA, not 30 days). 上诉14天，向MND。
11. **"EXCEPT / NOT" inversions** — re-read any stem containing these words before answering. 看到EXCEPT/NOT放慢。
12. **New RES are CPD-exempt only in their FIRST cycle**, not permanently. 新人只第一个周期豁免CPD。

## (d) Worked Examples / 计算例题

This topic is mostly rules-based, but a few quantitative/threshold checks appear.

**Example 1 — CPD hours allocation (most likely calculation in P1.3)**
*An RES attends, in 2026: 5h of a Professional Competencies course, 4h of the Prescribed Essentials (PMLPFTF) course, and logs 4h reading CEA circulars/self-study. Has she met the CPD requirement?*

- Structured Learning (SL) attended = 5h (Prof. Competencies) + 4h (PE) = **9h SL** → requirement is **12h SL**. **Short by 3h.**
- PE within SL = 4h → meets the **≥4h PE** rule. ✓
- Self-Directed Learning (SDL) = 4h → meets the **4h SDL** rule. ✓
- **Verdict: NOT compliant** — she needs **3 more hours of Structured Learning** (total SL must be 12h; total = 16h).

中文：结构化学习只有9小时，差3小时——虽然PE和SDL都达标，整体仍**不合格**，还需补**3小时SL**。

**Example 2 — Penalty forum selection**
*An RES is found to have committed 3 separate AML/CDD breaches in August 2025, referred to the Disciplinary Committee. What is the theoretical maximum financial penalty?*

- Breaches are **AML** and committed **on/after 1 Jul 2025** → penalised **PER BREACH**.
- DC max for an **RES** = **$100,000 per breach**.
- 3 breaches × $100,000 = **$300,000 maximum**.

中文：反洗钱违规(2025年7月1日后)按"每项"计，RES每项上限$10万 → 3项 = 最高**$30万**。这正是"per breach"会被考的原因。

**Example 3 — Dispute forum threshold**
*A consumer disputes $25,000 in fees with an EA. Both parties sign a Memorandum of Consent to use the Small Claims Tribunals. Is SCT available?*

- SCT base limit = **$20,000**; with a **Memorandum of Consent** signed by both parties → up to **$30,000**.
- $25,000 > $20,000 (standard limit) but ≤ $30,000 with consent → **Yes, SCT can hear it** via the Memorandum of Consent. (Alternatively, CEA's arbitration scheme up to $60,000 is available.)

中文：$25,000 超过标准上限 $20,000，但双方签署同意书（Memorandum of Consent）可提高到 $30,000，故 SCT **可受理**。

# P1.4 Basic Land Law: Estates & Interests in Land, Ownership & Co-ownership

## P1.4 Basic Land Law: Estates & Interests in Land, Ownership & Co-ownership
### RES Paper 1 — Revision Notes / 复习笔记 (Exam: 18 July 2026)

> Figures verified against SLA, IRAS, AGC Statutes Online and CEA primary sources, current as of June 2026. Currency rule: you will **not** be tested on changes implemented within ~1 month before the exam (after ~18 June 2026).

## (a) Key Concepts / 核心概念

### A1. The hierarchy of land law in Singapore
Land in Singapore is held under a **Torrens (title-by-registration) system**, governed by the **Land Titles Act 1993 (LTA)** and administered by the **Singapore Land Authority (SLA)** via the Land Titles Registry. Three things sit on top of every parcel:

1. **The estate** — *how long* and *with what quality* you hold the land (fee simple, estate in perpetuity, leasehold, life estate).
2. **The interests** — *what rights* others (or you) have *over* the land (legal vs equitable; easements, mortgages, leases, caveats).
3. **The ownership structure** — *who* holds it and *in what shares* (sole owner; or co-owners as joint tenants / tenants-in-common).

> 中文：新加坡土地采用"托伦斯登记制"(Torrens System),由 SLA 管理,核心法律是《1993 年土地权属法》(LTA)。一块地上叠加三层:地权类型(持有多久)、权益(谁对土地有什么权利)、产权结构(谁拥有、占多少份额)。

### A2. "Estate" vs "Interest" — the foundational distinction
- An **estate** is a *possessory* entitlement measured by duration (freehold = indefinite; leasehold = fixed term).
- An **interest** is any right *in or over* land that is *less than* full possessory ownership (e.g. a mortgage, an easement, a tenancy, a caveat).

> 中文:Estate(地产权)= 占有权,按时间长短衡量;Interest(权益)= 对土地的某种权利,但小于完整所有权。

### A3. Tenure (freehold vs leasehold) is about TIME, not quality of title
- **Freehold** = held indefinitely (fee simple) or near-indefinitely (999-yr is treated commercially as freehold).
- **Leasehold** = held for a *fixed term* (commonly 99, 60, 30 years), after which the land **reverts to the State**.
- HDB flats and most new condos sit on **99-year leasehold**. JTC industrial land is commonly **30 + 30 years**.

> 中文:Tenure 指持有时间。永久地契(freehold)= 无限期;租赁地契(leasehold)= 固定年限,到期归还国家。组屋为 99 年地契。

## (b) Must-Know Rules & Exact Figures / 必背规则与数字

### B1. Types of estate in land

| Estate | Tenure | Key features | Reverts? |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Fee Simple (freehold)** | Indefinite | Highest estate. Unconditional, inheritable, fully alienable. Owner has the most complete rights short of the State. | No |
| **Estate in Perpetuity (freehold)** | Indefinite | Freehold but **conditional** — granted under the **State Lands Act** with conditions/restrictions attached. | No (unless conditions breached) |
| **Leasehold (estate for a term)** | Fixed (99 / 60 / 30 / 999 yr) | Right to exclusive possession for the term. On expiry, land returns to State (reversion). 999-yr ≈ freehold commercially. | Yes, on expiry |
| **Life Estate** | Duration of a person's life | Holder enjoys land for their lifetime only; on death it passes to the reversioner/remainderman. Cannot pass by will. | Yes, on death |

> 中文小结:Fee Simple = 最高、永久、无条件;Estate in Perpetuity = 永久但**有条件**(《国家土地法》);Leasehold = 固定年限到期归还国家;Life Estate = 仅限本人在世期间,死后归还,**不能用遗嘱处置**。

### B2. Legal vs Equitable interests

| | Legal interest | Equitable interest |
|---|---|---|
| Nature | Right *in rem* — enforceable against the **whole world** | Beneficial right — enforceable against all **EXCEPT** a *bona fide purchaser for value without notice* |
| When it arises (Torrens) | On **registration** of the instrument | On a recognised equity (e.g. signing/exercising OTP or S&P; under a trust; resulting trust by contribution) |
| Example | Registered proprietor; registered mortgagee | A buyer who has exercised the OTP but completion/registration not yet done |

- **Trust:** trustee holds the **legal** title; beneficiary holds the **equitable** (beneficial) interest.
- **Resulting trust:** beneficial interest follows **financial contribution**, unless a common intention is proven.
- A buyer acquires an **equitable interest** upon exercising the OTP / signing the S&P — this is the basis for **lodging a caveat**.

> 中文小结:Legal(法定权益)对**全世界**有效;Equitable(衡平权益)对所有人有效,**唯独不能对抗"善意、付了对价、且不知情的买家"**。信托中:受托人持法定产权,受益人持衡平权益。买家行使 OTP 后取得衡平权益 → 可登记 caveat。

### B3. Co-ownership — Joint Tenancy (JT) vs Tenancy-in-Common (TIC) ★高频考点★

| Feature | Joint Tenancy (JT) 联权 | Tenancy-in-Common (TIC) 分权 |
|---|---|---|
| Shares | **No distinct shares** — all own the whole together | **Distinct undivided shares** (may be **unequal**, e.g. 70/30) |
| Unities required | **All 4 — PITT** (Possession, Interest, Title, Time) | **Only Possession** |
| Right of survivorship (*jus accrescendi*) | **YES** — on death, interest passes **automatically** to surviving joint tenant(s) | **NO** — share passes by **will or intestacy** |
| Can leave share in a will? | **NO** (survivorship overrides the will) | **YES** |
| Typical use | Married couples wanting auto-transfer on death | Investors / unequal contributors wanting separate, willable shares |

**Four unities (PITT):** **P**ossession, **I**nterest, **T**itle, **T**ime.

**Severance — converting JT → TIC** (any one of these):
1. Alienating / dealing with one's own share (e.g. selling/mortgaging it)
2. Mutual agreement of all co-owners
3. Mutual conduct showing intention to treat as TIC
4. Unilateral written declaration / deed of severance
5. Court order

> 中文小结:**JT(联权)= 不分份额、需四统一(PITT)、有"生存者取得权"——一方去世,产权自动归另一方,遗嘱无效**。**TIC(分权)= 有独立份额(可不等)、只需占有统一、无生存者取得权——份额可由遗嘱继承**。从 JT 变 TIC 叫"severance(切断)"。

### B4. Land Titles Act 1993 / Torrens System

**Three principles:**
| Principle | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Mirror** | The register accurately *reflects* all current facts about the title |
| **Curtain** | No need to look *behind* the register — the folio is conclusive |
| **Insurance** | The State *guarantees* the title; compensation if loss caused by registry error/fraud |

- **s46 LTA — indefeasibility** of the registered proprietor's title. **NOT absolute.** Exceptions: **fraud or forgery** by the proprietor or their agent; statutory / **overriding interests**.
- **Folio = conclusive evidence** of title. **Registration vests legal title.**

> 中文小结:托伦斯三原则——镜子(登记簿如实反映)、窗帘(无需查登记簿背后)、保险(国家担保)。s46 赋予登记产权人"不可推翻的产权",但**非绝对**——产权人本人/代理人的欺诈或伪造、法定凌驾性权益可推翻。

### B5. Registered vs Unregistered land

| | Registered (Torrens / LTA) | Unregistered (Deeds system / Registration of Deeds Act) |
|---|---|---|
| Evidence of title | Register/folio is **conclusive**, indefeasible | **Chain of title deeds**; no State guarantee |
| Takes effect | On **registration** | Priority by order of registration / notice |
| Prevalence | Almost all private land today | Tiny residue remaining |

> 中文:登记土地(托伦斯)——登记簿即终局证据;未登记土地(契据制)——靠一连串地契文件,无国家担保。

### B6. Caveats ★高频考点★

- A **caveat** is a statutory notice / "freeze" lodged with SLA by anyone holding a **caveatable interest** (e.g. a buyer who has exercised the OTP/S&P, a mortgagee, **CPF Board**, a tenant, an easement holder).
- Lodged via the **STARS** portal; fee ≈ **S$64.45**.
- **Validity: 5 years**, extendable a further 5.
- On an inconsistent dealing, SLA serves notice → caveator must obtain a **court order within 30 days (s127)**, else caveat lapses.
- **The Registrar CANNOT remove a caveat on their own motion.**
- A caveat is **precautionary, NOT mandatory.** A **wrongful caveat** → liable in damages (s128).
- Key sections: s115 (lodge), s119 (effect), s121 (lapsing), s122 (extension), s126 (withdrawal), s127 (lapsing on notice), s128 (compensation).

> 中文小结:Caveat = 向 SLA 登记的"警示/冻结",任何有"可登记权益"者(买家行使 OTP 后、贷款人、公积金局、租户)均可登记。**有效 5 年,可再延 5 年**;遇到冲突交易,SLA 发通知后须**30 天内拿到法院命令**(s127)。**登记官不能自行删除;caveat 是预防性的、非强制;错误登记须赔偿(s128)。**

### B7. Easements

- A right to use **another person's land** for a limited purpose (right of way, drainage, run services).
- Requires a **dominant tenement** (benefits) **AND** a **servient tenement** (burdened).
- A **registered** easement is noted on the folio and **binds successors**. It is both a **caveatable interest** and an **encumbrance**.

> 中文:地役权 = 为有限目的使用**他人土地**的权利(通行、排水、铺设管线)。必须有"受益地(dominant)"和"承受地(servient)"。已登记的地役权随地转移,约束后续业主;既是可登记权益,也是产权负担。

### B8. Encumbrances, Mortgages & Charges

- **Encumbrance** = any third-party right burdening the land: mortgage, charge, easement, lease, lien, **caveat**, restrictive covenant.
- **Torrens mortgage does NOT transfer ownership** — it operates as a **registered charge**; the mortgagor *retains* title.
- Effective only on **registration**. On default, the mortgagee has a **statutory power of sale**. Folio is conclusive of the encumbrance.

> 中文小结:产权负担(encumbrance)= 第三方对土地的权利(抵押、地役权、租约、留置、caveat 等)。**托伦斯抵押不转移所有权**——只是"登记的押记",业主保留产权;登记后生效,违约时贷款人有法定出售权。

### B9. Foreign ownership — Residential Property Act 1976 (SLA / LDAU) ★高频考点★

- **"Foreign person" includes Singapore PRs**, foreign companies and entities.

| **NON-restricted (NO approval needed)** | **RESTRICTED (LDAU approval required)** |
|---|---|
| Condominium unit; private flat/apartment | Vacant residential land |
| Strata-landed **within** an approved condo | Terrace / semi-detached / bungalow (landed) |
| Leasehold landed **≤ 7 years** | Strata-landed **outside** a condo |
| Commercial shophouses; industrial / commercial property | **Sentosa Cove** landed |
| Registered hotels | Residential-use shophouses |
| Fully privatised EC (≥10 yr); HDB flats (per HDB rules) | Serviced apartments / dormitories; mixed commercial-residential |

- **LDAU approval criteria:** PR for **≥ 5 years** + exceptional economic contribution. Fee **S$1,220/property**; ~**30 working days**.
- **Conditions on approved restricted purchase:** mainland land area **≤ 15,000 sq ft**; Sentosa Cove **≤ 1,800 sqm**; **no disposal within 5 years**; **owner-occupation only (no rental)**; no subdivision.
- **Sentosa Cove** is the **only landed enclave** open to non-resident foreigners (with LDAU approval).

> 中文小结:**"外国人"包括 PR**。可自由买:公寓、批准公寓内的有地住宅、≤7 年租约有地、商业/工业、商铺、酒店。**须 LDAU 批准:空置住宅地、独立/半独立/排屋等有地住宅、公寓外的有地、圣淘沙湾、住宅用商铺。** 批准条件:PR≥5 年+杰出经济贡献,费用 S$1,220/套,约 30 个工作日;买后 5 年内不得出售、仅自住不得出租。

### B10. En Bloc / Collective Sale — Land Titles (Strata) Act 1967, s84A ★高频考点★

| Age of development (from latest **TOP**, or CSC if no TOP) | Required consent (by **BOTH** share value **AND** total strata floor area) |
|---|---|
| **< 10 years old** | **90%** |
| **≥ 10 years old** | **80%** |

**Process:** form **Collective Sale Committee (CSC)** at EGM → independent valuation → sign **Collective Sale Agreement (CSA)** → consent must be reached **within 12 months of the first signature** → **5-day cooling-off** to rescind → public tender → application to **Strata Titles Board (STB)** if not unanimous.

**STB** mediates/adjudicates: must be satisfied the sale is in **good faith** and no objector suffers **financial loss**. Objection grounds = financial loss; lack of good faith (assessed via sale price, distribution method, purchaser–owner relationship). Typical timeline 18–24 months.

> 中文小结:集体出售(en bloc),门槛**按份额价值 + 总建筑面积"双重"达标**:楼龄 **<10 年须 90%**,**≥10 年须 80%**(楼龄从最近的 **TOP** 算起)。流程:成立 CSC → 估值 → 签 CSA(**首签后 12 个月内**集齐同意)→ **5 天冷静期** → 公开招标 → 非全体同意须报 **STB**。STB 须确认"善意"且无人遭受"经济损失"。

## (c) Common Exam Traps / 易错点

1. **JT vs TIC — do NOT swap survivorship.** JT = right of survivorship (auto to survivor, will is overridden); TIC = willable share, no survivorship. **JT needs all 4 unities (PITT); TIC needs only Possession.** 中文:别把"生存者取得权"装反——JT 有、TIC 无。
2. **Equitable interest is defeated by a *bona fide purchaser for value without notice*.** A **legal** interest survives against *everyone*. 中文:衡平权益唯独输给"善意付价不知情买家";法定权益对所有人有效。
3. **Estate in Perpetuity is FREEHOLD but CONDITIONAL** (State Lands Act) — not the same as unconditional Fee Simple. 中文:Estate in Perpetuity 是永久但**有条件**,≠ Fee Simple。
4. **A caveat is precautionary, NOT mandatory**; the **Registrar cannot remove it on their own motion**; on notice the caveator has **30 days** to get a court order (s127). 中文:caveat 非强制、登记官不能自行删、收到通知 30 天内须取法院命令。
5. **"Foreign person" INCLUDES Singapore PRs.** Foreigners/PRs **CAN** buy condo units freely but **CANNOT** buy landed/GCB/vacant residential land **without LDAU approval**. 中文:"外国人"含 PR;公寓可自由买,有地住宅须 LDAU 批准。
6. **Strata-landed *within* an approved condo = NON-restricted; *outside* = RESTRICTED.** 中文:批准公寓**内**的有地不受限,公寓**外**的有地受限。
7. **En bloc consent must satisfy BOTH share value AND strata floor area** — not either/or — and the threshold depends on **age from TOP** (<10 yr → 90%; ≥10 yr → 80%). 中文:同意门槛须**份额价值与建筑面积双达标**;楼龄从 TOP 算。
8. **Torrens mortgage does NOT transfer ownership** — it is a registered *charge*; mortgagor keeps title. 中文:托伦斯抵押不转移所有权。
9. **s46 indefeasibility is NOT absolute** — defeated by fraud/forgery by the proprietor/agent and by overriding interests. 中文:s46 的"不可推翻"非绝对。
10. **Watch "EXCEPT / NOT / restricted vs non-restricted" inversions** in the stem — these are deliberate distractor patterns. 中文:留意题干中的"除外/不/受限-不受限"反转。
11. **Easement needs TWO tenements** (dominant + servient). A right over your *own* land alone is not an easement. 中文:地役权需"受益地+承受地"两块地。
12. **Life estate cannot be left by will** — it ends at death. 中文:终身地产权不能用遗嘱处置。

## (d) Worked Examples / 计算与情景演练

This is a *legal-concepts* topic, so "calculations" are mainly **threshold / counting** problems.

### Example 1 — En bloc consent threshold
*An estate obtained its TOP in March 2014. A collective sale is launched in June 2026. What consent is required, and by what measures?*
- Age from TOP = June 2026 − March 2014 = **12 years ≥ 10 years → 80% required**.
- Must be **80% by share value AND 80% by total strata floor area** (both, not either).
- The 80% must be reached **within 12 months of the first CSA signature**.
- **Answer: 80% by both share value and strata floor area.**

### Example 2 — En bloc, newer block
*A condo got TOP in January 2019; en bloc launched June 2026. Threshold?*
- Age = 7.4 years **< 10 years → 90%** by both share value and strata floor area. **Answer: 90%.**

### Example 3 — Survivorship (JT)
*A and B own a flat as joint tenants. A's will leaves "my share" to A's son C. A dies. Who owns the flat?*
- JT → **right of survivorship** overrides the will. A's interest passes **automatically to B**. C gets nothing from the flat.
- **Answer: B owns the whole flat. The gift to C fails.** (If they had been **tenants-in-common**, A's share would pass to C under the will.)

### Example 4 — Equitable interest vs bona fide purchaser
*X holds an unregistered (equitable) interest in land. Y, knowing nothing of X, buys and pays full price and registers. Can X enforce against Y?*
- Y is a **bona fide purchaser for value without notice** → X's **equitable** interest is **defeated**.
- **Answer: No.** (If X's interest were a *registered legal* interest, it would bind Y.)

### Example 5 — Foreign buyer eligibility
*A Singapore PR of 6 years wants to buy (i) a condo unit, (ii) a freehold bungalow. What approval is needed?*
- A PR is a "foreign person." **Condo unit = non-restricted → no approval.** **Bungalow (landed) = restricted → LDAU approval required** (PR ≥5 yr + exceptional economic contribution; fee S$1,220; ~30 working days; no sale within 5 yr; owner-occupation only).
- **Answer: (i) no approval; (ii) LDAU approval required.**

- **Registered vs unregistered:** register conclusive vs chain of deeds.

## (a) Key Concepts / 核心概念

### 1. The Torrens System & Land Titles Act 1993 (LTA)
Singapore runs a **title-by-registration** (Torrens) system, administered by the **Singapore Land Authority (SLA)** through the **Land Titles Registry**. Almost all private land is now under the LTA.

The system rests on **three principles** (memorise these — classic MCQ):

| Principle | Meaning | 中文 |
|---|---|---|
| **Mirror** | The register accurately *reflects* all material facts about the title. | 镜子原则：登记簿如实反映产权 |
| **Curtain** | A buyer need *not look behind* the register (no tracing historic deeds). | 帷幕原则：无需追查历史契据 |
| **Insurance / Assurance** | The State *guarantees* the title; wrongly deprived parties may be compensated. | 保险原则：国家担保产权 |

> 中文小结：托伦斯制度 = 登记即产权。登记簿由 SLA 管理，登记后法定产权（legal title）才归属买方。三大原则：镜子、帷幕、保险。

### 2. Indefeasibility (s46 LTA)
Once registered, the proprietor's title is **indefeasible** — protected against unregistered/competing claims. The folio is **conclusive evidence** of title. **BUT it is NOT absolute.** Exceptions where title *can* be challenged:
- **Fraud or forgery** to which the registered proprietor (or their agent) was a party;
- **Statutory / overriding interests** (e.g. certain leases, statutory liens, public rights).

> 中文小结：登记产权"不可推翻"(s46) 但有例外 —— 产权人本人/代理人参与的**欺诈或伪造**，以及法定优先权益。登记簿不是绝对的。

### 3. Registered vs Unregistered Land

| | Registered (Torrens, LTA) | Unregistered (Deeds, Registration of Deeds Act) |
|---|---|---|
| Proof of title | Register (folio) is conclusive | Chain of title **deeds** |
| State guarantee | Yes | **No** |
| When effective | On **registration** | Priority by order of registration/notice |
| Prevalence today | Almost all private land | Tiny residue |

> 中文：托伦斯地登记即生效、国家担保；契据制看契据链、无担保。新加坡几乎全部转为托伦斯。

### 4. Legal vs Equitable Interest

| | Legal interest | Equitable interest |
|---|---|---|
| Enforceable against | **The whole world** ("in rem") | All **EXCEPT a bona fide purchaser for value without notice** |
| Arises (Torrens) | On **registration** | On exercising OTP / signing S&P, under a trust, etc. |
| Example | Registered proprietor, registered mortgagee | Buyer who has exercised OTP but not yet registered; trust beneficiary |

- **Trust:** trustee holds **legal** title; beneficiary holds **equitable** interest.
- The buyer's equitable interest (on exercising OTP / signing S&P) is the **basis for lodging a caveat**.

> 中文小结：法定权益对抗全世界；衡平权益对抗所有人 **除了**"善意、有偿、不知情的买方"(bona fide purchaser for value without notice)。买方行使 OTP 后取得衡平权益 → 据此登记 caveat。

### 5. Estates & Interests in Land

| Estate | Nature | 中文 |
|---|---|---|
| **Fee Simple** | Highest freehold — indefinite, unconditional, inheritable | 永久地契，最高产权 |
| **Estate in Perpetuity** | Freehold but **conditional** (State Lands Act) | 永久但有条件 |
| **Life Estate** | Lasts the holder's lifetime only; reverts on death | 终身产权，死后归还 |
| **Leasehold** | Fixed term (e.g. 99, 999 yr); reverts to State on expiry | 租赁地契，到期归还国家 |

HDB flats = **99-year leasehold**. 999-year ≈ freehold in practice.

### 6. Co-ownership: Joint Tenancy (JT) vs Tenancy-in-Common (TIC) — HIGH-YIELD

| | Joint Tenancy (JT) | Tenancy-in-Common (TIC) |
|---|---|---|
| Shares | Own the **whole jointly**, NO distinct shares | **Distinct undivided shares** (may be unequal) |
| Unities required | **All 4 — PITT** (Possession, Interest, Title, Time) | **Only Possession** |
| On death | **Right of survivorship** (*jus accrescendi*) — passes **automatically** to survivor(s) | **No survivorship** — share passes by **will / intestacy** |
| Willable? | NO (survivorship overrides will) | YES |

**Severance (JT → TIC)** can occur by: dealing with one's own share (alienation); mutual agreement; mutual conduct; unilateral declaration/deed; court order.

> 中文小结：JT = 共同持有、无份额、四统一(PITT)、**生存者取得权**（死后自动归生者，不能立遗嘱处分）。TIC = 有独立份额、只需占有统一、**无生存者取得权**（份额按遗嘱/无遗嘱继承）。考试最爱把两者对调，务必记牢。

## (b) Must-Know Rules & Exact Figures / 必背规则与数字

### Caveats (s115–s128 LTA) — VERIFIED 2026

| Item | Figure / Rule |
|---|---|
| What it is | A statutory **notice / "freeze"** lodged with SLA warning of a claimed interest |
| Who can lodge | Anyone with a **caveatable interest**: buyer (exercised OTP/S&P), mortgagee, lessee, easement holder, **CPF Board**, etc. |
| Where / how | **Land Titles Registry, SLA**, via the **STARS** eLodgment portal |
| **Lodgement fee** | **S$64.45 (incl. GST) per application** ✅ |
| **Validity** | **5 years**, extendable for a further **5 years** before expiry ✅ |
| On inconsistent dealing | SLA serves **notice** → caveator must obtain a **court order within 30 days (s127)** or caveat lapses |
| Registrar's power | **Cannot remove a caveat on its own motion** |
| Wrongful caveat | Caveator **liable in damages (s128)** |
| Mandatory? | **NO — precautionary only**, not compulsory |

**Key sections:** s115 lodge · s119 effect · s121 lapsing · s122 extension · s126 withdrawal · s127 lapsing on notice (30 days) · s128 compensation for wrongful caveat.

> 中文小结：Caveat = 向 SLA 登记的"冻结"通知，须有 caveatable interest。费用 **S$64.45/次**，有效 **5 年**可再延 **5 年**。出现冲突交易时收到通知后须 **30 天内**取得法院命令(s127)，否则失效。登记官**不能自行删除**；错误登记须**赔偿(s128)**；Caveat 是**预防性、非强制**。

### Easements / 地役权

| Item | Rule |
|---|---|
| Definition | Right to use **another's land** for a limited purpose (right of way, drainage, utility services) |
| Two tenements required | **Dominant tenement** (benefits) + **Servient tenement** (burdened) |
| Registration | Registered easement is **noted on the folio** and **binds successors in title** |
| Classification | Is both a **caveatable interest** AND an **encumbrance** |

> 中文小结：地役权 = 为有限目的使用他人土地。必须有**需役地(dominant)** 与**供役地(servient)** 两块地。登记后记在 folio 上，约束后续业主；既是可登记权益又是产权负担。

### Encumbrances, Mortgages & Charges / 产权负担与抵押

| Item | Rule |
|---|---|
| Encumbrance | A third-party right **burdening** land: mortgage, charge, easement, lease, lien, **caveat**, restrictive covenant |
| **Torrens mortgage** | Operates as a **registered charge — does NOT transfer ownership**; mortgagor **retains title** |
| When effective | Only on **registration** |
| On default | Mortgagee has a **statutory power of sale** |
| Proof | Folio is **conclusive** of the encumbrance |

> 中文小结：产权负担 = 加在土地上的第三方权利。**托伦斯抵押不转移产权** —— 只是登记的"押记"(charge)，抵押人保留产权；登记后才生效；违约时抵押权人有**法定出售权**。

### En Bloc / Collective Sale (Land Titles (Strata) Act 1967, s84A) — VERIFIED 2026

| Item | Rule |
|---|---|
| Consent threshold (by **both** share value **AND** strata floor area) | **< 10 years old → 90%** · **≥ 10 years → 80%** |
| Age measured from | Latest **TOP** (or **CSC** if no TOP) |
| Consent window | Must be obtained within **12 months** of the first signature |
| **Cooling-off** | **5 days** to rescind after signing the Collective Sale Agreement |
| Adjudicator | **Strata Titles Board (STB)** — mediates (up to ~60 days), then hears if not unanimous |
| STB must be satisfied | Sale is in **good faith** AND no objector suffers **financial loss** |
| Good-faith factors | Sale price, method of distributing proceeds, purchaser–owner relationship |

> 中文小结：集体出售同意门槛 —— **不足10年→90%**，**满10年→80%**（按份额价值 *和* 面积**双重**计算）。同意须在首签后 **12 个月**内取得；签 CSA 后有 **5 天冷静期**。非全体同意须报 **STB**，STB 须确认**善意**且无业主蒙受**经济损失**。

### Foreign Ownership — Residential Property Act 1976 (SLA / LDAU) — VERIFIED 2026
**"Foreign person" includes Singapore PRs**, foreign companies/entities.

| **NON-restricted (NO approval needed)** | **RESTRICTED (LDAU approval needed)** |
|---|---|
| Condominium unit; private flat/apartment | Vacant residential land |
| **Strata landed WITHIN an approved condo** | **Strata landed OUTSIDE a condo** |
| Leasehold landed **≤ 7 years** | Terrace / semi-D / bungalow (landed) |
| Commercial / industrial property; shophouse (commercial) | **Sentosa Cove** landed (special faster track) |
| Registered hotels | Residential-use shophouse |
| Fully privatised EC (≥10 yr); HDB (per HDB rules) | Serviced apartments / dormitories; mixed comm-resi |

**LDAU approval — key figures:**

| Item | Figure |
|---|---|
| **Eligibility** | PR for **≥ 5 years** + **exceptional economic contribution** (e.g. tax-assessable income) |
| **Application fee** | **S$1,220 per property** ✅ |
| Processing | ~30 working days |
| **Approval validity** | **1 year** from approval date — **cannot be extended** (lapses if no property submitted) ✅ |
| Land-area cap | Mainland ≤ **15,000 sq ft**; Sentosa Cove ≤ **1,800 sqm** |
| Holding restriction | **No disposal within 5 years** of legal completion ✅ |
| Use | **Owner-occupation only — no rental**, no subdivision |

> 中文小结："外国人"**包括 PR**。公寓单位可自由购买；**有地住宅 / 空地 / 公寓外的 strata landed / 圣淘沙湾**等属**受限**，须 LDAU 批准。批准条件：PR **满5年**+杰出经济贡献；费用 **S$1,220/物业**；批准**有效1年不可延**；批后**5年内不得出售**，**只可自住不可出租**。

## (c) Common Exam Traps / 易错点

1. **JT vs TIC inversion** — JT has **survivorship** (auto to survivor, NOT willable); TIC share is **willable**. Distractors swap these. *(JT 有生存者取得权且不可立遗嘱处分；TIC 可立遗嘱。)*
2. **Indefeasibility is NOT absolute** — fraud/forgery by the proprietor/agent and statutory overriding interests defeat it. A stem saying "registered title can never be challenged" is **wrong**.
3. **Equitable interest is defeated by a bona fide purchaser for value without notice** — but a **legal** interest survives against the whole world. Don't confuse which one survives.
4. **A caveat is precautionary, NOT mandatory**, and the **Registrar cannot remove it on its own motion**. Both are favourite "true/false" traps.
5. **Caveat lapsing — 30 days (s127)**: after SLA serves notice, caveator needs a **court order within 30 days**, not the other way round.
6. **Torrens mortgage does NOT transfer ownership** — it is a registered **charge**; the mortgagor keeps title. Distractor: "mortgagee becomes owner" = wrong.
7. **En bloc 80% vs 90% inversion** — **<10 yr = 90%**, **≥10 yr = 80%** (higher threshold for *newer* buildings). Also must satisfy **BOTH** share value **and** floor area.
8. **"Foreign person" includes PRs** under the RPA — a common trap is treating PRs as locals for landed property. PRs still need LDAU approval for restricted property.
9. **Leasehold landed ≤7 years is NON-restricted** — anything longer (or freehold landed) is restricted.
10. **Strata landed: WITHIN condo = non-restricted; OUTSIDE condo = restricted.** Easy to flip.
11. **"EXCEPT / NOT / LEAST" stems** — read carefully; the answer is the odd-one-out.
12. **Mirror / Curtain / Insurance** — questions ask which principle a scenario illustrates (e.g. "no need to investigate past deeds" = **Curtain**; "State compensates" = **Insurance**).
13. **Caveatable interest ≠ ownership** — a tenant, mortgagee, or CPF Board can lodge a caveat without owning the land.

## (d) Worked Examples / 计算示例

The only arithmetic in P1.5 is fees and consent thresholds. Memorise the figures, then apply.

**Example 1 — Caveat fees.**
A buyer lodges a caveat after exercising the OTP, then needs to extend it before the 5-year mark.
- Lodgement: **S$64.45** (incl. GST) per application.
- Validity: **5 years**; one extension grants a **further 5 years** (total 10 years if extended once).
- If SLA serves notice of an inconsistent dealing → caveator must get a **court order within 30 days (s127)** or it lapses.

**Example 2 — En bloc consent threshold.**
A condominium obtained TOP **8 years ago**, with 100 units of equal share value and equal floor area.
- Age < 10 years → **90%** consent required (by **both** share value **and** strata area).
- 90% of 100 units = **90 units** must consent.
- If only 80 units consent (80%), the sale **cannot proceed** — it would meet the ≥10-year threshold but the building is only 8 years old.
- Consent must be gathered within **12 months** of the first signature; signatories then have a **5-day** cooling-off window.

**Example 3 — En bloc, older block.**
Same 100-unit estate, but **12 years** since TOP.
- Age ≥ 10 years → **80%** threshold → **80 units** suffice. The same 80 signatures that failed in Example 2 would now succeed.

**Example 4 — LDAU foreigner purchase.**
A Singapore PR of 6 years wants to buy a semi-detached (landed, restricted) house on the mainland.
- Eligible to **apply** (PR ≥5 years) but must also show **exceptional economic contribution**.
- Fee **S$1,220** per property; approval **valid 1 year, non-extendable**.
- If granted: land area ≤ **15,000 sq ft**; **no sale within 5 years**; **owner-occupation only**.

## 速记总结 / Quick Recall

- **Torrens / LTA 1993, run by SLA.** Three principles: **Mirror, Curtain, Insurance.** Registration = legal title.
- **Indefeasibility (s46) is NOT absolute** — defeated by **fraud/forgery** (proprietor/agent) + statutory overriding interests.
- **Legal interest** beats the **whole world**; **equitable interest** loses only to a **bona fide purchaser for value without notice**.
- **Trust:** trustee = legal title; beneficiary = equitable.
- **JT = whole, no shares, 4 unities PITT, SURVIVORSHIP (not willable).** **TIC = distinct shares, possession only, NO survivorship (willable).**
- Estates: **Fee Simple** (freehold, unconditional) > **Estate in Perpetuity** (freehold, conditional) > **Life Estate** > **Leasehold** (HDB = 99 yr).
- **Caveat:** SLA via STARS · **S$64.45/application** · valid **5 yrs (+5)** · **30-day** court order after s127 notice · Registrar **can't self-remove** · **precautionary, not mandatory** · wrongful → **damages (s128)**.
- **Easement:** needs **dominant + servient** tenements; binds successors; both a caveatable interest and an encumbrance.
- **Torrens mortgage = registered CHARGE, ownership NOT transferred**; effective on registration; default → statutory power of sale.
- **En bloc:** **<10yr → 90%**, **≥10yr → 80%** (share value AND floor area); consent within **12 months**; **5-day** cooling-off; **STB** needs **good faith + no financial loss**.
- **RPA 1976 / LDAU:** "foreign person" **includes PRs**; condos free, landed/vacant land/Sentosa restricted; approval = PR **≥5 yrs** + economic contribution; **S$1,220/property**; valid **1 yr non-extendable**; **no sale within 5 yrs**, owner-occupation only.
- **Leasehold landed ≤7 yrs = non-restricted; strata landed within condo = non-restricted, outside = restricted.**

## P1.6 HDB: Eligibility Schemes, Grants & CPF Usage — Revision Notes
**Paper 1 | Exam date: 18 July 2026 | All figures verified against HDB, CPF, IRAS, MAS, SLA primary sources (current as of June 2026)**

> **Currency rule:** You will NOT be tested on policy changes implemented within ~1 month before the exam (i.e. after ~18 June 2026). Use the figures below. Where the government has *announced but not yet implemented* a change (e.g. review of the singles age-35 rule), use the **current** rule.

## (a) Key Concepts / 核心概念

This topic sits at the intersection of three regulators, and exam questions love to mix them up:

| Pillar | Owner | Governs |
|---|---|---|
| **Eligibility schemes** | HDB | *WHO* can buy a flat (citizenship, family nucleus, age, income ceiling) |
| **CPF Housing Grants** | HDB (paid into CPF OA) | *HOW MUCH* subsidy a first-timer gets |
| **CPF Usage** | CPF Board | *HOW* you may pay using CPF Ordinary Account (VL / WL / lease rules / retirement sum) |

Three foundational ideas to anchor everything:

1. **"First-timer" vs "second-timer."** Grants are overwhelmingly a *first-timer* benefit. A first-timer is someone who has never received a housing subsidy (no previous BTO/new flat, no CPF housing grant, no DBSS/EC from developer). 中文：第一次申请政府补贴的人才能拿到大部分津贴。
2. **New/subsidised flat = restricted; resale-without-grant = open.** The moment a buyer takes *any* grant or buys a *new* flat, eligibility conditions (income ceiling, MOP, citizenship rules) bite. Buying a resale flat **outright with cash/loan and no grant** removes the income ceiling and (for a flat bought with no grant) the MOP. 中文：拿津贴或买新房就受限；纯现金/贷款买二手房（不拿津贴）则没有收入顶限。
3. **CPF is a usage tool, not free money.** CPF Ordinary Account (OA) can pay the purchase, but its use is capped by the **Valuation Limit / Withdrawal Limit / remaining-lease / retirement-sum** rules. Grants are deposited *into* the CPF OA, not paid as cash.

## (b) Must-Know Rules & Exact Figures / 必背规则与数字

### B1. HDB Eligibility Schemes

| Scheme | Core requirement |
|---|---|
| **Public Scheme** | ≥1 Singapore Citizen (SC) + a family nucleus (e.g. spouse, children, parents). The standard route. |
| **Fiancé/Fiancée Scheme** | Couple not yet married; must register marriage **within 3 months** of key collection (BTO) / before completion (resale). |
| **Single Singapore Citizen Scheme** | Single SC aged **≥35**. (Includes unmarried, divorced, widowed.) |
| **Joint Singles Scheme** | 2–4 single SCs, **all ≥35**, buying together. |
| **Non-Citizen Spouse Scheme** | SC + non-citizen spouse (no other family member needed). |
| **Non-Citizen Family Scheme** | SC + non-citizen parent/child (no SC/SPR spouse). |
| **Orphans Scheme** | Orphaned siblings, ≥1 SC, at least one sibling ≥21 (or younger under conditions). |
| **Conversion Scheme** | Buy 2 adjoining flats to form a bigger one. |

> 中文注：单身公民买房年龄是 **35 岁**。政府 2025 年 8 月宣布将检讨这一年龄及收入顶限，但尚未生效——考试请用 **35 岁**。

### B2. Income Ceilings (gross average monthly household income)

| Purchase type | Income ceiling |
|---|---|
| BTO / new flat — **family** | **$14,000** |
| BTO / new flat — **extended / multi-gen family** | **$21,000** |
| BTO **2-room Flexi** (single SC) | **$7,000** |
| **Resale flat — NO grant** | **No ceiling** |
| Resale **with Family Grant** | $14,000 ($21,000 extended family) |
| **Executive Condominium (EC)** | **$16,000** |
| **EHG** | $9,000 (families) / $4,500 (singles) |

> 中文注：**只有买新房或申请津贴才有收入顶限**；纯买二手房不拿津贴 → 无顶限。EC = $16,000，最容易和 BTO 的 $14,000 搞混。

### B3. CPF Housing Grants (2026 figures)

| Grant | Eligible for | Amount | Income ceiling |
|---|---|---|---|
| **EHG (Families)** | BTO **and** resale | Up to **$120,000** (tiered by income) | $9,000 |
| **EHG (Singles)** | BTO **and** resale | Up to **$60,000** (tiered) | $4,500 |
| **CPF Housing / "Family" Grant** | **Resale only** | **$80,000** (2–4 rm) / **$50,000** (5-rm or larger) | $14,000 |
| **Singles Grant** (resale) | **Resale only** | **$40,000** (2–4 rm) / **$25,000** (5-rm+) | $7,000 |
| **Proximity Housing Grant (PHG)** | **Resale only** | **$30,000** live *with* parents/child; **$20,000** live *near* (≤4 km) | **No ceiling** |
| PHG (singles) | Resale only | $15,000 (with) / $10,000 (near) | No ceiling |

- **EHG is tiered**: the lower the income, the higher the grant. Requires **12 continuous months of employment** at application.
- **Max combined family resale grants ≈ $230,000** = Family Grant $80k + EHG $120k + PHG $30k.
- Grants are credited into the **CPF Ordinary Account**, not paid as cash. Used for downpayment/purchase.

> 中文注：EHG 同时适用于新房和二手房；**Family Grant 和 PHG 只适用于二手房**。家庭买二手房三大津贴合计上限约 **$230,000**。

### B4. Minimum Occupation Period (MOP) — from date of key collection

| Flat type | MOP |
|---|---|
| **Standard** flat | **5 years** |
| **Plus** flat | **10 years** |
| **Prime** flat | **10 years** |
| Resale flat bought with **no grant** | **No MOP** |

- 10-year MOP applies to **Plus/Prime** flats launched from the new framework (BTO from ~May 2024). Plus/Prime: **no whole-flat renting even after MOP** (Prime); buyer-eligibility restrictions on resale.

> 中文注：标准房 MOP = **5 年**，Plus/Prime = **10 年**。不拿津贴买的二手房没有 MOP。

### B5. CPF Usage — Valuation Limit (VL) & Withdrawal Limit (WL)

| Term | Definition / Rule |
|---|---|
| **Valuation Limit (VL)** | The **lower** of purchase price OR market value at time of purchase. CPF usable up to **100% of VL** freely. |
| **Withdrawal Limit (WL)** | **120% of VL** — absolute cap, **PRIVATE PROPERTY ONLY**. To use CPF beyond 100% VL up to the WL, members aged 55+ must set aside the **Basic Retirement Sum (BRS)**. |
| **HDB flats** | **NO WL** — CPF can fund beyond VL with no 120% cap (subject to BRS condition where applicable). HDB flat bought with **HDB concessionary loan**: neither VL nor WL applies. |
| **Lease coverage** | Full CPF if remaining lease **≥20 yrs AND covers youngest buyer using CPF to age 95**. **Pro-rated** if lease ≥20 yrs but does NOT cover youngest buyer to 95. **NO CPF at all if remaining lease <20 yrs.** |

**Pro-ration formula (lease covers <age 95):**
> CPF usable = VL × (remaining lease at purchase ÷ (95 − age of youngest buyer using CPF))

**Retirement Sums (turning 55 in 2026):**

| Sum | 2026 amount (turning 55 in 2026) | 2025 cohort |
|---|---|---|
| **BRS** | **$110,200** | $106,500 |
| **FRS** | **$220,400** | $213,000 |
| **ERS** | **$440,800** | $426,000 |

> 中文注：**VL = 买价与估价取低者**；CPF 可用到 VL 的 100%。**WL = 120% × VL，只限私宅**；HDB 组屋没有 WL 上限。剩余地契 <20 年 → **完全不能用 CPF**。买价/估价取低（VL），与印花税"取高"正好相反——易混淆！

## (c) Common Exam Traps / 易错点

1. **VL = LOWER of price or value** — but BSD/ABSD/SSD use the **HIGHER**. The exam deliberately pairs these to confuse you. 中文：CPF 的 VL 取**低**，印花税取**高**。
2. **WL (120%) is PRIVATE PROPERTY ONLY.** HDB flats have **no Withdrawal Limit**. A classic distractor says "HDB CPF use is capped at 120% of VL" — FALSE.
3. **Family Grant & PHG are RESALE-ONLY.** They are *not* available for BTO/new flats. EHG is the one grant available for both. 中文：只有 EHG 新房二手房都能拿。
4. **PHG has NO income ceiling**; Family Grant ($14k) and EHG ($9k) do. Don't apply the $14k ceiling to PHG.
5. **EC income ceiling $16,000 ≠ BTO $14,000.** Easy swap in a distractor.
6. **No income ceiling for resale bought WITHOUT a grant.** "All HDB purchases have a $14,000 ceiling" — FALSE.
7. **Singles must be ≥35** (Single SC / Joint Singles). The 2-room Flexi singles income ceiling is **$7,000**, EHG singles ceiling **$4,500** — two different numbers, both tested.
8. **Remaining lease <20 years → ZERO CPF and no HDB loan.** Not "pro-rated" — it's nil. Pro-ration only applies when lease ≥20 yrs but falls short of covering youngest buyer to 95.
9. **Grants go into CPF OA, not as cash.** A trap option says "PHG is paid in cash to the buyer" — FALSE.
10. **2026 BRS = $110,200** (turning 55 in 2026). If a question specifies an earlier cohort, use that cohort's number (2025 = $106,500).
11. **MOP runs from key collection, NOT from OTP / S&P / completion.** 中文：MOP 从**领钥匙**那天起算。
12. **HDB flat with HDB concessionary loan → neither VL nor WL applies** (most generous CPF usage). Don't confuse with HDB flat bought with a *bank* loan, where VL/lease rules still matter.

## (d) Worked Examples / 计算示例

**Example 1 — Valuation Limit (VL):**
A couple buys a resale flat. Purchase price = $600,000; HDB-assessed market value = $620,000.
- VL = **lower** of the two = **$600,000**.
- CPF OA usable freely up to **100% of VL = $600,000** (HDB has no 120% WL cap).
> 易错点：若有人取估价 $620,000 即错——VL 取**低**。

**Example 2 — Private property Withdrawal Limit (WL):**
Private condo, purchase price $1,000,000, market value $1,050,000.
- VL = lower = **$1,000,000**.
- WL = **120% × VL = $1,200,000** (the absolute CPF cap).
- To use CPF beyond $1,000,000 (100% VL) up to $1,200,000, a member aged 55+ must first set aside the **BRS ($110,200 for 2026 cohort)**.

**Example 3 — Pro-rated CPF (lease shortfall):**
Buyer (youngest using CPF) is **age 45**; flat has **40 years** remaining lease; VL = $500,000.
- Lease needed to cover to 95 = 95 − 45 = **50 years**. Lease is 40 yrs (≥20, so CPF allowed but pro-rated).
- CPF usable = VL × (40 ÷ 50) = $500,000 × 0.8 = **$400,000**.

**Example 4 — Lease too short:**
Buyer age 45, flat has **18 years** remaining lease.
- 18 < 20 → **No CPF can be used, and no HDB loan granted.** Must pay fully in cash. 中文：地契不足 20 年 → 完全不能用 CPF，也拿不到 HDB 贷款。

**Example 5 — Max resale grants (first-timer family):**
2-room to 4-room resale flat, household income $4,500/mo, buying to live *with* parents, both first-timers.
- Family Grant (2–4 rm) = $80,000
- EHG (income ≤$9,000, max tier for very low income) = up to $120,000
- PHG (living *with* parents) = $30,000
- **Total = up to $230,000**, all credited into CPF OA.

## 速记总结 / Quick Recall

- **3 regulators:** HDB (eligibility + grants), CPF Board (usage). Grants land in **CPF OA**, never cash.
- **Singles age = 35** (Single SC / Joint Singles). Multi-gen ceiling **$21k**; family BTO **$14k**; **EC $16k**; 2-rm Flexi singles **$7k**.
- **No income ceiling** for resale bought **without a grant** (also no MOP).
- **Grant amounts:** EHG fam **≤$120k** / singles **≤$60k** (both BTO + resale); **Family Grant $80k/$50k (resale only)**; **PHG $30k with / $20k near, no ceiling, resale only**. Family resale max ≈ **$230k**.
- **EHG ceiling $9k (fam)/$4.5k (single)**; needs **12 months continuous employment**.
- **MOP from key collection:** Standard **5 yrs**, Plus/Prime **10 yrs**; no-grant resale = no MOP.
- **VL = LOWER of price/value** (印花税取 HIGHER — opposite!). CPF free to **100% VL**.
- **WL = 120% × VL, PRIVATE ONLY. HDB has NO WL.** HDB + HDB concessionary loan = neither VL nor WL applies.

- **2026 retirement sums:** BRS **$110,200** / FRS **$220,400** / ERS **$440,800** (turning 55 in 2026). Set aside BRS to exceed 100% VL (private) for 55+.

## (a) Key Concepts / 核心概念

| Concept | Plain meaning | 中文 |
|---|---|---|
| **MOP (Minimum Occupation Period)** | The minimum number of years you must physically live in your flat before you can sell it or rent out the **whole** flat. Counted from **key collection** date, excluding any period the flat is not occupied (e.g. rented out, overseas). | 最低居住年限。从**领钥匙日**起算,期间须实际居住,出租整间/出国期间不计入。 |
| **EIP (Ethnic Integration Policy)** | Caps the proportion of each ethnic group (Chinese / Malay / Indian-Others) per **block** and per **neighbourhood** to keep estates racially mixed. Applies to **HDB flats and ECs (during MOP)** — NOT private property. | 族群比例政策。按**栋/邻里**限制各族群比例,促进种族融合。仅适用组屋,不适用私宅。 |
| **SPR Quota** | Separate cap limiting the proportion of **Singapore PR (non-Malaysian)** households per block/neighbourhood. Runs **in addition to** EIP. Malaysians are **exempt**. | 永久居民(非马来西亚籍)配额,与EIP叠加执行。马来西亚籍PR豁免。 |
| **Renting out** | Letting your flat (whole) or bedroom(s) to tenants. Whole-flat rental requires MOP + HDB approval; bedrooms can be rented earlier if owner still lives in. | 出租。整间须满MOP+HDB批准;睡房可在屋主仍居住的情况下提前出租。 |
| **HFE Letter (HDB Flat Eligibility letter)** | Mandatory upfront eligibility/financing assessment before buying any flat or granting an OTP. Replaces old HLE. | 购屋资格信,购买/给OTP前必须先取得。 |
| **Resale procedure** | The step-by-step HDB process from Intent to Sell/Buy → OTP → Request for Value → resale application → completion. | 转售流程:意向登记→OTP→估价→提交申请→交易完成。 |

**Why MOP/EIP/quota matter for the RES:** These determine *whether a transaction can even legally happen*. An RES who advertises or grants an OTP on a flat that fails EIP/SPR quota, or before MOP, exposes the client to a failed deal and exposes the RES to disciplinary action for inadequate due diligence (CEPCC).

## (b) Must-Know Rules & Exact Figures / 必背规则与数字

### B1. MOP by flat type (counted from key collection)

| Flat / scenario | MOP |
|---|---|
| Standard BTO / new flat (bought with grant or from HDB) | **5 years** |
| **Plus** flat | **10 years** |
| **Prime** flat (incl. older PLH) | **10 years** |
| Resale flat bought **with** a CPF housing grant | **5 years** |
| Resale flat bought **WITHOUT any grant and without an HDB loan** | **No MOP** |
| EC (Executive Condo) | **5 years** (SC/SPR only during this period) |

中文小结:Standard 5年,Plus/Prime 10年;不含津贴且自有资金购买的转售组屋无MOP。MOP从领钥匙起算,出租整间或空置期间不计入。

### B2. Ethnic Integration Policy (EIP) — maximum proportions

| Ethnic group | Neighbourhood limit | Block limit |
|---|---|---|
| **Chinese** | **84%** | **87%** |
| **Malay** | **22%** | **25%** |
| **Indian / Other** | **12%** | **15%** |

### B3. SPR Quota (non-Malaysian SPR households)

| Level | Limit |
|---|---|
| **Neighbourhood** | **5%** |
| **Block** | **8%** |

**How the caps behave:**
- When the relevant limit is **already reached/full**, a seller whose flat would *increase* that group's count **cannot sell to a buyer of that group** at that block/neighbourhood.
- **Exception:** if **buyer and seller are the SAME ethnic group** (or both SPR), the transaction is allowed — the proportion does not change. *(EIP/SPR check is only triggered when buyer's group differs from seller's.)*
- Quotas are recalculated and updated on the **1st of each month**.
- **Malaysians are EXEMPT** from the SPR quota.
- EIP/SPR apply to HDB resale and to ECs **during their MOP**; they do **NOT** apply to private property.

中文小结:配额满时,卖家不能把房子卖给会使该族群比例上升的买家;但**买卖双方同族**则不受限。配额每月1号更新,马来西亚籍PR豁免SPR配额。

### B4. Renting out — whole flat vs bedrooms

| Aspect | Whole flat | Bedroom(s) |
|---|---|---|
| Who may rent out | **SC owners only** (SPR cannot rent out whole flat) | SC **or** SPR owner (owner must continue living in flat) |

| HDB approval | Required (registered with HDB) | Required (registered with HDB) |

| Max tenancy per approval | **3 years** (tenants are SC/Malaysian) / **2 years** (if any tenant is a non-Malaysian non-citizen) | Same |
| Plus flat | **Whole-flat rental PROHIBITED** even after 10-yr MOP (bedrooms OK) | Bedrooms allowed |
| Prime flat | **Whole-flat rental PROHIBITED at all times** (bedrooms OK) | Bedrooms allowed |

**Bedroom rental — how many rooms may be rented:**
| Flat type | Max bedrooms rentable |
|---|---|
| 1- / 2-room | Cannot rent out bedrooms (whole-flat only, after MOP) |
| 3-room | **1** bedroom |
| 4-room or larger | **2** bedrooms |

## (c) Common Exam Traps / 易错点

1. **EIP/SPR same-group exception.** If buyer and seller are the **same ethnic group**, the transaction proceeds even when the quota is "full" — the proportion doesn't change. Distractors will say "cannot sell." 中文:同族买卖不受配额限制。

2. **EIP/SPR apply to HDB only, NOT private property.** Watch for stems applying EIP to a condo — wrong. 中文:EIP/SPR仅限组屋。

3. **Malaysians are EXEMPT** from both the SPR quota and the rental Non-Citizen Quota. A "Malaysian SPR" or "Malaysian tenant" does NOT count. 中文:马来西亚籍在SPR配额与非公民出租配额中都豁免。

4. **SPR cannot rent out the WHOLE flat** — only SC owners can. SPR may rent out **bedrooms** only (and must still live in the flat). 中文:永久居民只能出租睡房,不能出租整间。

5. **Bedrooms can be rented before MOP**; whole flat cannot. The "5-year MOP before any rental" statement is a trap — it applies only to whole-flat rental. 中文:睡房出租无需满MOP,整间才需要。

6. **Plus / Prime flats: NO whole-flat rental ever** — not even after the 10-year MOP. Bedrooms are still allowed. 中文:Plus/Prime永远不可整间出租。

7. **MOP counted from KEY COLLECTION**, not from OTP, SPA signing, or selection. Periods the flat is rented out/unoccupied are **excluded** (so MOP can effectively stretch beyond 5 calendar years). 中文:MOP从领钥匙起算,出租/空置期不计入。

8. **No income ceiling for resale flats bought without a grant.** Distractors insert "$14,000". 中文:无津贴转售无收入顶限。

9. **Two different quota families** — don't confuse:
   - **EIP/SPR quota** = who may **BUY** (resale purchase).
   - **Non-Citizen Quota (8%/11%)** = who may be a **TENANT** when renting out the whole flat.
   These are separate sets of numbers. 中文:购买看EIP/SPR配额;出租看非公民配额(8%/11%)。

10. **Fee caps:** Option Fee ≤ $1,000; Option + Exercise combined ≤ **$5,000**. Don't pick "$5,000 option fee." 中文:订金≤$1,000,两笔合计≤$5,000。

11. **Option period = 21 calendar days, expires 4 pm.** Private OTP (~14 days) is a different number — don't cross-apply. 中文:组屋OTP 21天,下午4点截止。

12. **Occupancy cap is the relaxed figure (8 for 4-room+) only until 31 Dec 2026** — valid for the July 2026 exam. 中文:入住上限放宽到2026年底,考试期间仍有效。

## (d) Worked Examples / 计算与情景例题

**Example 1 — EIP block quota (same-group exception)**
A Chinese seller's block is at the Chinese block limit of 87%.
- Buyer is **Chinese** → allowed (Chinese proportion unchanged, same-group exception). ✓
- Buyer is **Malay** (and Malay quota has headroom) → allowed only if Malay limit not breached; Chinese count *drops* when a Chinese seller sells to a non-Chinese, so the Chinese cap is not the issue here — the **buyer's** group (Malay) cap is what's checked. ✓ if Malay <25% block / <22% nbhd.
- Key point: the check is on the **buyer's** ethnic group at the block AND neighbourhood, triggered because buyer ≠ seller group.

**Example 2 — SPR quota**
A block has SPR households at **8%** (block cap reached). A SC seller wants to sell to a **non-Malaysian SPR** buyer.
- SPR block quota is **full** → **transaction rejected**. ✗
- If the buyer were a **Malaysian SPR** → **exempt** → allowed (subject to EIP). ✓

**Example 3 — Renting out eligibility**
Owner is an **SPR**, flat passed 5-year MOP, owner lives overseas now.
- Whole flat? **No** — SPR cannot rent whole flat, and owner not residing. ✗
- Bedrooms? **No** — owner must continue living in the flat to rent bedrooms. ✗

**Example 4 — Non-Citizen Quota (rental)**
4-room flat (SC owner), MOP done. Block NCQ is at **11%** (full). Owner wants to rent the whole flat to a **Filipino** tenant.
- NCQ full → can only rent to **Singaporeans / Malaysians**. Filipino tenant **rejected**. ✗
- Occupancy: up to **8** unrelated persons allowed (4-room, relaxed cap to 31 Dec 2026). ✓

**Example 5 — Resale fee cap**
Seller wants Option Fee $2,000 and Exercise Fee $4,000 (total $6,000).
- Option Fee $2,000 > $1,000 cap → **not allowed**. ✗
- Even if Option Fee were $1,000, total must be ≤ **$5,000**, so Exercise Fee ≤ $4,000. Correct combo e.g. $1,000 + $4,000 = $5,000. ✓

# P1.8 Private Residential Property & Residential Property Act (Foreign Ownership)

## P1.8 Private Residential Property & Residential Property Act (Foreign Ownership) — Revision Notes
**RES Paper 1 · Exam date: 18 July 2026 · Figures verified against SLA, IRAS, CEA, MAS primary sources (current as of June 2026)**

> 货币规则 / Currency rule: You will NOT be tested on policy changes made within ~1 month before the exam (after ~18 June 2026). All figures below are the operative ones for the 18 July 2026 sitting.

---

## (a) Key Concepts / 核心概念

### 1. The two parallel regimes you must never confuse
Foreign residential purchase in Singapore is governed by **TWO separate gatekeepers** that operate independently:

| Regime | Question it answers | Administered by | Statute |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Residential Property Act 1976 (RPA)** | *Am I ALLOWED to buy this property at all?* (eligibility / approval) | **SLA – Land Dealings Approval Unit (LDAU)** | Residential Property Act 1976 |
| **ABSD (Stamp Duties Act)** | *How much EXTRA tax do I pay if allowed?* (cost) | **IRAS** | Stamp Duties Act 1929 |

> 中文核心: RPA 管"能不能买"(批准/资格),ABSD 管"买了要交多少税"(成本)。两者完全独立——即使获得 LDAU 批准买有地住宅,外国人仍要交 60% ABSD。

### 2. Who is a "Foreign Person" (RPA definition)
A **"foreign person"** under the RPA is anyone who is **NOT** a Singapore Citizen, a Singapore company, a Singapore society, or a Singapore limited liability partnership. Crucially:

- **Singapore Permanent Residents (SPRs) ARE "foreign persons" under the RPA.** This is the single most-tested trap. A PR still needs LDAU approval to buy restricted (landed) property.
- Foreign companies, foreign-controlled entities, and unincorporated associations are also "foreign persons."

> 中文: RPA 下"外国人"包括**新加坡永久居民(PR)**。PR 买有地住宅同样需要 LDAU 批准——这是最常考的陷阱。

### 3. Restricted vs Non-restricted property
The RPA classifies residential property into **restricted** (need LDAU approval) and **non-restricted** (free to buy, no approval). The dividing line is essentially **landed = restricted; non-landed strata = non-restricted**, with specific carve-outs.

> 中文: 核心分界线——**有地住宅 = 受限制(需批准)**;**分层公寓单位 = 不受限制(自由购买)**。

## (c) Common Exam Traps / 易错点

1. **SPR = foreign person under the RPA.** A PR needs LDAU approval for landed property, just like any other foreigner. (中文:PR 也算"外国人",买有地房屋同样需批准。)
2. **Foreigners CAN buy a condo unit freely, but CANNOT buy landed / vacant land / GCB** without LDAU approval. Distractors swap these.
3. **Strata-landed: location decides.** Inside an approved condo = **non-restricted**; outside = **restricted**. Same physical house type, opposite answer.
4. **RPA approval ≠ ABSD.** Getting LDAU approval does NOT exempt a foreigner from the **60% ABSD**. They are independent.
5. **BSD is profile-independent; only ABSD depends on citizenship.** A common distractor claims foreigners pay higher BSD — false.
6. **FTA list is closed and specific:** US, Iceland, Liechtenstein, Norway, Switzerland. Note the EFTA four cover **citizens AND PRs**, but the US covers **citizens only**. (Watch a trap that adds e.g. the EU, UK, China, or Australia — none are exempt.)
7. **Leasehold landed ≤ 7 years is NON-restricted**; a longer lease or freehold landed becomes restricted. The 7-year figure is examinable.
8. **Sentosa Cove ≠ mainland.** Sentosa Cove has a **1,800 sq m** cap (not 15,000 sq ft), no "exceptional contribution" bar, and **no 5-year MOP**. Distractors mix Sentosa and mainland conditions.
9. **PR-for-≥5-years + exceptional economic contribution** is the mainland approval criterion. A distractor saying "any PR may buy landed" is false.
10. **Approved restricted purchase = owner-occupation only, no rental, no subdivision, no disposal within 5 years.** A trap may say the owner can rent it out — false.
11. **Fully privatised EC (≥10 yrs) is non-restricted**; an EC within MOP cannot be sold to foreigners. Watch the "5 years" vs "10 years" swap.
12. **Read "EXCEPT / NOT" stems carefully** — e.g. "Which of the following does NOT require LDAU approval?"

## (d) Worked Examples / 计算例题

These calculations turn on ABSD because RPA approval itself is qualitative (no formula). BSD = higher of price or market value.

**Worked Example 1 — Foreigner (no FTA) buys a $2,000,000 condo (1st property)**
- This is a **condo unit → NON-restricted**, so **no LDAU approval** needed.
- **BSD** (residential): 1%×180k + 2%×180k + 3%×640k + 4%×500k + 5%×500k
 = 1,800 + 3,600 + 19,200 + 20,000 + 25,000 = **$69,600**
- **ABSD**: Foreigner = **60% × 2,000,000 = $1,200,000**
- **Total stamp duty = $69,600 + $1,200,000 = $1,269,600**

**Worked Example 2 — US citizen buys the same $2,000,000 condo (1st property)**
- US citizen → **FTA exemption → treated as SC**, 1st property ABSD = **0%**.
- BSD unchanged = **$69,600**; ABSD = **$0**.
- **Total = $69,600.** (Saving vs Example 1 = **$1,200,000** in ABSD.)

**Worked Example 3 — Singapore PR buys a $1,500,000 condo (1st property)**
- Non-restricted; no approval needed.
- **BSD**: 1%×180k + 2%×180k + 3%×640k + 4%×500k = 1,800 + 3,600 + 19,200 + 20,000 = **$44,600**
- **ABSD**: SPR 1st = **5% × 1,500,000 = $75,000**
- **Total = $119,600.**

**Worked Example 4 — Eligibility (no calculation)**
A 6-year SPR with documented exceptional economic contribution applies to LDAU to buy a $4m bungalow on the mainland (land area 9,000 sq ft).
- Bungalow = **restricted** → needs LDAU approval. ✔ PR ≥5 yrs, ✔ contribution, ✔ ≤15,000 sq ft.
- If approved: **owner-occupation only, no rental, no disposal within 5 years.**
- Plus **60% ABSD** ($2.4m) on top of BSD — RPA approval does not waive ABSD.

## 速记总结 / Quick Recall

- **RPA = "can I buy?" (SLA/LDAU). ABSD = "what tax?" (IRAS). 两者独立。**
- **"Foreign person" under RPA INCLUDES Singapore PRs.** PR 也是外国人。
- **Non-restricted (free):** condo unit, private apartment, strata-landed *inside* approved condo, leasehold landed ≤7 yrs, commercial/industrial, hotel, **fully privatised EC ≥10 yrs**, HDB (per HDB rules).
- **Restricted (need LDAU approval):** vacant land, ALL landed (terrace/semi-D/bungalow/GCB), strata-landed *outside* condo, **Sentosa Cove** landed, residential shophouse.
- **Mainland approval criteria:** PR **≥5 years** + **exceptional economic contribution** (high bar).
- **Mainland conditions:** ≤**15,000 sq ft**, owner-occupation only, **no rental**, no subdivision, **no disposal within 5 years**; fee ~**$1,220**; ~**30 working days**.
- **Sentosa Cove:** ≤**1,800 sq m**, no "exceptional contribution" needed, **no 5-year MOP** — more permissive.
- **ABSD foreigner = 60%** on every residential property (2026, unchanged). **PR = 5% / 30% / 35%.**
- **FTA = treated as SC (0%/20%/30%):** **US citizens**; **citizens + PRs of Iceland, Liechtenstein, Norway, Switzerland.**
- **BSD is the same for everyone** — only ABSD varies by nationality/count.
- **EC: 0–5 yr SC/SPR only → 5–10 yr SC/SPR resale → ≥10 yr anyone (incl. foreigners).**
- **Leasehold landed ≤7 yrs = non-restricted; longer/freehold landed = restricted.**

## P1.9 Revision Notes — Commercial & Industrial Property, URA Zoning, GST on Property
**RES Paper 1 (CU1–CU2) | Exam: 18 July 2026 | Figures verified against IRAS, URA, SLA, MAS (current June 2026)**

> Currency rule: You will NOT be tested on changes announced/implemented within ~1 month before the exam (after ~18 June 2026). All figures below are current and safe.

---

## (a) Key Concepts / 核心概念

This topic spans three connected pillars. Understand how they interlock — the exam loves to test whether you can keep them apart.

**1. Property classification (residential vs non-residential).** Singapore's whole regulatory system pivots on whether a property is *residential* or *non-residential* (commercial / industrial). This single distinction drives: foreign-ownership rules (RPA), GST treatment, ABSD liability, property-tax rate, and financing rules. *中文：一栋房产是"住宅"还是"非住宅"，决定了外国人能否购买、是否收 GST、是否要交 ABSD、物业税率等几乎所有规则。*

**2. URA Master Plan & zoning.** The Urban Redevelopment Authority (URA) is Singapore's national land-use planning authority and conservation authority. The **Master Plan** is the statutory land-use plan that assigns every parcel a **zone** (Residential, Commercial, White, B1/B2 Industrial, Business Park, etc.) and an **intensity** (Gross Plot Ratio + height). Zoning = what you may legally *use* the land for; plot ratio = how *much* you may build. *中文：URA 是新加坡的土地规划机构；总体规划（Master Plan）规定每块地的用途分区和开发强度。*

**3. GST on property.** GST (Goods & Services Tax) at **9%** is a consumption tax. The sale/lease of **residential property is an EXEMPT supply** (no GST). The sale/lease of **commercial & industrial property is a STANDARD-RATED supply (9% GST)**. *中文：住宅买卖/出租免 GST；商业、工业房产买卖/出租要收 9% GST。*

**Industrial sub-concepts you must know:**
- **B1 (Light Industrial)** vs **B2 (General/Heavy Industrial)** vs **Business Park** — distinguished by pollution/nuisance buffer and permitted trades.
- **60:40 rule** — minimum 60% of GFA for core industrial use; max 40% ancillary.
- **JTC** (Jurong Town Corporation) — the master industrial landlord granting industrial leases.

**Foreign ownership concept (RPA 1976):** "Restricted residential property" needs LDAU/SLA approval for foreigners; **commercial and industrial property is NOT restricted** — foreigners buy freely. *中文：外国人买商业、工业地产自由，无需审批；住宅（landed 等）则受限。*

## (b) Must-Know Rules & Exact Figures

### Table 1 — GST on Property (rate 9%, since 1 Jan 2024; unchanged 2026)

| Property type | Sale | Lease/Rent | GST supply type |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Residential** (HDB, condo, landed, apartment) | No GST | No GST | **EXEMPT supply** |
| **Commercial** (shops, offices, retail) | **9% GST** | **9% GST** | **Standard-rated** |
| **Industrial** (B1, B2, warehouse, factory) | **9% GST** | **9% GST** | **Standard-rated** |
| **Mixed-use** (commercial + residential) | GST on **non-residential portion only** | Same | Split |
| **Furnished residential** | Fixtures = exempt (with property); **movable furniture/fittings = 9% GST** | Same | Split |

- GST is charged/collected only by a **GST-registered** seller/landlord. **Registration is compulsory when taxable turnover exceeds S$1 million** (per calendar year, or expected next 12 months).
- Buyer of commercial/industrial property pays GST **on top of** the price; if buyer is GST-registered, the GST is recoverable as input tax.

*中文：GST 税率 9%；住宅免税，商业工业征税。卖方/房东须年营业额超 100 万新元才强制注册 GST。带家具的住宅，活动家具部分要收 GST，固定装置随房免税。*

| Value portion | Residential rate | Non-residential rate (commercial/industrial) |

*中文：商业、工业地产的 BSD 最高只到 5%（超过 150 万部分都是 5%），没有住宅那种超过 300 万的 6% 档。BSD 按"成交价或市值取高者"计算，不分国籍。*

## (c) Common Exam Traps / 易错点

1. **GST vs ABSD swap.** Commercial/industrial = **GST (9%), NO ABSD**. Residential = **ABSD, NO GST**. Distractors will offer "9% ABSD on commercial" — wrong. *中文：商业工业收 GST 不收 ABSD，别搞反。*

2. **Residential lease is EXEMPT, not zero-rated.** Exempt ≠ zero-rated. Residential property = **exempt supply**; commercial/industrial = standard-rated. (Zero-rated would be exports/international services.) *中文：住宅是"免税供应(exempt)"，不是"零税率(zero-rated)"。*

3. **Non-residential BSD caps at 5%.** No 6% tier (that's residential-only, above $3m). *中文：非住宅 BSD 最高 5%，无 6% 档。*

4. **B1 vs B2 buffer numbers.** B1 = **≤50m** buffer (light/clean); B2 = **>50m** (general/heavy). Don't reverse. The fact pack's "100m" describes the practical B2 heavy-industry buffer, but URA's defining cut-off is **50m**. *中文：B1 ≤50 米，B2 >50 米。*

5. **60:40 direction.** ≥**60% core industrial**, ≤**40% ancillary** — not the other way round. *中文：60% 核心工业，40% 配套，别反。*

6. **Foreigners CAN buy commercial & industrial freely** — no LDAU approval. But **"Commercial & Residential" mixed-zone sites are treated as residential** (restricted since 20 Jul 2023). *中文：纯商工自由；商住混合受限。*

7. **No residential use in industrial zones** — workers' dormitories/quarters are tightly controlled and generally not "residential use" in the ordinary sense. Don't assume you can live in a B1/B2 unit.

8. **Master Plan ~5 years vs Concept Plan ~10 years** — distractors swap these. *中文：总体规划 5 年，概念规划 10 年。*

9. **GPR formula = GFA ÷ Site Area** (not Site ÷ GFA). Higher GPR = MORE intensity. *中文：容积率 = 建筑面积 ÷ 地块面积。*

10. **Furnished residential rental:** the property itself is GST-exempt, but **movable furniture/fittings attract 9% GST**. A common "all exempt" distractor.

11. **Industrial has SSD; commercial does NOT.** Industrial SSD = 15/10/5/0% over 3 years (acquired on/after 12 Jan 2013). Pure commercial property has **no SSD at all**. *中文：工业地产有 SSD（15/10/5%）；商业地产没有 SSD。*

12. **Property tax for non-residential = flat 10% AV** (not progressive). *中文：非住宅物业税固定 10%。*

13. **GST registration threshold = $1 million** taxable turnover (not the property price). A small landlord under $1m turnover need not be GST-registered and won't charge GST even on a commercial unit.

## (d) Worked Examples / 计算例题

### Example 1 — BSD on a commercial property ($2,000,000)

Non-residential tiers: 1% / 2% / 3% / 4% / 5% (cap).

| Band | Amount | Rate | BSD |
|---|---|---|---|
| First $180,000 | 180,000 | 1% | $1,800 |
| Next $180,000 | 180,000 | 2% | $3,600 |
| Next $640,000 | 640,000 | 3% | $19,200 |
| Next $500,000 | 500,000 | 4% | $20,000 |
| Remaining (above $1.5m) | 500,000 | 5% | $25,000 |
| **Total BSD** | **$2,000,000** | | **$69,600** |

Note: a **residential** property at $2m would have the same BSD here ($69,600), because the 6% tier only bites above $3m. But the commercial buyer **also pays 9% GST = $180,000** (if seller GST-registered) and pays **NO ABSD**, whereas a residential second-property SC buyer would add 20% ABSD = $400,000. *中文：商业地产 $200 万，BSD = $69,600，外加 9% GST = $18 万；无 ABSD。*

### Example 2 — GST on commercial purchase

Commercial unit, price $1,500,000, seller GST-registered:
- **GST = 9% × $1,500,000 = $135,000** payable on top of price.
- Total cash outlay (price + GST) = $1,635,000 (plus BSD).
- If buyer is GST-registered and uses unit for taxable business, the $135,000 is recoverable as input tax.

### Example 3 — Industrial SSD

Buyer acquires a B2 factory on 1 Mar 2024, sells it 18 months later (within >1–2 year band):
- Industrial SSD band >1–2 years = **10%**.
- Sale price/market value (higher) = $3,000,000 → **SSD = 10% × $3,000,000 = $300,000**.
*中文：工业地产持有 1–2 年内转售，SSD = 10%。*

### Example 4 — Gross Plot Ratio

Site area = 5,000 sqm; GPR = 2.5.
- **Max GFA = GPR × Site Area = 2.5 × 5,000 = 12,500 sqm.**
*中文：地块 5,000 平米，GPR 2.5 → 最大建筑面积 12,500 平米。*

### Example 5 — 60:40 rule check

A B1 development has 12,500 sqm GFA. Owner wants 5,500 sqm of office/showroom ancillary.
- Max ancillary = 40% × 12,500 = **5,000 sqm**. Proposed 5,500 sqm **exceeds** the cap → not allowed (must be ≤5,000 sqm; ≥7,500 sqm core industrial).
*中文：12,500 平米的 B1，配套最多 40% = 5,000 平米；申请 5,500 平米超标，不获批。*

## 速记总结 / Quick Recall

- **GST = 9%.** Residential = **EXEMPT**; commercial & industrial = **9% standard-rated** (sale AND lease). *住宅免，商工 9%。*
- **GST registration threshold = $1m** taxable turnover. Furnished residential: movable furniture = 9% GST, fixtures exempt.
- **Commercial/industrial: pay GST, NO ABSD.** Residential: pay ABSD, NO GST. *商工收GST无ABSD；住宅反之。*
- **Non-residential BSD caps at 5%** (no 6% tier). Same bands as residential up to $1.5m.
- **Property tax non-residential = flat 10% of AV.**
- **SSD:** industrial = 15/10/5/0% over 3 yrs; **commercial = no SSD**; residential = 16/12/8/4% over 4 yrs (purchases on/after 4 Jul 2025).
- **B1 = ≤50m buffer (light/clean); B2 = >50m (general/heavy); Business Park = high-tech R&D.**
- **60:40 rule: ≥60% core industrial, ≤40% ancillary. No residential use in industrial zones.**
- **Foreigners buy commercial & industrial FREELY** (no LDAU approval). **"Commercial & Residential" mixed sites = treated as residential → approval needed** (since 20 Jul 2023).
- **URA Master Plan ~every 5 yrs** (zoning + intensity); **Concept Plan ~every 10 yrs** (broader). Change of use needs URA planning permission.
- **GPR = GFA ÷ Site Area.** Higher GPR = more buildable = higher value. Max GFA = GPR × Site Area.
- **JTC industrial lease = 30+30 years; subletting needs prior written JTC approval.**
- **BSD/ABSD/SSD all on the HIGHER of price or market value.**
- Mantra: **住宅 = ABSD + exempt; 商工 = GST 9% + no ABSD; 工业还有 SSD，商业没有。**

# P1.10 Financing: LTV, TDSR, MSR, Loan Types & Cooling Measures

## P1.10 Financing: LTV, TDSR, MSR, Loan Types & Cooling Measures
### RES Paper 1 — Exam-Focused Revision Notes (Sitting: 18 July 2026)

> **Currency rule reminder:** You will NOT be tested on any policy change announced/implemented within ~1 month of the exam (after ~18 June 2026). All figures below are verified current as of June 2026 against MAS, HDB, IRAS, CPF and CEA primary sources.
> 货币规则提醒：考前一个月内（约2026年6月18日之后）才公布或实施的新政策不会考。下列数字截至2026年6月已核实。

---

## (a) Key Concepts / 核心概念

| Term | What it controls | Plain meaning |
|---|---|---|
| **LTV (Loan-to-Value)** | **How much you can BORROW** as a % of property value | Caps the *loan size*. The rest is your downpayment. 控制贷款额度。 |
| **TDSR (Total Debt Servicing Ratio)** | **How much of your INCOME can go to ALL debts** | Caps total monthly debt repayment vs gross income. 控制总还债比例。 |
| **MSR (Mortgage Servicing Ratio)** | How much of income can go to the **property loan only** | HDB & developer-EC only. 仅适用于组屋和向发展商购买的执行共管公寓。 |
| **Stress-test (medium-term) rate** | Interest rate used to *compute* TDSR/MSR | A floor rate banks must apply even if actual rate is lower. 用于计算的最低利率。 |
| **Cooling measures** | Government tools to moderate demand/prices | LTV cuts, TDSR/MSR, ABSD, SSD, loan-tenure caps. 房市降温措施。 |
| **Cash vs CPF downpayment** | Source of the non-loan portion | Part of downpayment must be **hard cash**; rest can be CPF OA. 部分首付必须现金。 |

**The big picture / 全局逻辑:**
A buyer is constrained by **THREE simultaneous limits** — the actual loan granted is the **LOWEST** result of:
1. **LTV** (loan ≤ X% of value),
2. **TDSR** (total debt ≤ 55% of gross income), and
3. **MSR** (for HDB/EC only: property loan ≤ 30% of gross income).

> 中文笔记：实际贷款额取三者中最小值——LTV、TDSR、MSR（组屋/EC才有MSR）。三个限制同时成立，取最严的那个。

**Who regulates what:**
- **MAS** sets LTV/TDSR/MSR/stress-test for **bank (FI) loans**.
- **HDB** sets the LTV for the **HDB concessionary loan** (75%).
- **IRAS** administers stamp-duty cooling measures (ABSD/SSD/BSD).

## (b) Must-Know Rules & Exact Figures / 必背规则与数字

### B1. LTV Limits — Bank (FI) Loans

| Outstanding housing loans | Max LTV (all conditions met) | LTV if tenure >30yr (>25yr HDB) **OR** loan extends beyond age 65 | Min cash downpayment |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1st housing loan** | **75%** | 55% | **5%** cash (at 75% LTV); 10% cash (at 55%) |
| **2nd housing loan** | **45%** | 25% | **25%** cash |
| **3rd or more** | **35%** | 15% | **25%** cash |

**Conditions for the HIGHER LTV (75/45/35):**
- Loan tenure ≤ **30 years** (private) / ≤ **25 years** (HDB flat), **AND**
- Loan does **NOT** extend beyond borrower's **age 65**.
If either condition fails → the lower LTV band (55/25/15) applies.

> 中文笔记：要拿到最高LTV，贷款年限不能超过30年（组屋25年），且还清贷款时借款人不超过65岁。否则只能拿较低的LTV。

### B2. LTV — HDB Concessionary Loan

| Item | Value |
|---|---|
| **Max LTV** | **75%** |
| Previous LTV | 80% (**cut to 75% on 20 Aug 2024** — a cooling measure) |
| History | 90%→85% (Dec 2021) → 80% (Sep 2022) → **75% (Aug 2024)** |
| Downpayment | **25%**, **fully payable by CPF OA** — no mandatory cash component |

> 中文笔记：HDB优惠贷款LTV是75%（2024年8月20日从80%下调，属降温措施）。25%首付可全用公积金（OA），不强制现金。

### B3. TDSR / MSR / Stress-Test

| Metric | Exact figure | Applies to |
|---|---|---|
| **TDSR** | **≤ 55%** of gross monthly income (ALL debts: car, personal, credit-card, other property) | **All** property loans (since 16 Dec 2021; cut from 60%) |
| **MSR** | **≤ 30%** of gross monthly income (property loan repayment only) | **HDB flats & ECs bought directly from developer ONLY** |
| **Stress-test floor — residential** | **4.0% p.a.** (or actual rate, whichever higher) | Computing TDSR & MSR (since 30 Sep 2022) |
| **Stress-test floor — non-residential** | **5.0% p.a.** | Computing TDSR |

> 中文笔记：TDSR上限55%（所有债务）；MSR上限30%（只算房贷，且只适用组屋和直接向发展商买的EC）。计算时按4%（住宅）或5%（非住宅）的"压力测试利率"，即使实际利率更低也用这个。

### B4. Loan Tenure Caps

| Loan type | Max tenure |
|---|---|
| Private property — bank loan | **35 years** |
| HDB flat — bank loan | **30 years** |
| HDB concessionary loan | **25 years** |

(Note: choosing a tenure beyond 30yr private / 25yr HDB, OR beyond age 65, drops you to the LOWER LTV band — see B1.)

> 中文笔记：私宅银行贷款最长35年，组屋银行贷款30年，HDB优惠贷款25年。超过30/25年或超过65岁会触发较低LTV。

### B5. Loan Types — HDB Concessionary vs Bank Loan

| Feature | HDB Concessionary Loan | Bank (FI) Loan |
|---|---|---|

| Max LTV | 75% | 75%/45%/35% (by loan count) |

## (c) Common Exam Traps / 易错点

1. **LTV ≠ TDSR ≠ MSR.** LTV caps the *loan amount* (vs property value); TDSR/MSR cap *monthly repayment* (vs income). Don't confuse "75%" (LTV) with "55%/30%" (income ratios). 别把LTV的75%和TDSR/MSR的55%/30%搞混。
2. **The binding constraint is the LOWEST of LTV, TDSR, and MSR** — the actual loan is whichever gives the smallest amount, not the largest. 取最小值，不是最大值。
3. **MSR applies ONLY to HDB flats and ECs bought from a developer** — NOT to private property and NOT to resale EC bought after privatisation. Private property has NO MSR, only TDSR. MSR只适用组屋和直接向发展商买的EC。
4. **HDB concessionary loan LTV is 75% (not 80%, not 90%)** — cut on 20 Aug 2024. A classic "outdated figure" distractor. 注意是75%，2024年8月才改的。
5. **Stress-test rate is 4% (residential), even when actual interest is ~2.6%/3%.** Calculations MUST use the higher of 4% or actual. 计算用4%，不是实际利率。
6. **Higher LTV requires BOTH conditions** (tenure ≤30/25yr AND not beyond age 65). Failing **either one** drops you to 55/25/15%. 两个条件都要满足。
7. **2nd loan = 45% (not 50%), 3rd+ = 35%.** And both require **25% cash** downpayment (vs only 5% cash on 1st loan). 第二套45%、第三套35%，现金首付25%。
8. **ABSD depends on buyer profile + count; BSD does NOT** (BSD is the same for everyone). 别把ABSD和BSD混。
9. **SSD = SELLER pays; ABSD/BSD = BUYER pays.** And SSD is now a **4-year** window (16/12/8/4%) for purchases on/after 4 Jul 2025. SSD是卖家付、4年制。
10. **All cooling-measure stamp duties use the HIGHER of price or market value** — not the lower, not the average. 取较高者。
11. **Watch "EXCEPT / NOT / ONLY" inversions** in the stem — they flip the correct answer. 注意否定词。
12. **HDB downpayment (25%) can be fully CPF** — no mandatory cash; but **bank loan** 1st-loan 5% must be **cash**. HDB可全用公积金，银行贷款的5%首付必须现金。

## (d) Worked Examples / 计算示例

### Example 1 — LTV and downpayment (1st bank loan)
Property price = market value = **$1,500,000**, first housing loan, tenure 25 yrs, borrower aged 35 (loan ends at 60 → conditions met → 75% LTV).

- Max loan = 75% × $1,500,000 = **$1,125,000**
- Total downpayment = 25% = **$375,000**
- Minimum **cash** = 5% × $1,500,000 = **$75,000**
- Remaining 20% ($300,000) = CPF OA and/or cash.

> 中文：贷款最高112.5万，首付37.5万，其中至少7.5万现金，其余可用公积金。

### Example 2 — LTV drops due to long tenure / age
Same $1,500,000, but borrower is **55**, wants **30-year** tenure → loan ends at age 85 (beyond 65) → **lower band applies = 55% LTV**.

- Max loan = 55% × $1,500,000 = **$825,000**
- Downpayment = 45% = **$675,000**, of which min cash = **10%** = $150,000.

> 中文：因为还款超过65岁，LTV降到55%，贷款只剩82.5万，现金首付10%（15万）。

### Example 3 — TDSR ceiling (the binding constraint)
Buyer gross monthly income = **$10,000**; existing car loan = **$800/mth**; buying **private** property, 30-yr tenure.

- TDSR cap on repayment = 55% × $10,000 = **$5,500**
- Less existing debt $800 → max **new housing repayment** = **$4,700/mth**
- Convert to loan using **stress-test 4%**, 30 yrs:
 Monthly factor at 4%/30yr ≈ **$4.774 per $1,000** borrowed.
 Max loan ≈ $4,700 ÷ 4.774 × 1,000 ≈ **$984,500**.
- Compare with LTV cap; **the smaller figure is the actual maximum loan.**

> 中文：TDSR下，可用月供 = 55%×收入 − 现有债务 = $4,700。再按4%、30年折算成贷款额（约$98.45万），与LTV额度比较，取小者。

### Example 4 — MSR for an HDB flat
Buying **HDB resale**, gross income = **$8,000/mth**, no other debt, 25-yr tenure.

- **MSR** cap = 30% × $8,000 = **$2,400/mth** (property loan only)
- **TDSR** cap = 55% × $8,000 = $4,400/mth (here MSR is tighter → MSR binds)
- Max repayment = **$2,400**; convert at **4%, 25 yrs** (factor ≈ $5.278 per $1,000):
 Max loan ≈ $2,400 ÷ 5.278 × 1,000 ≈ **$454,700**, then capped further by **75% LTV**.

> 中文：组屋同时受MSR(30%)和TDSR(55%)约束，通常MSR更严先触发。月供上限$2,400，用4%/25年折算，再受75% LTV限制，取最小值。

### Example 5 — ABSD on a 2nd property (SC)
SC buying 2nd residential property, price = value = **$1,200,000**.

- ABSD = **20%** × $1,200,000 = **$240,000** (on top of BSD).
- BSD (residential $1.2m) = 1%×180k + 2%×180k + 3%×640k + 4%×200k = **$32,600**.

> 中文：新加坡公民买第二套房，ABSD 20% = $240,000，另加买方印花税BSD $32,600。

- **LTV (loan size):** 1st **75%** · 2nd **45%** · 3rd+ **35%**. Lower band 55/25/15 if tenure >30yr (>25 HDB) **OR** beyond **age 65**.
- **Cash downpayment:** 1st loan **5%** cash (at 75%); 2nd/3rd **25%** cash. HDB loan 25% **= CPF OK, no cash needed**.

# P1.11 Stamp Duties: BSD, ABSD, SSD (current rates + calculation)

## P1.11 Stamp Duties: BSD, ABSD, SSD — Revision Notes (RES Paper 1)
**Exam: 18 July 2026 | Figures verified against IRAS primary sources, current June 2026**

> **Currency rule:** You will NOT be tested on changes implemented within ~1 month before the exam (after ~18 June 2026). All figures below are current and examinable.

---

## (a) Key Concepts / 核心概念

**Stamp duty (印花税)** is a tax on documents (instruments) executed for the sale, transfer or lease of property. It is a **document-based tax**, not a transaction tax — no document, no duty (with anti-avoidance exceptions). There are three duties on property *acquisition/disposal* you must master:

| Duty | Full name | Who pays | When | On what |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **BSD** | Buyer's Stamp Duty | **Buyer** | On purchase/acquisition | ALL property (residential + non-residential) |
| **ABSD** | Additional Buyer's Stamp Duty | **Buyer** | On purchase, *on top of* BSD | **Residential only** |
| **SSD** | Seller's Stamp Duty | **Seller** | On sale within holding period | Residential & industrial |

**THE GOLDEN RULE (考点核心):** BSD, ABSD and SSD are ALL computed on the **HIGHER of the purchase price OR the market value** — never the lower.
> 中文: 三种印花税都按"成交价"与"市场价"中的**较高者**计算,千万别选较低者。

**Key conceptual splits:**
- **BSD depends only on the property** (price/value + residential vs non-residential). It does NOT depend on who the buyer is.
- **ABSD depends on the BUYER's profile** (citizenship/entity status) AND the count of residential properties owned.
- **SSD depends on the HOLDING PERIOD** (how long the seller held the property).

> 中文: BSD看"房子",ABSD看"买家身份+第几套",SSD看"持有多久"。三句话记住区别。

**Payment deadline:** Stamp duty payable within **14 days** of execution if the document is signed **in Singapore**, or **30 days** if signed **overseas**. (SSD: within 14 days of sale/disposal.)

## (b) Must-Know Rules & Exact Figures

### B1. BSD — Buyer's Stamp Duty (current rates effective 15 Feb 2023)

| Portion of price/value | **Residential** | **Non-residential** |
|---|---|---|
| First $180,000 | 1% | 1% |
| Next $180,000 (→ $360k) | 2% | 2% |
| Next $640,000 (→ $1m) | 3% | 3% |
| Next $500,000 (→ $1.5m) | 4% | 4% |
| Next $1,500,000 (→ $3m) | 5% | **5% (everything above $1.5m)** |
| Above $3,000,000 | **6%** | — |

**Critical distinction:** Residential goes up to **6%** (above $3m); non-residential **caps at 5%** (above $1.5m). BSD is rounded **down** to the nearest dollar; minimum duty applies.

> 中文: BSD是"累进税"(marginal),逐档计算,不是整套乘一个税率。住宅最高6%,非住宅最高5%。买家身份不影响BSD。

**Shortcut formulas (memorise — they save time in the exam):**
- Residential, price **$1m–$1.5m**: BSD = **(3% × price) − $15,400**
- Residential, price **$360k–$1m**: BSD = (3% × price) − $5,400
- Residential, price **$180k–$360k**: BSD = (2% × price) − $1,800

### B2. ABSD — Additional Buyer's Stamp Duty (residential only; rates effective 27 Apr 2023, unchanged 2026)

ABSD is a **flat rate on the entire price/value** (NOT marginal/tiered like BSD), determined by buyer profile and number of residential properties already owned.

| Buyer profile | 1st property | 2nd property | 3rd & subsequent |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Singapore Citizen (SC)** | **0%** | **20%** | **30%** |
| **Singapore PR (SPR)** | **5%** | **30%** | **35%** |
| **Foreigner (FR)** | **60%** | 60% | 60% |
| **Entity** (company) | **65%** | 65% | 65% |
| **Trustee** (living trust) | **65%** | 65% | 65% |
| **Housing Developer** | 35% (remittable, conditions) **+ 5% non-remittable** | — | — |

**FTA exemption (treated as SC → 0% / 20% / 30%):** Nationals AND PRs of the **U**SA*, **I**celand, **L**iechtenstein, **N**orway, **S**witzerland.
> Mnemonic: **U.S. + the "NILS" four** (Norway, Iceland, Liechtenstein, Switzerland). *US: only US citizens (US PRs not covered same way — but for exam: US citizens treated as SC).

**Married-couple ABSD remission:** A married couple with ≥1 SC buying a 2nd residential property can claim a **refund** of ABSD paid on the 2nd, *provided they sell their 1st property within the timeline* (within 6 months of purchase of completed 2nd property / TOP, etc.).

> 中文: ABSD是**整套乘一个税率**(flat rate),不是累进。SC第一套免税(0%)是最大福利。外国人统一60%,公司/信托65%。FTA五国(美/冰/列/挪/瑞)国民按SC算。

SSD discourages short-term "flipping." Rate depends on holding period from date of purchase (OTP acceptance) to date of sale.

**CURRENT — residential purchased ON/AFTER 4 Jul 2025** (holding period extended to **4 years**, rates +4 ppt per tier):

| Holding period | SSD rate |
|---|---|
| ≤ 1 year | **16%** |
| > 1 to 2 years | **12%** |
| > 2 to 3 years | **8%** |
| > 3 to 4 years | **4%** |
| > 4 years | **0%** |

**PRIOR — residential purchased 11 Mar 2017 to 3 Jul 2025** (still examinable for "old purchase" scenarios):

| Holding period | SSD rate |
|---|---|
| ≤ 1 year | 12% |
| > 1 to 2 years | 8% |
| > 2 to 3 years | 4% |
| > 3 years | 0% |

**Industrial property (acquired on/after 12 Jan 2013):** 15% / 10% / 5% / 0% over **3 years**.

> 中文: SSD看"持有期"。2025年7月4日**及之后**买的住宅 → 新规:4年内卖才交税,16/12/8/4%。之前买的 → 旧规:3年,12/8/4%。工业房产是15/10/5%,3年。注意"购买日期"决定用哪套表。

## (c) Common Exam Traps / 易错点

1. **Higher of price OR value** — distractors will offer the answer computed on the *lower* figure. Always use the higher. (按较高者!)
2. **BSD is marginal/tiered; ABSD is flat.** A classic trap: applying ABSD's 20% to only a "portion." ABSD = single rate × **entire** price. (BSD分档,ABSD整套乘。)
3. **BSD is buyer-profile-independent.** A foreigner and an SC pay the *same BSD* on the same $1.2m flat — only ABSD differs. Don't let "foreigner" tempt you into changing BSD.
4. **Residential 6% vs non-residential 5% top rate** — and non-residential's 5% kicks in above **$1.5m**, residential's 6% above **$3m**. Easy to swap.
5. **ABSD applies to residential ONLY.** No ABSD on commercial/industrial. (商业/工业无ABSD。)
6. **SSD holding period: 4 years (new) vs 3 years (old)** — the cut-off is the **purchase date 4 Jul 2025**, NOT the sale date. A property *bought* before 4 Jul 2025 but *sold* in 2026 still uses the OLD 3-year/12-8-4% table.
7. **SSD is paid by the SELLER; BSD/ABSD by the BUYER.** Watch the question's perspective.
8. **Counting properties for ABSD** — partial interests and pending sales count. The "number owned" is at the time of the new purchase.
9. **FTA nationals counted as SC, not exempt outright** — they still pay SC rates (0%/20%/30%), NOT zero across the board.
10. **"EXCEPT / NOT" stems** — read carefully; e.g. "Which is NOT subject to ABSD?" → answer is the commercial/industrial option.
11. **Payment deadline 14 days (local) / 30 days (overseas)** — not the other way round.

## (d) Worked Examples

### Example 1 — BSD on a $1,200,000 residential property (marginal)
| Tier | Rate | Duty |
|---|---|---|
| First $180,000 | 1% | $1,800 |
| Next $180,000 | 2% | $3,600 |
| Next $640,000 | 3% | $19,200 |
| Next $200,000 (to reach $1.2m) | 4% | $8,000 |
| **Total BSD** | | **$32,600** |

Check via shortcut: (3% × $1,200,000) − $15,400 = $36,000 − $15,400 = **$32,600**. ✓

### Example 2 — Full duty: SPR buying a $1,200,000 2nd residential property
- BSD (same as Ex.1, profile-independent) = **$32,600**
- ABSD: SPR 2nd property = **30% flat** × $1,200,000 = **$360,000**
- **Total stamp duty on purchase = $32,600 + $360,000 = $392,600**
> 中文: BSD不变(看房子),ABSD按SPR第二套30%整套乘。

### Example 3 — Foreigner buying a $2,000,000 residential property (1st)
- BSD: 1%×180k + 2%×180k + 3%×640k + 4%×500k + 5%×500k
  = $1,800 + $3,600 + $19,200 + $20,000 + $25,000 = **$69,600**
- ABSD: Foreigner = **60% flat** × $2,000,000 = **$1,200,000**
- **Total = $1,269,600** (unless FTA national → then SC 1st-property rate 0% ABSD, total = $69,600)

### Example 4 — SSD: property bought 1 Aug 2025 (NEW rules), sold 10 months later for $1,500,000
- Held ≤ 1 year → **16%** × $1,500,000 = **$240,000** SSD (paid by seller).
> If it had been bought 1 Aug 2024 (OLD rules) and sold within 1 year: 12% × $1,500,000 = $180,000.

### Example 5 — BSD on non-residential (commercial) $2,000,000
- 1%×180k + 2%×180k + 3%×640k + 4%×500k + 5%×500k = same tiers but top tier caps at 5%
  = $1,800 + $3,600 + $19,200 + $20,000 + $25,000 = **$69,600**. No ABSD (non-residential). No 6% tier.

## 速记总结 / Quick Recall

- **三税一句话:** BSD看房子(买家付)· ABSD看身份+第几套(买家付,仅住宅)· SSD看持有期(卖家付)。
- **黄金法则:** 全部按 **价格 vs 市值 的较高者** 计算。
- **BSD = 累进/分档:** 1% / 2% / 3% / 4% / 5% / **6%**(住宅,>$3m);非住宅 **封顶5%**(>$1.5m)。
- **BSD shortcut ($1m–$1.5m residential):** 3% × price − $15,400.
- **ABSD = 整套乘一个税率 (flat):** SC **0 / 20 / 30**; SPR **5 / 30 / 35**; FR **60**; 公司/信托 **65**.
- **ABSD FTA "US + NILS"** (美/挪/冰/列/瑞) → 按SC算.
- **ABSD仅住宅;商业/工业无ABSD.**
- **SSD residential NEW (买于 ≥ 4 Jul 2025):** 4年 → **16 / 12 / 8 / 4 / 0%**.
- **SSD residential OLD (买于 11 Mar 2017–3 Jul 2025):** 3年 → **12 / 8 / 4 / 0%**.
- **SSD industrial:** 3年 → **15 / 10 / 5 / 0%**.
- **决定SSD用哪套表的是"购买日期",不是"卖出日期"。**
- **付款期限:** 本地签 **14天**,海外签 **30天**;SSD为卖出后14天。
- **关键陷阱:** BSD与买家身份无关;ABSD是flat不是分档;SSD是卖家付。

# P1.12 Property Tax, CPF for Property, Cooling-Measure Overview

## P1.12 — Property Tax, CPF for Property & Cooling-Measure Overview
### RES Paper 1 Revision Notes / 复习笔记 — Exam date: 18 July 2026
*All figures verified against IRAS, MOF, MAS, CPF Board primary sources (current as of June 2026). Currency rule: nothing implemented after ~18 June 2026 is examinable.*

---

## (a) Key Concepts / 核心概念

This topic sits at the intersection of three regulators. Know **who governs what** — examiners love mixing them up.

| Concept | Plain meaning | Governing body |
|---|---|---|
| **Property Tax (PT)** | A **wealth tax on property ownership**, charged annually whether or not the property earns income or is occupied. NOT income tax, NOT a transaction tax. | **IRAS** |
| **Annual Value (AV)** | The **estimated gross annual rent** the property could fetch if rented out (excluding furniture, furnishings, maintenance fees). PT = AV × tax rate. AV is determined by IRAS, not the owner. | **IRAS** |
| **CPF for property** | CPF Ordinary Account (OA) savings may fund the purchase price, stamp duties, and monthly loan instalments — subject to **Valuation Limit (VL)** and **Withdrawal Limit (WL)** caps. | **CPF Board** |
| **Cooling measures** | Government interventions to **curb speculation and keep housing affordable**: stamp duties (BSD, ABSD, SSD), loan limits (LTV, TDSR, MSR, stress test). | **MAS (loans) + IRAS (stamp duties) + MND/URA (policy)** |

> 中文说明：本节横跨三个监管机构。**Property Tax（房产税）由 IRAS 征收**，是按"年值 AV"计算的**财产持有税**（无论是否出租或自住每年都要交），不是所得税、也不是交易税。**CPF（公积金）用于买房**由 CPF Board 管，受 VL 和 WL 两个上限限制。**降温措施（cooling measures）**包括各种印花税（由 IRAS 管）和贷款限制（由 MAS 管）。考试最爱考"哪个机构管什么"，务必分清。

**The four cooling-measure levers (记忆框架):**
1. **Buy-side stamp duties** — BSD (everyone) + ABSD (depends on profile).
2. **Sell-side stamp duty** — SSD (penalises short-term flipping).
3. **Loan caps** — LTV (how much you can borrow), TDSR/MSR (relative to income).
4. **Stress test** — loans assessed at a higher "floor" interest rate.

---

## (b) Must-Know Rules & Exact Figures / 必背规则与精确数字

### B1. Property Tax — Owner-Occupier Residential (eff. 1 Jan 2025, applies in 2026)

Lower, progressive rates — the concession for living in your own home.

| AV portion | Rate |
|---|---|
| First $12,000 | **0%** |
| > $12,000 – $40,000 | 4% |
| > $40,000 – $50,000 | 6% |
| > $50,000 – $75,000 | 10% |
| > $75,000 – $85,000 | 14% |
| > $85,000 – $100,000 | 20% |
| > $100,000 – $140,000 | 26% |
| Above $140,000 | **32%** |

> 中文：自住住宅（owner-occupier）享受**累进优惠税率**，首 $12,000 年值免税，最高 32%。"自住"才有这个优惠。

### B2. Property Tax — Non-Owner-Occupier Residential (eff. 1 Jan 2024)

Higher rates — applies to **rented-out / vacant residential** (e.g. investment condo, second home not lived in).

| AV portion | Rate |
|---|---|
| First $30,000 | **12%** |
| > $30,000 – $45,000 | 20% |
| > $45,000 – $60,000 | 28% |
| Above $60,000 | **36%** |

> 中文：非自住住宅（出租或空置）税率高得多，从 12% 起跳，最高 36%，**没有免税区间**。

### B3. Property Tax — Non-Residential / Land

| Property type | Rate |
|---|---|
| Commercial, industrial, vacant land | **Flat 10% of AV** (no progressive tiers) |

> 中文：商业、工业、空地 = **统一 10%**，没有累进。

### B4. 2026 Property Tax Rebate (one-off, MOF/IRAS — VERIFIED)

| Owner-occupied property | Rebate |
|---|---|
| **HDB flat** | **15%** of PT payable |
| **Private residential** | **10%**, **capped at $500** |

- Automatically offset against the 2026 PT bill (issued from Dec 2025; due **31 Jan 2026**).
- 1- and 2-room HDB owner-occupiers continue to pay **no PT**.

> 中文：2026 一次性回扣——自住 **HDB 15%**，自住**私宅 10%（封顶 $500）**。自动抵扣，账单 1 月 31 日到期。

### B5. CPF for Property — Valuation Limit (VL) & Withdrawal Limit (WL) (VERIFIED)

| Term | Rule |
|---|---|
| **Valuation Limit (VL)** | **The LOWER of purchase price OR market value at time of purchase.** CPF OA usable up to **100% of VL** with no extra conditions. |
| **Withdrawal Limit (WL)** | **120% of VL.** The absolute cap on CPF usage — **PRIVATE PROPERTY ONLY.** |
| **To use CPF between 100%–120% of VL** | Each CPF member using funds must have set aside the prevailing **Basic Retirement Sum (BRS)** in their RA (for members 55+) / OA+SA. |
| **HDB flats** | **NO Withdrawal Limit** — CPF can fund beyond VL with no 120% cap (BRS condition still applies where relevant). HDB flat bought with HDB concessionary loan: **neither VL nor WL applies**. |
| **Remaining lease rule** | Full CPF if remaining lease ≥ 20 yrs **AND** covers the youngest buyer to age 95; **pro-rated** otherwise; **NO CPF if remaining lease < 20 yrs**. |

**2026 Retirement Sums (for those turning 55 in 2026):** BRS **$110,200** · FRS **$220,400** · ERS **$440,800**.
*(2025 cohort: BRS $106,500 / FRS $213,000 / ERS $426,000 — use whichever cohort the question names.)*

> 中文：**VL = 买价与市值取低者**，CPF 用到 100% VL 无条件。**WL = 120% VL，仅适用于私宅**——超过 100% VL 想用到 120% 之间，必须先存够 **BRS（基本存款额）**。**HDB 组屋没有 WL（120% 上限）**，可超过 VL 继续用 CPF。剩余地契 <20 年不能用 CPF。2026 年满 55 岁者：BRS $110,200。

### B6. Buyer's Stamp Duty (BSD) — everyone pays (eff. 15 Feb 2023)

Charged on the **HIGHER of purchase price or market value**. **Same for all buyers regardless of citizenship.**

| Portion of price/value | Residential | Non-residential |
|---|---|---|
| First $180,000 | 1% | 1% |
| Next $180,000 | 2% | 2% |
| Next $640,000 (→ $1m) | 3% | 3% |
| Next $500,000 (→ $1.5m) | 4% | 4% |
| Next $1,500,000 (→ $3m) | 5% | **5% (everything above $1.5m)** |
| Above $3,000,000 | **6%** | — |

> 中文：BSD **所有买家都交，与国籍无关**，按"买价或市值取高者"计算。住宅最高 6%（超 $3m），非住宅最高 5%（超 $1.5m）。

### B7. Additional Buyer's Stamp Duty (ABSD) — residential only (eff. 27 Apr 2023, unchanged 2026 — VERIFIED)

On top of BSD. Charged on the **HIGHER of price or market value**. **Depends on buyer profile + number of residential properties owned.**

| Buyer profile | 1st property | 2nd | 3rd+ |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Singapore Citizen (SC)** | **0%** | 20% | 30% |
| **Singapore PR (SPR)** | 5% | 30% | 35% |
| **Foreigner (FR)** | **60%** | 60% | 60% |
| **Entity (company)** | **65%** | 65% | 65% |
| **Trustee** (living trust) | **65%** | 65% | 65% |

- **FTA exemption** (treated as SC = 0%/20%/30%): **U.S. citizens**; citizens **and** PRs of **Iceland, Liechtenstein, Norway, Switzerland**. (Mnemonic: **"US + ILNS"** / 美国 + 冰列挪瑞.)
- **Married-couple remission:** refund of ABSD paid on a 2nd property if the 1st is sold within the timeline, provided at least one spouse is an SC.

> 中文：ABSD **只针对住宅**，看**买家身份 + 已拥有住宅数量**。新加坡公民第一套 **0%**，外国人**任何一套都 60%**，公司/信托 **65%**。**FTA 豁免**：美国公民、以及冰岛/列支敦士登/挪威/瑞士的公民和 PR，按公民税率算。

### B8. Seller's Stamp Duty (SSD) — residential, deters flipping (VERIFIED)

On the **HIGHER of price or market value**. **CURRENT schedule** = properties purchased (OTP exercised) **ON/AFTER 4 Jul 2025**: holding period extended to **4 years**, rates +4 ppt per tier.

| Holding period | SSD rate (current, on/after 4 Jul 2025) | Prior (11 Mar 2017 – 3 Jul 2025) |
|---|---|---|
| ≤ 1 year | **16%** | 12% |
| > 1 – 2 years | **12%** | 8% |
| > 2 – 3 years | **8%** | 4% |
| > 3 – 4 years | **4%** | 0% |
| > 4 years | **0%** | 0% |

- **Industrial property** (acquired on/after 12 Jan 2013): **15% / 10% / 5% / 0%** over 3 years.
- Holding period starts from **date of acceptance of OTP**. SSD payable within **14 days** of sale.
- **No SSD on commercial property.**

> 中文：SSD 打击短期炒卖。**现行规则（2025年7月4日起买入）持有期延长到 4 年**，税率每档 +4%，1 年内卖出 **16%**，超过 4 年免征。从 OTP 接受日起算。商业地产无 SSD。

### B9. Loan & Affordability Limits (MAS — cooling measures)

| Metric | Value | Applies to |
|---|---|---|
| **LTV — 1st housing loan** | **75%** (max) | All (conditions: tenure ≤30yr & not beyond age 65) |
| **LTV — 2nd loan** | 45% | — |
| **LTV — 3rd+ loan** | 35% | — |
| **HDB concessionary loan LTV** | **75%** (cut from 80% on 20 Aug 2024) | HDB |
| **TDSR** | **55%** of gross monthly income | ALL property loans |
| **MSR** | **30%** of gross monthly income | **HDB flats & EC bought from developer ONLY** |
| **Stress-test floor (residential)** | **4.0% p.a.** (or actual, higher) | TDSR/MSR computation |
| **Stress-test floor (non-residential)** | **5.0% p.a.** | TDSR |

> 中文：**LTV** = 最高可贷比例（首套 75%）。**TDSR 55%** 适用于**所有**房贷（总负债占月收入比）。**MSR 30%** **只**适用于组屋和向发展商买的 EC。压力测试利率底线住宅 **4%**。

## (c) Common Exam Traps / 易错点

1. **Owner-occupier vs non-owner-occupier PT rates** — examiners give a rented-out condo and offer the (lower) owner-occupier rate as a distractor. Rented/vacant = **non-owner-occupier (12%+)**. 自住 vs 出租税率别选错。
2. **Property Tax is on AV (annual rental value), NOT on purchase price or market value.** A high-value but low-rent property still uses AV. 房产税按**年值 AV**，不是买价或市值。
3. **BSD vs ABSD** — BSD is **profile-independent (same for everyone)**; only **ABSD** depends on citizenship + property count. Don't say BSD differs by nationality. BSD 与国籍无关，只有 ABSD 才看身份。
4. **BSD / ABSD / SSD use the HIGHER of price or market value; Valuation Limit (VL) uses the LOWER.** Easy to flip. 印花税取**高者**，VL 取**低者**。
5. **WL (120%) applies to PRIVATE property only — HDB has NO Withdrawal Limit.** Classic trap. 120% 上限只管私宅，组屋没有 WL。
6. **MSR (30%) applies ONLY to HDB & developer-bought EC — never to private resale or resale EC.** MSR 只管组屋和发展商 EC。
7. **ABSD FTA list** — only **US + Iceland/Liechtenstein/Norway/Switzerland**. Australia, China, etc. are NOT exempt. 别把澳洲、中国当作豁免国。
8. **SSD current holding period is 4 years (16/12/8/4%)** for purchases on/after 4 Jul 2025 — don't pick the old 3-year/12% schedule unless the question dates the purchase before 4 Jul 2025. 现行 SSD 是 4 年期。
9. **2026 private PT rebate is capped at $500; HDB rebate (15%) is NOT capped.** 私宅回扣封顶 $500，组屋不封顶。
10. **No CPF if remaining lease < 20 years**, and full CPF needs the lease to cover the youngest buyer to **age 95**. 剩余地契 <20 年不能用 CPF。
11. **Commercial/industrial property: PT is flat 10%; no SSD; GST 9% applies** (residential is GST-exempt). 商业地产房产税统一 10%，无 SSD，但有 9% GST。
12. **"EXCEPT / NOT / ONLY" inversions** — read the stem twice; cooling-measure questions are full of them.

## (d) Worked Examples / 计算例题

### Example 1 — Owner-Occupier Property Tax (before rebate)
Condo with **AV = $60,000**, owner-occupied.

| Tier | Calculation | Tax |
|---|---|---|
| First $12,000 @ 0% | — | $0 |
| Next $28,000 @ 4% ($12k–$40k) | 0.04 × 28,000 | $1,120 |
| Next $10,000 @ 6% ($40k–$50k) | 0.06 × 10,000 | $600 |
| Next $10,000 @ 10% ($50k–$60k) | 0.10 × 10,000 | $1,000 |
| **Gross PT** | | **$2,720** |

**With 2026 private rebate (10%, capped $500):** 10% × $2,720 = $272 (under cap) → **net PT = $2,720 − $272 = $2,448.**

### Example 2 — Same AV, but RENTED OUT (non-owner-occupier)
**AV = $60,000**, not lived in.

| Tier | Calculation | Tax |
|---|---|---|
| First $30,000 @ 12% | 0.12 × 30,000 | $3,600 |
| Next $15,000 @ 20% ($30k–$45k) | 0.20 × 15,000 | $3,000 |
| Next $15,000 @ 28% ($45k–$60k) | 0.28 × 15,000 | $4,200 |
| **PT** | | **$10,800** |

*Same property — occupancy status quadruples the tax ($2,720 vs $10,800). No rebate (rebate is owner-occupier only).* This contrast is a favourite MCQ.

### Example 3 — BSD on a residential purchase ($1,200,000)
1% × 180,000 = $1,800
+ 2% × 180,000 = $3,600
+ 3% × 640,000 = $19,200
+ 4% × 200,000 = $8,000 (the $1m–$1.2m portion)
**BSD = $32,600.**

### Example 4 — ABSD: Singapore PR buying 2nd residential property at $1,200,000
ABSD = **30%** (SPR, 2nd property) × $1,200,000 = **$360,000.**
Total stamp duty = BSD $32,600 + ABSD $360,000 = **$392,600.**

### Example 5 — SSD (current schedule): sold 18 months after buying (bought Aug 2025)
Holding > 1–2 years → **12%**. Sale price $1,500,000 (= market value).
SSD = 0.12 × $1,500,000 = **$180,000.**

### Example 6 — CPF Withdrawal Limit (private property)
Purchase price $1,300,000; market value $1,250,000.
**VL = lower = $1,250,000.** **WL = 120% × $1,250,000 = $1,500,000** (private property cap).
Buyer may use CPF up to $1,250,000 freely; to use between $1,250,000 and $1,500,000, must have set aside the prevailing BRS.
*(If this were an HDB flat: no $1.5m WL cap.)*

## 速记总结 / Quick Recall

- **Property Tax = AV × rate** (AV = annual rental value, set by IRAS; NOT purchase price). 房产税按年值。
- **Owner-occupier residential:** 0% first $12k → 32% top. **Non-owner-occupier:** 12% → 36% top, no free band. **Non-residential: flat 10%.**
- **2026 PT rebate:** HDB **15%** (uncapped); private **10% capped $500**; owner-occupiers only.
- **BSD:** everyone, profile-independent, higher of price/value; res. top **6%** (>$3m), non-res top **5%** (>$1.5m).
- **ABSD:** residential only, by profile; SC 1st **0%**, FR **60%**, entity/trustee **65%**; FTA exempt = **US + Iceland/Liechtenstein/Norway/Switzerland**.
- **SSD (current, bought on/after 4 Jul 2025):** **4-year** holding, **16/12/8/4/0%**; from OTP date; pay within 14 days; none on commercial.
- **CPF VL = lower of price/value**; **WL = 120% of VL — PRIVATE ONLY**; **HDB has no WL**; >100% VL needs **BRS** set aside; no CPF if lease <20 yrs.
- **2026 retirement sums (turn 55 in 2026):** BRS **$110,200** / FRS **$220,400** / ERS **$440,800**.
- **Loan levers:** LTV 75% (1st); **TDSR 55%** (all loans); **MSR 30%** (HDB & developer-EC only); stress floor **4%** residential / **5%** non-residential.
- **Mantra:** *Stamp duties = HIGHER of price/value; CPF VL = LOWER.* 印花税取高，VL 取低。
- **Mantra:** *BSD same for all; ABSD by profile; SSD penalises quick sale.*
