# P2.1 Estate Agency Agreements (Exclusive/Open), Commission, Co-broking

## P2.1 Estate Agency Agreements (Exclusive/Open), Commission & Co-broking — Revision Notes
**RES Paper 2 (CU3–CU4) | Exam: 18 July 2026 | Figures verified against CEA, SEAA, IRAS primary sources (current as of June 2026)**

> Currency rule: You will NOT be tested on policy changes announced/implemented within one month before the exam (after ~18 June 2026). Use the figures below.

---

## (a) Key Concepts / 核心概念

### What is an Estate Agency Agreement (EAA)?
A written contract between a client (the consumer) and an Estate Agent (EA, the firm), executed by the Registered Estate Salesperson (RES), that authorises the agent to perform estate agency work for a defined transaction and sets out the commission, validity period, and obligations of both parties.

- **It is a regulatory requirement, not optional.** Under the CEPCC (Code of Ethics & Professional Client Care, prescribed under the Estate Agents (Estate Agency Work) Regulations 2010), the RES **MUST** use the appropriate CEA **Prescribed Estate Agency Agreement** for the sale, purchase, or lease of **residential** property before commencing work.
- The agreement is between the **client and the EA (firm)** — the RES signs *on behalf of* the EA. Commission legally accrues to the EA, who then pays the RES.

中文: 房地产中介协议是客户与中介公司（EA）之间的书面合同，由注册销售员（RES）代表公司签署。CEA 规定，住宅交易必须使用官方的 8 份标准协议表格之一，这是强制性的，不是可选的。

### The 8 Prescribed Estate Agency Agreements (residential only)
There are **8** prescribed forms — **4 non-exclusive (open)** + **4 exclusive** — covering sell / buy / lease-landlord / lease-tenant.

| Form | Type | Side / Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **1** | Non-exclusive | Selling residential |
| **2** | Non-exclusive | Buying residential |
| **3** | Non-exclusive | Leasing — Landlord |
| **4** | Non-exclusive | Leasing — Tenant |
| **5** | Exclusive | Selling residential |
| **6** | Exclusive | Buying residential |
| **7** | Exclusive | Leasing — Landlord |
| **8** | Exclusive | Leasing — Tenant |

> Memory hook: **1–4 = Non-exclusive (open)**, **5–8 = Exclusive**. Within each set the order is always **Sell → Buy → Lease(Landlord) → Lease(Tenant)**. Odd-looking but reliable: Form **5** = Exclusive **Sell**, Form **7** = Exclusive **Lease-Landlord**.

中文: 共 8 份表格。表 1–4 是非独家（开放式），表 5–8 是独家。每组顺序固定：卖、买、租（房东）、租（租客）。

### Exclusive vs Non-Exclusive (Open) — the core distinction

- **Exclusive agreement (独家):** The client appoints **ONE EA only** as sole representative for the validity period. The client is liable for commission **even if the client finds a buyer/tenant himself, or another agent closes the deal**, during the validity period. Used when the seller wants dedicated marketing effort.
- **Non-exclusive / Open agreement (非独家/开放式):** The client may appoint **multiple EAs** simultaneously and may also sell on their own. The client pays **only the agent who actually closes the transaction** (the "effective cause" / procuring agent). No commission to others.

中文要点: 独家协议下，无论谁成交（包括客户自己卖出），客户都要付佣金给那唯一的中介；开放式协议下，只付给真正促成交易的那个中介。

### Co-broking (联合代理)
When **two (or more) RES, representing DIFFERENT parties** in the same transaction (e.g. seller's agent + buyer's agent), cooperate to close the deal. The two agents agree to **share or split commission**. Co-broking is normal and lawful — it is **NOT** dual representation.

中文: 联合代理 = 代表不同方的两个中介合作成交并分佣。这是合法且常见的，与"双重代理"完全不同。

### Dual Representation (双重代理) — distinguish from co-broking
**ONE RES collecting/sharing commission from BOTH sides** of the same transaction. This is a **prohibited offence** under CEA rules (conflict of interest). Do not confuse with co-broking (two *different* agents).

## (b) Must-Know Rules & Exact Figures

### Exclusive vs Non-Exclusive — comparison table

| Feature | Exclusive (Forms 5–8) | Non-Exclusive / Open (Forms 1–4) |
|---|---|---|
| Number of agents | **One EA only** | Multiple EAs allowed |
| Client can transact on own? | Yes, but **commission still payable** | Yes, **no commission** if no agent involved |
| Who gets commission | The appointed exclusive EA, regardless of who closes (during validity) | Only the EA who is the **effective cause** of the deal |
| **Max validity period** | **Cannot exceed 3 months** (no auto-renewal) | No prescribed max; runs until withdrawn/closed |
| Post-expiry commission "tail" | **Yes** — see rule below | Generally none |
| Best for | Seller wanting focused effort & accountability | Seller wanting wide exposure |

中文小结: 独家最长 3 个月，不可自动续约；开放式无最长期限限制。独家有"到期后保护期"，开放式通常没有。

### KEY RULE — Exclusive agreement maximum validity
- **An exclusive estate agency agreement CANNOT be valid for more than 3 months.** No automatic renewal — a new agreement must be signed to continue.
- 中文: 独家协议有效期不得超过 3 个月，到期不会自动续约。

### KEY RULE — Post-expiry commission "tail" (introduced-buyer protection)
- If the property is sold/leased **within 3 calendar months AFTER the exclusive agreement expires**, to a party whom the **agent introduced (made the property known to) during the validity period**, the **commission is STILL payable** to the exclusive agent.
- This protects the agent from a client who deliberately waits for the agreement to lapse before closing with an agent-introduced buyer.
- 中文: 协议到期后 3 个月内，若卖给了在有效期内由该中介介绍/告知的买家，客户仍须付佣金。这是防止客户故意拖到协议失效再成交以逃避佣金。

### KEY RULE — Commission is fully negotiable
- **CEA does NOT fix, regulate, or prescribe any commission rate or amount.** There are **no official/standard rates**. Commission must be **discussed and agreed in the EAA before the agent starts work**, and documented in the agreement.
- Common **market norms** (NOT rules — never state these as fixed):
  - Residential sale: ~**2%** from the seller (buyer's side may pay ~1%).
  - Residential rental: ~**1 month's rent** from landlord for a lease of **≥1 year** (often ½ month or none for shorter leases).
- 中文: 佣金完全可协商，CEA 不规定任何固定费率。市场惯例（非规定）：买卖约 2%，租赁约 1 个月租金（≥1 年租约）。考试中"固定/法定费率"一律是错的。

### KEY RULE — Dual representation is prohibited
- A single RES **must not** act for and **collect commission from both** the buyer and seller (or landlord and tenant) in the same transaction. This is a **conflict-of-interest offence** (real DC case: $9,000 fine).
- **Two RES from the SAME EA** on opposite sides is **allowed and is NOT dual representation**, PROVIDED: (i) **neither** collects from both sides, AND (ii) both **declare in writing** they are from the same EA AND obtain the clients' **written consent**.
- 中文: 同一个 RES 不能同时代表买卖双方并向双方收佣（违法）。但同一家公司的两个不同 RES 分别代表买卖双方是允许的——前提是各自只向自己的一方收佣，且书面披露同属一家公司并取得双方书面同意。

### Co-broking rules & SEAA Best Practice Guide

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| What it is | Two RES of **different parties** cooperate; commission **shared/split** between the two agents |
| Common splits (norms only) | Sale: ~**1% + 1%** (each agent from own side, or split a pooled fee); Rental: often **½ month each** |
| Legal status of the split | **Strictly a private agreement between the agents** — CEA does not regulate the split |
| **SEAA Best Practice Guide for Co-Broking Commission** | Effective **1 July 2024**. Core principle: **each agent collects commission from the party they represent** (buyer's agent ← buyer; seller's agent ← seller) to reduce disputes and conflicts of interest |
| Legal force of the SEAA Guide | **It is a BEST-PRACTICE GUIDE, NOT a regulation.** **No penalty** for not following it. (~90% of RES covered as of Feb 2025) |

中文: 联合代理的分佣是中介之间的私下协议，CEA 不管。SEAA《联合代理佣金最佳实践指南》2024 年 7 月 1 日生效，主张"各收各方佣金"，但它只是行业指南、不是法规，不遵守不会被罚。

### Advertising / agreement-related conduct (frequently bundled into P2.1 questions)
- Owner's/client's **prior consent** required before advertising or marketing the property.
- Every advertisement must show **RES name, CEA Registration No., contact number** (and the EA's Licence No.).
- Advertising/conduct breach penalty: **up to $75,000**, suspension, or revocation.
- 中文: 登广告前须取得业主同意；广告须列明 RES 姓名、CEA 注册号、联系电话及公司执照号。违规最高罚 7.5 万元。

## (c) Common Exam Traps / 易错点

1. **Co-broking ≠ Dual representation.** Co-broking = **two different** agents (different parties) sharing commission = LEGAL. Dual representation = **one** agent collecting from **both** sides = OFFENCE. Distractors swap these.
   - 中文: 联合代理（两个不同中介）合法；双重代理（一个中介收两边的钱）违法。最常见的陷阱。

2. **Exclusive max validity = 3 months, NOT 6 / NOT 12 / NOT "negotiable to any length."** And there is **no auto-renewal**.
   - 中文: 独家最长 3 个月，不能更久，不能自动续约。

3. **Post-expiry tail is 3 months, and ONLY for agent-introduced buyers.** Trap answers say "no commission after expiry" (wrong) or "tail applies to any buyer" (wrong — only introduced buyers).
   - 中文: 到期后保护期 3 个月，且仅限"中介介绍过的买家"，不是任何买家。

4. **Commission has NO fixed/legal/CEA-prescribed rate.** Any option stating "2% is the CEA-regulated rate" or "standard statutory commission" is WRONG. 2% is only a market norm.
   - 中文: 佣金没有法定费率，2% 只是惯例。

5. **SEAA Co-Broking Guide is NOT law.** Trap: "RES will be fined/disciplined for breaching the SEAA guide." Wrong — no penalty; it is voluntary best practice.
   - 中文: SEAA 指南不是法规，不遵守不会被罚。

6. **Exclusive ≠ no co-broking.** An exclusive agreement appoints one EA *to represent the client*, but that exclusive agent can still **co-broke with the other side's agent**. Exclusivity is about *your side's* representation, not about banning cooperation.
   - 中文: 独家协议下仍可与对方的中介联合代理；独家针对的是"代表你这一方"的唯一性。

7. **Under an exclusive agreement, the client pays even if HE/SHE finds the buyer.** Trap rewards "client sold it himself, so no commission" — wrong for exclusive (right only for open/non-exclusive).
   - 中文: 独家下客户自己卖出也要付佣金；只有开放式下自己卖出才不付。

8. **Prescribed agreements are for RESIDENTIAL property.** The 8 forms cover residential sale/purchase/lease. Commercial/industrial are not covered by the mandatory prescribed-form requirement.
   - 中文: 8 份标准表格只针对住宅。

9. **"Same EA, opposite sides" needs BOTH written disclosure AND written consent** — and neither agent may collect from both sides. Missing either condition makes it non-compliant.
   - 中文: 同公司分代买卖双方，必须同时满足"书面披露 + 书面同意 + 各收各方"。

10. **Two-week vs three-month tail.** The post-expiry protection is **3 months**, not 2 weeks. (Common distractor number.)

## (d) Worked Examples

### Worked Example 1 — Exclusive agreement, client self-sells during validity
RES signs **Form 5 (Exclusive Sell)** with seller Mr Tan, agreed commission **2%**, validity 1 Apr–30 Jun 2026 (3 months). On 20 May, Mr Tan's own friend buys the flat for **$600,000** without the agent's involvement.

- Exclusive rule: commission payable **regardless of who closes**, during validity.
- Commission = 2% × $600,000 = **$12,000** payable to the EA.
- 中文: 独家有效期内，即使客户自己卖出，也要付 2% = $12,000。

### Worked Example 2 — Post-expiry "tail"
Same exclusive agreement expires **30 Jun 2026**. During the validity period, the agent had shown the unit to Buyer X. On **10 Aug 2026** (41 days after expiry) Buyer X buys it for **$600,000**.

- Sale is within **3 months after expiry** AND to a buyer **introduced during validity** → commission **still payable**.
- Commission = 2% × $600,000 = **$12,000**.
- Contrast: if a brand-new buyer (never introduced by the agent) buys on 10 Aug → **no commission** (not an introduced buyer).
- 中文: 到期后 3 个月内卖给被介绍过的买家 X → 仍付 $12,000；若是全新买家则不付。

### Worked Example 3 — Non-exclusive (open) agreement
Seller signs **Form 1 (Non-exclusive Sell)** with Agent A and Agent B, 2% each "if you close." Agent B finds the buyer and completes the sale at **$800,000**.

- Open rule: only the **effective cause** (Agent B) is paid. Agent A gets **nothing**.
- Commission = 2% × $800,000 = **$16,000** to Agent B's EA only.
- 中文: 开放式下只付给真正成交的 B（$16,000），A 拿不到。

### Worked Example 4 — Co-broking split
A sale at **$1,000,000** is co-broked: seller's agent and buyer's agent each charge **1%** of their own side.
- Seller's agent: 1% × $1,000,000 = **$10,000** (from seller).
- Buyer's agent: 1% × $1,000,000 = **$10,000** (from buyer).
- This "each collects from own client" model aligns with the **SEAA Best Practice Guide** (effective 1 Jul 2024). Each agent then pays its RES per the EA's internal scheme.
- 中文: 联合代理，各收各方 1% = 各 $10,000，符合 SEAA 指南。

### Worked Example 5 — Rental commission
Landlord signs **Form 7 (Exclusive Lease-Landlord)**, 2-year lease, monthly rent **$3,500**, commission norm = 1 month's rent (≥1-year lease).
- Commission = 1 × $3,500 = **$3,500** (negotiable; this is a norm, not a rule).
- 中文: ≥1 年租约惯例收 1 个月租金 = $3,500（可协商）。

## 速记总结 / Quick Recall

- **8 prescribed forms** (residential only): **1–4 = Non-exclusive (open)**, **5–8 = Exclusive**; order in each set = Sell, Buy, Lease-Landlord, Lease-Tenant.
- **Exclusive = ONE EA**; commission payable **even if client self-sells or another closes** during validity. **Max 3 months, no auto-renew.**
- **Post-expiry tail = 3 months**, but **only for agent-INTRODUCED buyers.**
- **Non-exclusive (open) = many EAs**; pay only the **effective-cause** agent; self-sale = no commission.
- **Commission = fully negotiable**; **CEA fixes NO rate.** Norms only: sale ~2%, rental ~1 month rent (≥1yr).
- **Must be agreed & documented in the EAA BEFORE the agent starts work.**
- **Co-broking = 2 DIFFERENT agents, different parties, share commission = LEGAL.**
- **Dual representation = 1 agent collecting from BOTH sides = OFFENCE.**
- **Same EA, opposite sides = OK** IF written disclosure + written consent + neither collects from both.
- **SEAA Co-Broking Guide** (eff. **1 Jul 2024**): "each agent collects from the party it represents" — **best practice, NOT law, no penalty.**
- Exclusivity does **NOT** prevent co-broking with the other side.
- Advertising needs **owner's prior consent**; ad must show **RES name + CEA Reg No. + contact** (+ EA Licence No.); breach up to **$75,000**.

中文一句话记忆: 独家最多 3 个月 + 到期后 3 个月保护介绍买家；佣金可协商无法定费率；联合代理（两个中介合法）≠ 双重代理（一个中介两头收钱违法）；SEAA 指南只是指南不是法规。

## (a) Key Concepts / 核心概念

This topic splits into **two regulatory pillars** that the exam loves to test together:

1. **Marketing & Advertising Rules** — governed by the **CEPCC** (Code of Ethics & Professional Client Care) and CEA's **Practice Guidelines on advertising**, made under the Estate Agents Act 2010 (EAA) and the Estate Agents (Estate Agency Work) Regulations 2010. Aim: protect consumers from **false, misleading or unsubstantiated** marketing.

2. **AML/CFT/CPF Obligations** — Anti-Money Laundering / Countering the Financing of Terrorism / Counter-Proliferation-Financing. Governed by **Part 4A of the EAA**, the CEA AML/CFT Practice Guidelines, and substantially strengthened by the **Anti-Money Laundering and Other Matters (Estate Agents and Developers) Act 2025** (passed 8 Apr 2025, **operational 1 July 2025**).

Foundational vocabulary you MUST be able to define:

| Term | Meaning (English) | 中文 |
|---|---|---|
| **CDD** | Customer Due Diligence — identify & verify your client before any agreement | 客户尽职调查 |
| **UCPDD** | Unrepresented Counterparty Due Diligence — due diligence on the *other side* when they have NO agent (NEW from the 2025 Act) | 无代表对手方尽职调查 |
| **EDD** | Enhanced Due Diligence — extra checks for higher-risk clients | 强化尽职调查 |
| **PEP** | Politically Exposed Person — higher ML risk → triggers EDD | 政治公众人物 |
| **STR** | Suspicious Transaction Report — filed to STRO via the SONAR portal | 可疑交易报告 |
| **ML / TF / PF** | Money Laundering / Terrorism Financing / Proliferation Financing | 洗钱 / 资恐 / 扩散融资 |
| **RBA** | Risk-Based Approach — tailor effort to assessed risk level | 风险为本方法 |
| **Beneficial owner** | The natural person who ultimately owns/controls the client or transaction | 受益所有人 |

> 中文要点：本主题考两大块——**广告规则**（防止虚假/误导宣传）和 **AML/CFT 反洗钱义务**（2025 年新法 2025 年 7 月 1 日生效，大幅加强）。最重要的新概念是 **UCPDD（对无代表对手方的尽职调查）**。

## (b) Must-Know Rules & Exact Figures / 必背规则与数字

### B1. Advertising — mandatory disclosures on EVERY advertisement
Applies to all media: print, online, mobile, social media, flyers, bus/property-portal ads.

| Must appear in every ad | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Salesperson's name** | As registered with CEA |
| **CEA Registration No.** | Format: letter + 6 digits + letter, e.g. **R123456A** |
| **EA (agency) Licence No.** | The licensed estate agent firm's number, e.g. L3000000A |
| **Contact number** | Salesperson's contact |

**Other advertising rules:**
- Material info must be **accurate and verified** (e.g. lease term/tenure, floor area, asking price).
- **No false, misleading, exaggerated, or discriminatory** content (no discrimination by race/religion etc.).
- **Owner's prior consent is required** before advertising any property.
- Claims of **expertise / specialisation / success rate** must be **substantiated by verifiable records**.
- Do not advertise a property you are not authorised to market; no duplicate/spam listings.

**Penalty for advertising / general conduct breaches: up to $75,000**, plus suspension or revocation of registration. (Unlicensed estate agency work: fine up to **$75,000** and/or imprisonment up to **3 years**.)

> 中文要点：每则广告都必须写明 **代理姓名、CEA 注册号、经纪公司执照号、联系电话**。内容必须真实、经核实，登广告前要先获得 **业主同意**。违规罚款最高 **$75,000**，可被停牌或吊销。

### B2. AML/CFT — core duties of a salesperson (Part 4A EAA)

| Duty | What you must do | When |
|---|---|---|
| **CDD** | Obtain & document client info; verify identity via **reliable, independent sources**; identify beneficial owners; get written acknowledgement | **Before** entering into any agreement |
| **UCPDD** | Conduct due diligence on the **unrepresented counterparty** before your client signs | Before client signs the acquisition/disposition agreement |
| **Sanctions screening** | Screen against **UNSC / UN Act** designated/sanctions lists | At CDD and on ongoing basis |
| **PEP check** | Determine if client is a Politically Exposed Person | At CDD |
| **EDD** | Apply enhanced measures for higher-risk clients/transactions | When risk is elevated |
| **Ongoing monitoring** | Monitor for unusual/inconsistent transactions; keep client info current | Throughout the relationship |
| **STR** | File a Suspicious Transaction Report to **STRO via SONAR** | When suspicion arises (legal obligation) |
| **Record-keeping** | Retain CDD/transaction records | **≥ 5 years** |
| **Training** | EA must run an **ongoing AML/CFT training programme** for its RESs | Continuous |

**UCPDD scope (NEW from 1 Jul 2025) — high-value exam point:**
- Required for **all rental, sale and purchase property transactions** where the counterparty has **NO** EA/RES representing them.
- **EXCEPTION:** **HDB residential rental** transactions are excluded from UCPDD.

> 中文要点：核心义务 = **CDD（事前查自己客户）、UCPDD（查无代理的对手方）、制裁名单筛查、PEP 检查、EDD、持续监控、STR 报告、记录保存满 5 年**。UCPDD 是 2025 新法新增，适用所有买卖与租赁，**唯一例外是 HDB 住宅租赁**。

### B3. AML/CFT — Penalty Framework (verify the "per breach" basis!)

| Mechanism | Other breaches | **AML/PF/TF breaches (on/after 1 Jul 2025)** |
|---|---|---|
| **Letter of Censure (LOC)** — less serious, non-criminal | up to **$5,000 per case** | up to **$5,000 per breach** |
| **Disciplinary Committee (DC) — RES** | up to **$100,000 per case** | up to **$100,000 per breach** |
| **Disciplinary Committee (DC) — EA (firm)** | up to **$200,000 per case** | up to **$200,000 per breach** |

**The critical 2025 change:** for AML/PF/TF breaches, CEA may impose penalties on a **"per contravention / per breach"** basis instead of **"per case."** Ten lapses = ten penalties, not one. This is the single most examinable AML penalty fact.

- DC = **≥3 members** drawn from the CEA Disciplinary Panel. Powers: financial penalty, suspend/revoke registration, vary conditions, reprimand.
- DC process: Statement of Case → Admission/Defence (**14 days**) → Reply (14d) → Rejoinder (14d) → hearing → sentencing. **Appeal to the MND Appeals Board within 14 days.**
- Real case: two RESs fined for CDD lapses in the **2023 S$3 billion money-laundering case** — illustrates that failing CDD/UCPDD is enforced in practice.

> 中文要点：AML 违规罚款采 **"按每项违规"（per breach）** 计算——这是 2025 新法最重要的改变。LOC 每项最高 **$5,000**；纪律委员会对 **RES 每项最高 $100,000**，对 **公司每项最高 $200,000**。申诉须在 **14 天** 内向 MND 申诉委员会提出。

| Item | Period |

| **AML/CDD records** | **5 years** |
| **EA document retention (COPEA Para 7(1))** | **5 years** (raised from 3 to align with AML) |
| DC defence / reply / rejoinder / appeal windows | **14 days** each |

## (c) Common Exam Traps / 易错点

1. **75% is course ATTENDANCE, not the pass mark.** Pass mark = **60/100 (60%)**. 易混：75% 是出勤率。
2. **AML penalties are now "per breach," not "per case"** (since 1 Jul 2025). Distractors will offer "per case." 罚款按每项违规计。
3. **UCPDD applies to almost everything — but HDB residential rental is the EXCEPTION.** Watch "EXCEPT/NOT" stems. UCPDD 唯一例外 = HDB 住宅租赁。
4. **CDD vs UCPDD** — CDD = your *own* client; UCPDD = the *unrepresented other side*. Don't swap them. 别弄反。
5. **Owner's consent is required BEFORE advertising** — not after. 登广告前必须先取得业主同意。
6. **Every ad needs FOUR identifiers**: RES name, CEA Reg No., EA Licence No., contact number. Dropping the EA licence number is a common wrong "complete" list.
7. **STR goes to STRO via SONAR** — NOT to CEA, NOT to MAS, NOT to the police directly. Distractor portals abound. STR 报给 STRO（用 SONAR 系统）。
8. **Advertising/general penalty cap = $75,000.** Don't confuse with AML DC caps ($100k RES / $200k EA). 广告罚款上限 $75,000，别和 AML 罚款混。
9. **Record retention = 5 years** (was 3). The old "3 years" is a trap. 保存 5 年。
10. **Dual representation is an offence** — you may collect commission only from the party you represent. Two RES from the *same* EA on opposite sides is allowed **only** with written disclosure + written consent and neither collects from both sides.
11. **DC ≠ LOC.** LOC handles *less serious* breaches (max $5k); DC handles *serious* ones (up to $100k/$200k). 别把两者的金额张冠李戴。
12. **Appeal window = 14 days to the MND Appeals Board** (not to CEA, not 30 days).

## (d) Worked Examples / 计算与情景例题

**Example 1 — AML "per breach" penalty calculation (DC, RES).**
A salesperson failed to perform CDD on **4** separate clients and skipped sanctions screening on **2** transactions = **6 breaches**. Under the post-1 Jul 2025 "per breach" rule, the DC may impose up to **$100,000 per breach**.
→ Theoretical maximum exposure = 6 × $100,000 = **$600,000**.
*(Contrast: under the old "per case" basis this would have been capped at one penalty of $100,000. This is the exam's favourite illustration of why "per breach" matters.)*
中文：6 项违规 × 每项 $100,000 = 最高 **$60 万**。旧制"按案件"只罚一次 $10 万。

**Example 2 — Identify the correct due-diligence duty.**
Your client (the buyer, whom you represent) is purchasing a private condo from a seller who has **no agent**. Which checks apply?
→ **CDD on your buyer-client** AND **UCPDD on the unrepresented seller**, **before** your client signs the S&P/exercises the OTP. (If it were an HDB *rental*, UCPDD would NOT apply.)
中文：对买方做 CDD，对无代理的卖方做 UCPDD，都要在签约前完成。

**Example 3 — Is the advertisement compliant?**
An online listing shows the salesperson's name, contact number and CEA Registration No. but **omits the EA Licence No.** and overstates the floor area.
→ **Non-compliant** on two counts: (i) missing the EA Licence No. (one of the four mandatory identifiers); (ii) inaccurate material info (floor area not verified). Penalty exposure: up to **$75,000** + possible suspension/revocation.
中文：缺经纪公司执照号 + 面积不实 = 违规，最高罚 **$75,000**。

**Example 4 — Penalty mechanism selection.**
A minor, first-time, non-criminal advertising lapse (e.g. a single missing contact number) is most likely dealt with via a **Letter of Censure (LOC)** — max **$5,000** — not a full DC referral.
中文：轻微初犯走 LOC，最高 $5,000；严重的才转纪律委员会。

- **Pass mark 60/100 (60%)**; 75% = course attendance only. 及格 60，出勤 75。

# P2.3 Sale of Completed Property: Option to Purchase (OTP) & Sale & Purchase Agreement

## P2.3 Sale of Completed Property: Option to Purchase (OTP) & Sale & Purchase Agreement (S&P)
**RES Paper 2 (CU3–CU4) | Exam: 18 July 2026 | All figures verified current as of June 2026**

> Scope note: This topic covers the **resale of completed/existing property** (not new-launch developer sales). The mechanism is OTP → Exercise → completion. You must master the differences between **HDB resale** and **private resale** OTP rules, the conveyancing chain, caveats, requisitions, and the stamp-duty consequences that crystallise on the OTP/S&P. 本节专讲"二手完工房产"的买卖流程：选购书(OTP)→ 行使(Exercise)→ 完成(Completion)。重点区分组屋(HDB)与私宅(Private)规则。

---

## (a) Key Concepts / 核心概念

| Concept | What it is | 中文要点 |
|---|---|---|
| **Option to Purchase (OTP)** | A legal contract where the **seller (Grantor)** grants the **buyer (Grantee)** an exclusive, irrevocable right to buy the property at an agreed price within a fixed **Option Period**, in exchange for an **Option Fee**. | 卖方给买方一个"独家、不可撤销"的购买权，买方付"订金(Option Fee)"换取。 |
| **Granting the OTP** | Seller signs and hands the OTP to the buyer; buyer pays the Option Fee. The buyer is **not yet bound** — only the seller is bound to hold the offer open. | 卖方授予OTP，买方付订金。此时只有卖方被"锁住"，买方尚未受约束。 |
| **Exercising the OTP** | Buyer **accepts** the offer by signing the acceptance/exercise portion and paying the **Exercise Fee** before the Option expires. This creates a **binding sale contract**. | 买方在到期前签署并付"行使费(Exercise Fee)"，合同正式生效。 |
| **Option Period** | The window within which the buyer must decide to exercise. If buyer does not exercise → option **lapses** and the Option Fee is **forfeited** to the seller. | 决定期。逾期不行使 → 订金被卖方没收(forfeit)。 |
| **Sale & Purchase Agreement (S&P / SPA)** | The full sale contract. For **private resale**, the **exercised OTP itself usually serves as the binding contract**; a separate S&P is more typical of new-launch/developer sales. | 买卖协议。私宅二手房中，"已行使的OTP"本身即合同；正式S&P多见于新盘。 |
| **Equitable interest** | On **exercise**, the buyer acquires an **equitable interest** in the property — the legal basis for lodging a **caveat**. | 行使后买方取得"衡平法权益"，可据此登记 caveat(预告登记/权益冻结)。 |
| **Completion** | Final step: balance paid, title transferred, keys handed over, transfer instrument registered. | 完成交易：付尾款、过户、交钥匙。 |

**The transaction spine (memorise the sequence):**
`OTP granted (pay Option Fee) → buyer secures financing/IPA → Exercise OTP (pay Exercise Fee) → lodge caveat → legal requisitions + title investigation → Completion (balance + transfer + keys)`

---

## (b) Must-Know Rules & Exact Figures / 必背规则与数字

### B1. HDB Resale OTP vs Private Resale OTP (THE core comparison table)

| Feature | **HDB Resale** (statutory, fixed) | **Private Resale** (market convention, negotiable) |
|---|---|---|
| **Option Fee** | **$1 – $1,000** (mutually agreed) | ~**1%** of purchase price |
| **Exercise Fee** | Such that **Option Fee + Exercise Fee ≤ $5,000 combined** (so Exercise Fee ≤ $4,000) | ~**4%** of purchase price (so 1% + 4% = **5% total deposit**) |
| **Both fees** | Form **part of the resale price** | Form part of price; deducted from balance at completion |
| **Option Period** | **21 calendar days** (incl. weekends/PH), expires **4pm** on the 21st day | ~**14 days** (negotiable) |
| **OTP form** | **Prescribed HDB OTP** (standard template, cannot alter terms) | Solicitor/agent-drafted; terms negotiable |
| **Pre-OTP step** | Both parties register **Intent to Sell / Intent to Buy** on HDB Resale Portal (HFE letter check); **7-day seller cooling-off** before granting OTP | No statutory cooling-off |
| **Completion** | ~**8 weeks** after HDB accepts resale application | ~**10–12 weeks** after exercise |

> 中文小结：HDB 的订金与行使费**合计上限 $5,000**（订金 ≤ $1,000），决定期**固定 21 个日历日，下午 4 点截止**；私宅则按市场惯例 **1% 订金 + 4% 行使费 = 5% 定金**，决定期约 **14 天**，皆可议。

### B2. The legal mechanics — who is bound, when

| Stage | Seller bound? | Buyer bound? | Money at stake |
|---|---|---|---|
| OTP granted | **YES** — must hold offer open for the whole Option Period | **NO** — free to walk away | Option Fee paid |
| Buyer lets it lapse (no exercise) | Released | Released | **Option Fee FORFEITED** to seller |
| OTP exercised | **YES** | **YES** — binding contract formed | Exercise Fee paid; full deposit at risk |
| Buyer defaults after exercise | Can forfeit deposit / sue | Liable | Typically forfeits **up to the deposit** (private) |

> 中文：授予后只锁卖方；买方放弃则没收订金；一旦行使，双方都被约束，买方违约通常损失定金。

### B3. Stamp duties that crystallise on the OTP/S&P (verified 2026)

Stamp duty is computed on the **HIGHER of purchase price or market value**, and payable **within 14 days** of exercise (signed in SG) / **30 days** (signed overseas).

**Buyer's Stamp Duty (BSD) — same for ALL buyers, residential (eff. 15 Feb 2023):**

| Portion of price/value | Rate |
|---|---|
| First $180,000 | 1% |
| Next $180,000 | 2% |
| Next $640,000 (→ $1m) | 3% |
| Next $500,000 (→ $1.5m) | 4% |
| Next $1,500,000 (→ $3m) | 5% |
| Above $3,000,000 | 6% |

**Additional Buyer's Stamp Duty (ABSD) — residential only, by buyer profile (eff. 27 Apr 2023, unchanged 2026):**

| Buyer | 1st | 2nd | 3rd+ |
|---|---|---|---|
| Singapore Citizen | 0% | **20%** | **30%** |
| Singapore PR | **5%** | **30%** | **35%** |
| Foreigner | **60%** | 60% | 60% |
| Entity / Trustee | **65%** | 65% | 65% |

*FTA exemption (treated as SC = 0/20/30%): US citizens; citizens & PRs of Iceland, Liechtenstein, Norway, Switzerland.*

**Seller's Stamp Duty (SSD) — residential, purchased ON/AFTER 4 Jul 2025 (CURRENT):**

| Holding period | Rate |
|---|---|
| ≤1 year | **16%** |
| >1–2 years | **12%** |
| >2–3 years | **8%** |
| >3–4 years | **4%** |
| >4 years | 0% |

> 中文：所有印花税按"价格或市值取高者"计算，行使后 **14 天内**缴纳。**BSD 对所有买家相同**；**ABSD 看身份+第几套**；**SSD 看持有年限**（2025年7月4日后购入者持有期延长至 4 年，税率 16/12/8/4%）。

### B4. Conveyancing & Completion chain (post-exercise)

1. **Appoint conveyancing lawyer** (often at OTP stage).
2. **Lodge caveat** at SLA (via STARS portal, fee ≈ S$64.45) to protect the buyer's equitable interest. Caveat valid **5 years**, extendable. **Precautionary, NOT mandatory.**
3. **Legal requisitions** sent to government bodies (PUB, LTA, NEA, IRAS, URA, etc.) to confirm **no acquisition/road/development plans** affect the property + confirm **good root of title**; examine **Certificate of Title**.
4. **Completion:** exchange cashier's orders for the balance, hand over keys, title deed and **transfer instrument**; register transfer (Torrens — legal title vests on **registration**).

**HDB resale specifics:** Request for Value by buyer ($120; valid 3 months); submit resale application (admin fee $40 for 1–2rm / $80 for 3rm+, each side); HDB processing ~2–4 weeks; completion ~8 weeks after acceptance. If HDB acts as solicitor, parties attend in person at HDB Hub.

> 中文：行使后 → 委托律师 → 登记 caveat 保护权益 → 向政府部门"法律查询(requisitions)"确认无征收/道路/发展计划、产权清晰 → 完成过户（Torrens 制度下登记才取得法定产权）。

## (c) Common Exam Traps / 易错点

1. **Who is bound after OTP is granted?** Only the **SELLER**. The buyer is NOT bound until **exercise**. Distractors will say "both bound on granting" — wrong.
   > 授予后只锁卖方，行使后才锁买方。

2. **HDB $5,000 cap is a COMBINED cap** (Option Fee + Exercise Fee), not $5,000 each. Option Fee alone capped at **$1,000**, so Exercise Fee ≤ $4,000.
   > $5,000 是两项合计上限，不是各 $5,000。

3. **HDB Option Period = 21 calendar days expiring 4pm** — calendar (incl. weekends/PH), not working days. Private ≈ 14 days. Don't swap them.

4. **Stamp duty on the HIGHER of price or market value** — never the lower. Applies to BSD, ABSD, SSD.

5. **BSD is buyer-profile-INDEPENDENT** (same for citizen/PR/foreigner). Only **ABSD** depends on citizenship + count. A classic distractor adds ABSD-style tiers to BSD.

6. **SSD holding period is now 4 years** for purchases on/after 4 Jul 2025 (16/12/8/4%). Older 3-year (12/8/4%) version is a distractor — read the purchase date in the stem.

7. **Caveat is precautionary, NOT mandatory**, and the **Registrar cannot remove it on his own motion**. The buyer's interest exists from **exercise**, not from granting.

8. **Exercising = paying Exercise Fee + signing acceptance**, not merely "intending to buy". Letting the option lapse forfeits the Option Fee.

9. **For private resale, the exercised OTP IS the binding contract** — a separate formal S&P is characteristic of **new-launch/developer** sales, not resale. Don't assume every resale needs a separate S&P.

10. **"EXCEPT / NOT / LEAST" inversions** in stems — re-read carefully (e.g., "Which is NOT a step before completion?").

11. **Option Fee + Exercise Fee form part of the price** (HDB) — they are not "extra" charges on top.

## (d) Worked Examples / 计算例题

**Example 1 — HDB resale OTP fees.**
Buyer and seller agree Option Fee = $1,000. What is the maximum Exercise Fee?
- Combined cap = $5,000. Exercise Fee = $5,000 − $1,000 = **$4,000 max.**

**Example 2 — Private resale deposit.**
Private condo, agreed price **$1,500,000**. Standard 1% Option Fee + 4% Exercise Fee.
- Option Fee = 1% × $1,500,000 = **$15,000** (paid at granting).
- Exercise Fee = 4% × $1,500,000 = **$60,000** (paid at exercise).
- Total deposit = 5% = **$75,000**; balance 95% = **$1,425,000** at completion.

**Example 3 — BSD on the same $1.5m purchase (residential).**
- 1% × $180,000 = $1,800
- 2% × $180,000 = $3,600
- 3% × $640,000 = $19,200
- 4% × $500,000 = $20,000
- **BSD = $1,800 + $3,600 + $19,200 + $20,000 = $44,600.**

**Example 4 — ABSD: Singapore PR buying her 2nd residential property at $1.5m.**
- ABSD rate (SPR, 2nd) = **30%**.
- ABSD = 30% × $1,500,000 = **$450,000.**
- Total stamp duty = BSD $44,600 + ABSD $450,000 = **$494,600**, payable within 14 days of exercise.

**Example 5 — SSD: SC sold residential property bought 1 Aug 2025, sold after 18 months, price $1,200,000.**
- Bought on/after 4 Jul 2025 → current 4-year SSD scale. Holding >1–2 years → **12%**.
- SSD = 12% × $1,200,000 = **$144,000** (on higher of price/market value).

**Example 6 — BSD on a $1.2m residential property (full tier walk-through).**
- 1%×180k=$1,800; 2%×180k=$3,600; 3%×640k=$19,200; 4%×200k=$8,000 → **BSD = $32,600.**

## 速记总结 / Quick Recall

- **OTP sequence:** Granted (pay Option Fee) → Exercise (pay Exercise Fee, contract binds) → Caveat → Requisitions → Completion.
- **On granting: only SELLER bound.** On exercise: BOTH bound. Lapse = **Option Fee forfeited**.

- **Private:** ~**1% Option + 4% Exercise = 5% deposit**; ~**14-day** option; completion ~**10–12 weeks**; exercised OTP = the contract.

- **BSD:** same for all buyers — 1/2/3/4/5/6%. **ABSD:** SC 0/20/30, PR 5/30/35, FR 60, Entity/Trustee 65. **SSD (bought ≥4 Jul 2025):** 16/12/8/4% over **4 years**.

- **Requisitions** confirm no acquisition/development plans + good root of title; **Torrens — legal title vests on registration.**
- **Traps:** $5,000 is COMBINED; 21 days is CALENDAR; duty on HIGHER value; BSD ≠ profile-dependent; SSD now 4 years.

# P2.4 Sale of Uncompleted/Developer Property: Progressive Payment, SSD, Sales Process

## P2.4 Sale of Uncompleted / Developer Property: Progressive Payment, SSD & Sales Process
**RES Paper 2 — Revision Notes | Exam: 18 July 2026**
*Figures verified against IRAS, MAS, CEA, URA, SLA primary sources (current as of June 2026). Currency rule: you will NOT be tested on changes implemented after ~18 June 2026.*

---

## (a) Key Concepts / 核心概念

**Uncompleted property (Building Under Construction, "BUC")** — a residential unit sold by a licensed **housing developer** before construction is finished. The whole sale is regulated by the **Housing Developers (Control & Licensing) Act** and the **Housing Developers Rules (HDR)**, administered by the **Controller of Housing (COH)** under URA. This is fundamentally different from a private resale (which is governed by ordinary contract law).
> 中文：新盘/楼花（未建成单位）由持牌发展商出售，受《房屋发展商法令》及《房屋发展商规则》监管，监管机构是市区重建局（URA）下的住房管制官（COH）。这与私人转售（普通合同法）完全不同。

**Progressive Payment Scheme (PPS) / 渐进式付款** — the buyer pays the purchase price in **prescribed instalments tied to verified construction milestones**, NOT all at once. Percentages are **fixed by law (HDR Schedule)** and developers **cannot vary them**. Each stage is triggered by a **Architect's Certificate** that the milestone is complete; the developer then issues a notice and the buyer (or bank) has **14 days** to pay.
> 中文：渐进式付款 = 按建筑进度分期付款，每一阶段的百分比由法律固定，发展商不可更改；每阶段须由建筑师证书确认完工，买方/银行收到通知后14天内付款。

**Statutory protection documents:**
- **Option to Purchase (OTP)** + **booking fee 5–10%** → **Sale & Purchase Agreement (SPA)** in the **prescribed statutory form** (Form in HDR). Developer cannot use its own non-standard contract.
- **Project Account** — all buyer monies go into a developer's project account (a trust-like account), released only against certified work. Protects buyers from developer insolvency.
> 中文：购买流程标准化——意向金/订金（5–10%）→ 法定格式的买卖合约（SPA）；所有买家款项进入"工程账户"（类信托），按工程进度释放，保护买家。

**SSD (Seller's Stamp Duty) / 卖方印花税** — a duty on the **seller** if a residential property is sold within the holding period. Designed to deter speculative "flipping". Highly relevant to BUC because buyers who sub-sell before completion can be hit hard.

---

## (b) Must-Know Rules & Exact Figures / 必背规则与数字

### B1. Progressive Payment Scheme — 8 statutory stages (Normal scheme)

| # | Construction milestone | % of price | Cumulative | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Upon grant of **OTP** (booking fee) | **5%** | 5% | Paid on booking |
| 2 | Within **8 weeks** — signing **S&P Agreement** (balance of 20% downpayment) | **15%** | 20% | Stamp duty (BSD/ABSD) due here |
| 3 | Completion of **foundation** work | **10%** | 30% | |
| 4 | Completion of **reinforced concrete framework** | **10%** | 40% | |
| 5 | Completion of **brick walls** (partition walls) | **5%** | 45% | |
| 6 | Completion of **roofing/ceiling** | **5%** | 50% | |
| 7 | Completion of **door/window frames, electrical wiring, plumbing** | **5%** | 55% | |
| 8 | Completion of **car parks, roads & drains** serving the project | **5%** | 60% | |
| 9 | **Temporary Occupation Permit (TOP)** | **25%** | 85% | Keys handed over; buyer can occupy/rent |
| 10 | **Certificate of Statutory Completion (CSC)** / Legal completion | **15%** | 100% | Final 15% held until CSC |

*(The 5 mid-construction items #5–#8 are sometimes grouped; total of the construction-stage block is the load-bearing figure. The exam-critical numbers are 20% by S&P, 10% foundation, 25% at TOP, 15% at CSC.)*
> 中文必记：订金5% → 签约共20% → 地基10% → 钢筋混凝土框架10% → 砖墙/屋顶/门窗水电各5% → 停车场道路排水5%（累计60%）→ TOP取钥匙付25%（累计85%）→ CSC法定完工付最后15%（100%）。百分比法定固定，发展商不可改。

### B2. SSD — Residential, purchased ON/AFTER 4 Jul 2025 (CURRENT for 2026)

| Holding period | SSD rate |
|---|---|
| Up to 1 year | **16%** |
| > 1 to 2 years | **12%** |
| > 2 to 3 years | **8%** |
| > 3 to 4 years | **4%** |
| > 4 years | **0%** |

- **Charged on the HIGHER of selling price or market value.**
- **Holding period = 4 years** (extended from 3 years on 4 Jul 2025; rates raised +4 ppt per tier).
- Prior regime (purchased **11 Mar 2017 – 3 Jul 2025**): **12% / 8% / 4% / 0%** over **3 years**.
- For BUC, the holding period generally runs from the **date the buyer exercises the OTP / signs the S&P** (date of acquisition), NOT from TOP/CSC.
> 中文：2025年7月4日起买入的住宅，持有期延长至4年，税率16/12/8/4%；按售价与市值中较高者征收；楼花的持有期从行使OTP/签约日起算（不是TOP）。

### B3. ABSD on developers (sale-side context)

| Non-remittable | **5%** | Payable within **14 days**, no remission |

### B4. Buyer financing limits on BUC (apply at S&P stage)

| Metric | Value | Applies |
|---|---|---|

## (c) Common Exam Traps / 易错点

1. **PPS percentages are statutory and cannot be varied** by the developer. Distractor: "developer sets its own schedule." ❌
2. **TOP = 25%, CSC = 15%** — candidates swap these. TOP (keys/occupation) takes the bigger 25%; final 15% is held back until CSC. 别搞反。
3. **SSD is paid by the SELLER, not the buyer**; BSD/ABSD are buyer-side. SSD holding period is now **4 years / 16% top rate** for purchases on/after **4 Jul 2025**. Old 3-year/12% applies only to pre-4-Jul-2025 purchases.
4. **SSD & all stamp duties = HIGHER of price or market value**, never the lower.
5. **MSR (30%) only applies to EC bought from a developer**, NOT to private condo BUC. 私人公寓不适用MSR。
6. **Holding period for SSD runs from acquisition (OTP exercise/S&P), not from TOP/CSC.** A buyer who sub-sells a not-yet-completed unit can still be liable.
7. **5% non-remittable developer ABSD** is always payable — even if the developer sells everything on time. Only the 35% is remittable.
8. **Booking fee forfeiture cap = 25%** (developer must return at least 75% if buyer withdraws before signing S&P). Distractor: "developer keeps the full booking fee." ❌
9. **Defects Liability Period = 12 months from TOP** (not from CSC, not 6 months).
10. **Min cash 5%** of the 20% downpayment for a 75%-LTV first loan — the remaining 15% may be CPF/cash. Don't say "20% must be cash."
11. **"EXCEPT/NOT" inversions** — read stems carefully (e.g. "Which is NOT a statutory PPS stage?").

## (d) Worked Examples / 计算示例

**Example 1 — Progressive payments on a $1,500,000 new launch condo (75% loan):**
- OTP booking (5%) = **$75,000** (cash)
- On S&P, balance of 20% (15%) = **$225,000** (5% min cash already in booking; remainder cash/CPF)
- Foundation (10%) = **$150,000** → drawn from 75% bank loan
- RC framework (10%) = $150,000 (loan)
- Walls + roofing + M&E + carparks (5%+5%+5%+5% = 20%) = $300,000 (loan)
- TOP (25%) = **$375,000** (loan)
- CSC (15%) = **$225,000** (loan)
- **Total = $1,500,000.** Buyer cash/CPF outlay = 20% = $300,000; bank funds the 80%… capped at 75% LTV, so buyer tops up the extra 5% from cash/CPF (total equity 25% = $375,000).

**Example 2 — SSD on sub-sale of a BUC unit:**
Buyer exercised OTP **1 Sep 2025** at $1,200,000; sub-sells **1 Mar 2027** at $1,400,000 (market value $1,380,000).
- Purchased on/after 4 Jul 2025 → 4-year regime.
- Holding period = ~1.5 years → falls in **">1 to 2 years" = 12%**.
- Charge on higher of price/market value = $1,400,000.
- **SSD = 12% × $1,400,000 = $168,000.**

**Example 3 — Developer ABSD:**
Developer buys residential land for $200,000,000.
- Total ABSD 40% = $80,000,000.
- Non-remittable 5% = **$10,000,000** (pay within 14 days, no refund).
- Remittable 35% = $70,000,000 — kept only if all units sold within **5 years**. If 90% sold by deadline, clawback applies on the unsold 10% (up to 10 ppt of price) + **5% p.a. interest**.

## (a) Key Concepts / 核心概念

**Conveyancing (产权转让 / 交割流程)** = the legal process of transferring legal title to property from seller to buyer. It runs from the moment a binding contract is formed (exercised OTP / signed S&P) to **completion** (legal title + keys handed over against the balance payment). Average timeline: **~10–12 weeks** for private property; **~8 weeks after HDB acceptance** for HDB resale.

- **Equitable vs Legal interest 衡平法权益 vs 法定权益**: The buyer acquires an **equitable interest** the moment the OTP is exercised / S&P signed — this is the legal basis for lodging a caveat. **Legal title** only passes at **completion**, on registration of the transfer instrument at SLA. 行使购买权后买方取得衡平法权益；交割并在 SLA 注册后才取得法定所有权。
- **The conveyancing lawyer 产权律师** drives the process: appointed after OTP, lodges the caveat, sends legal requisitions, examines title, prepares the transfer, and attends completion. The **RES's role** is to facilitate, explain timelines, ensure documents are executed correctly, and refer parties to legal/financial advice (never give legal advice yourself). RES 协助而非提供法律意见。
- **OTP → Exercise → Completion** is the spine of every transaction question. Know the three documents: **OTP**, **S&P Agreement (SPA)**, and the **transfer/conveyance instrument**.
- **Caveat 警示登记** = precautionary notice of interest, NOT mandatory and NOT a transfer of title.
- **Legal requisitions 法律查询** = pre-completion enquiries to government bodies confirming the property is not affected by acquisition/development plans and has a **good root of title**.

> 中文小结：交割流程 = 从有约束力合同（行使 OTP）到交割（产权+钥匙换尾款）。买方先有衡平法权益（可下警示登记），交割注册后才有法定所有权。

### B1. OTP — Option to Purchase (购买选择权)

| Item | HDB Resale | Private Resale (negotiable) |
|---|---|---|
| Option Fee | **$1 – $1,000** | ~**1%** of price |
| Exercise Fee | Option + Exercise **≤ $5,000 combined** | ~**4%** (→ 5% total deposit) |
| Option Period | **21 calendar days**, expires **4pm** last day | ~**14 days** (negotiable) |
| Both fees | Form part of resale price | Form part of purchase price |
| Seller cooling-off (before granting OTP) | **7 days** | N/A |

中文：HDB OTP 选择费 $1–$1,000，选择费+行使费合计 ≤ $5,000，选择期 21 天（下午 4 点截止）。私宅惯例为 1% + 4%，约 14 天，可协商。

### B2. Conveyancing & Completion Sequence (交割步骤)

| Step | Action |
|---|---|
| 1 | Sign OTP → appoint conveyancing lawyer 委任产权律师 |
| 2 | **Lodge caveat** at SLA (buyer's equitable interest) 下警示登记 |
| 3 | **Exercise OTP** (pay exercise fee) within option period |
| 4 | **Legal requisitions** sent to government bodies (PUB, LTA, NEA, IRAS, etc.) + examine **Certificate of Title** / good root of title |
| 5 | Secure financing; pay **BSD/ABSD within 14 days** of exercise/signing |
| 6 | **Completion**: exchange cashier's orders for keys, title deed & transfer instrument; balance (~95%) paid |
| 7 | Register transfer at SLA → **legal title passes** |

- Legal requisitions go to **seven** government arms (PUB, LTA, NEA, IRAS, and others) — sale proceeds to completion only when all return **satisfactorily**.
- **Caveat lodgement fee ≈ S$64.45** (incl. GST) per application via SLA (STARS portal).
- **HDB resale completion**: ~8 weeks after HDB accepts the resale application. If HDB is the solicitor, parties attend **in person at HDB Hub** (a private solicitor's representative may attend on their behalf).

中文：步骤记牢——签 OTP→委任律师→下警示登记→行使 OTP→发法律查询+查产权证→付印花税（14天内）→交割换钥匙+尾款→注册过户。

### B3. Stamp Duties on Purchase (买方印花税)

**BSD — Buyer's Stamp Duty** (higher of price or market value; applies to ALL buyers; eff. 15 Feb 2023):

| Portion of price/value | Residential | Non-residential |
|---|---|---|
| First $180,000 | 1% | 1% |
| Next $180,000 | 2% | 2% |
| Next $640,000 (→$1m) | 3% | 3% |
| Next $500,000 (→$1.5m) | 4% | 4% |
| Next $1,500,000 (→$3m) | 5% | 5% (above $1.5m) |
| Above $3,000,000 | 6% | — |

**ABSD — Additional Buyer's Stamp Duty** (residential only; eff. 27 Apr 2023, unchanged 2026):

| Buyer | 1st | 2nd | 3rd+ |
|---|---|---|---|
| Singapore Citizen (SC) | 0% | **20%** | **30%** |
| Singapore PR (SPR) | **5%** | **30%** | **35%** |
| Foreigner (FR) | **60%** | 60% | 60% |
| Entity / Trustee | **65%** | 65% | 65% |

- **FTA exemption** (treated as SC = 0/20/30%): **US** citizens; citizens & PRs of **Iceland, Liechtenstein, Norway, Switzerland**.
- **Married-couple remission**: refund of ABSD on 2nd property if first sold within the timeline (≥1 SC spouse).
- **Payment: within 14 days** of executing the instrument (signed in SG) / **30 days** (signed overseas).

中文：BSD 对所有买家相同，按价格或市价较高者计；ABSD 只看公民身份+持有套数。两者均 14 天内缴付（境外签 30 天）。FTA 豁免国民按公民税率（0/20/30%）。

### B4. SSD — Seller's Stamp Duty (卖方印花税; higher of price or market value)

**Residential purchased ON/AFTER 4 Jul 2025 (CURRENT — 4-year holding period):**

| Holding period | Rate |
|---|---|
| ≤1 year | **16%** |
| >1–2 years | **12%** |
| >2–3 years | **8%** |
| >3–4 years | **4%** |
| >4 years | **0%** |

**Residential purchased 11 Mar 2017 – 3 Jul 2025 (prior — 3-year):** 12% / 8% / 4% / 0%.
**Industrial (acquired on/after 12 Jan 2013):** 15% / 10% / 5% / 0% over 3 years.

中文：2025 年 7 月 4 日起买入的住宅，SSD 持有期延长至 4 年，税率 16/12/8/4%。之前买入仍是 3 年 12/8/4%。

### B5. Lease / Tenancy Stamp Duty (租约印花税)

| Lease term | Duty |
|---|---|
| ≤ 4 years | **0.4% × total rent** (= 0.4% × AAR × years) |
| > 4 years / indefinite | **0.4% × 4 × AAR** |
| AAR ≤ $1,000 | **EXEMPT** |

- Rounded **down** to nearest dollar; minimum duty **$1**.
- Pay within **14 days** (signed in SG) / **30 days** (signed overseas).
- **Tenant pays by convention**, but **both parties jointly liable**. AAR = Average Annual Rent.

中文：租约 ≤4 年按总租金 0.4%；>4 年按 0.4%×4×年均租金。年均租金 ≤$1,000 免税。惯例租客付，但双方都有责任。

### B6. CPF Usage at Completion — VL & WL (公积金使用)

| Term | Rule |
|---|---|
| **Valuation Limit (VL)** | Lower of purchase price or market value at purchase; CPF usable up to **100% of VL** |
| **Withdrawal Limit (WL)** | **120% of VL** — absolute cap, **PRIVATE property only**; to exceed 100%→120%, members 55+ must set aside **BRS** |
| **HDB flats** | **NO Withdrawal Limit** (no 120% cap) |
| Lease coverage | Full CPF if remaining lease **≥20yr AND covers youngest buyer to age 95**; pro-rated otherwise; **no CPF if remaining lease <20yr** |

**Retirement sums (turning 55 in 2026):** BRS **$110,200** · FRS **$220,400** · ERS **$440,800**. (2025 cohort: BRS $106,500 / FRS $213,000 / ERS $426,000 — use the cohort the question specifies.)

中文：VL = 购买价/市价较低者，公积金最高用到 100% VL。私宅有 120% 提取上限（WL），超过 100% 须留 BRS。**HDB 没有 WL 上限。** 剩余地契 <20 年不能用公积金。

### B7. GST at Completion (商品及服务税)

- **Residential sale & lease = EXEMPT supply.** Commercial & industrial = **standard-rated 9%**.
- Furnished residential: GST on movable furniture/fittings; fixtures sold with property remain exempt.

中文：住宅买卖/出租免 GST；商业、工业按 9% 计税。

## (c) Common Exam Traps / 易错点

1. **BSD is buyer-profile-independent** — same for everyone. Only **ABSD** depends on citizenship + property count. 别把 BSD 当作随身份变化。
2. **BSD/ABSD/SSD computed on the HIGHER of price or market value** — never the lower. 取较高者，不是较低者。
3. **Stamp duty payment = 14 days (local) / 30 days (overseas)** — distractors will swap these or offer "7 days". 注意 14/30 天。
4. **Caveat is precautionary, NOT mandatory**, and the **Registrar cannot remove it on his own motion**; the caveator must obtain a **court order within 30 days** of SLA's notice (s127). 警示登记非强制，注册官不能自行删除。
5. **Equitable interest arises on exercising OTP; legal title only on registration at completion.** Don't say title passes at OTP signing. 行使 OTP 得衡平法权益，注册才得法定产权。
6. **SSD residential holding period is now 4 years** (purchases on/after 4 Jul 2025), rates 16/12/8/4% — older 3-year 12/8/4% applies only to earlier purchases. Read the **purchase date** in the stem. 看买入日期。
7. **HDB has NO CPF Withdrawal Limit** — the 120% WL applies to **private property only**. HDB 没有 120% 上限。
8. **MSR (30%) applies only to HDB flats & EC from developer**, not private resale; **TDSR (55%)** applies to all property loans. 别混淆 MSR/TDSR 适用范围。
9. **Lease AAR ≤ $1,000 is EXEMPT** from lease stamp duty — distractors will compute a duty anyway. ≤$1,000 免税。
10. **Legal requisitions are sent AFTER appointing the lawyer (around/after exercising OTP), to government bodies** — not to the seller. 法律查询发给政府部门。
11. **"EXCEPT / NOT" inversions** — read the stem carefully; conveyancing questions love negative phrasing. 看清"除…外/不属于"。
12. **Residential property GST-exempt** — candidates wrongly add 9% to a home sale. 住宅免 GST。

## (d) Worked Examples / 计算示例

**Example 1 — BSD on a $1.2m residential property:**
- 1% × $180,000 = $1,800
- 2% × $180,000 = $3,600
- 3% × $640,000 = $19,200
- 4% × $200,000 = $8,000 (remaining $1.2m − $1.0m)
- **BSD = $1,800 + $3,600 + $19,200 + $8,000 = $32,600**

**Example 2 — Total upfront stamp duty: SPR buying 2nd residential property at $1.2m:**
- BSD = **$32,600** (as above)
- ABSD (SPR, 2nd) = 30% × $1,200,000 = **$360,000**
- **Total stamp duty = $392,600** (both payable within 14 days of execution)

**Example 3 — SSD, residential bought 1 Sep 2025, sold after 18 months (purchase on/after 4 Jul 2025 → 4-year regime):**
- 18 months falls in the **">1–2 years"** band → rate **12%**
- If sale price $1,500,000 (assume ≥ market value): SSD = 12% × $1,500,000 = **$180,000**

**Example 4 — Lease stamp duty, 2-year lease at $3,000/month:**
- Total rent = $3,000 × 24 = $72,000; term ≤4 years → 0.4% × $72,000 = **$288**
- (1-year version: $3,000 × 12 = $36,000 → 0.4% = **$144**)

**Example 5 — Lease stamp duty, 6-year lease at $5,000/month:**
- AAR = $5,000 × 12 = $60,000; term >4 years → 0.4% × 4 × $60,000 = **$960**

**Example 6 — CPF Withdrawal Limit, private property purchased at $1,000,000 (= market value, so VL = $1,000,000):**
- Max CPF (Ordinary Account) usable = **120% of VL = $1,200,000**
- Beyond 100% VL ($1,000,000), a member aged 55+ must first set aside the **BRS ($110,200 for 2026 cohort)** before using the extra up to $1.2m.
- If this were an **HDB flat**: no 120% cap applies.

- **ABSD** (residential only): SC 0/20/30 · SPR 5/30/35 · FR 60 · Entity/Trustee 65. **FTA = US, Iceland, Liechtenstein, Norway, Switzerland → SC rates.**
- **SSD** (bought on/after 4 Jul 2025): **16/12/8/4% over 4 years**; older buys 12/8/4% over 3 years.
- **Lease SD**: ≤4yr = 0.4% × total rent; >4yr = 0.4% × 4 × AAR; **AAR ≤$1,000 exempt**; tenant pays by convention, both liable.
- **CPF**: VL = lower of price/value (100% usable); **WL = 120% VL, private only — HDB has no WL**; no CPF if remaining lease <20yr. **2026 BRS $110,200 / FRS $220,400 / ERS $440,800.**
- **GST 9%**: residential **exempt**; commercial/industrial standard-rated.
- **Caveat**: precautionary not mandatory; valid 5yr (+5); fee ~$64.45; Registrar can't self-remove; court order within **30 days** of s127 notice.
- **Calc check**: BSD $1.2m = **$32,600**; SPR 2nd-property ABSD on $1.2m = **$360,000**.
- **Traps**: BSD profile-independent · higher of price/value · 14/30-day payment · MSR(30%) HDB/EC only vs TDSR(55%) all · HDB no WL · negative-phrasing stems.

# P2.6 Tenancy & Leases: Agreements, Lease Stamp Duty, Deposits, Landlord/Tenant Rights

## P2.6 Tenancy & Leases: Agreements, Lease Stamp Duty, Deposits, Landlord/Tenant Rights
### RES Paper 2 — Exam Revision Notes (Study Guide)
**Exam date: 18 July 2026 · Figures verified against IRAS, HDB, URA, CEA primary sources (current as of June 2026)**

> **Currency rule:** You will NOT be tested on policy changes implemented within ~1 month of the exam (after ~18 June 2026). All figures below are current and exam-valid.

## (a) Key Concepts / 核心概念

A **lease (tenancy)** is a contract granting a tenant the right of **exclusive possession** of a property for a defined term, in exchange for rent. It creates an **interest in land** (a leasehold estate), distinguishing it from a mere **licence** (a personal permission to occupy, no possessory interest).

| Concept | Definition | 中文说明 |
|---|---|---|
| **Lease / Tenancy** | Grant of exclusive possession for a term at a rent. Creates an interest in land. | 租约：授予承租人在一定期限内独占占有权，并支付租金；构成土地权益。 |
| **Licence** | Personal permission to use premises; NO exclusive possession; not an interest in land. | 许可：仅个人使用许可，无独占占有权，非土地权益。 |
| **Lessor / Landlord** | The owner granting the lease. | 出租人/房东。 |
| **Lessee / Tenant** | The party taking the lease. | 承租人/租客。 |
| **Letter of Intent (LOI)** | Pre-contract document expressing intent to lease, usually with a **good-faith deposit**; precedes the TA. | 意向书：表达租赁意向，附诚意金，签 TA 前的文件。 |
| **Tenancy Agreement (TA)** | The binding lease contract setting out all terms. | 租赁协议：具约束力的正式合同。 |
| **AAR (Average Annual Rent)** | Total rent + other consideration (e.g. furniture, service, maintenance) ÷ years; the base for lease stamp duty. | 平均年租金：含家具/服务/管理等所有对价；计算印花税的基础。 |
| **Security deposit** | Sum held by landlord against tenant default/damage; NOT to cover fair wear and tear. | 押金：用于抵扣租客违约/损坏，不可扣正常损耗。 |
| **Fair wear and tear** | Natural deterioration from normal use; landlord's responsibility, NOT deductible from deposit. | 正常损耗：使用产生的自然磨损，由房东承担，不得扣押金。 |
| **Diplomatic clause** | Early-termination right (usually ≥24-month leases) if tenant is posted out of / leaves Singapore. | 外交条款：长租约（通常≥2年）中，租客被调离新加坡可提前解约。 |
| **Reinstatement clause** | Tenant must restore premises to original condition at lease end. | 复原条款：租约结束时须恢复原状。 |

**中文核心提示：** 租约 = 独占占有 + 期限 + 租金，三要素缺一即可能只是 licence（许可），不构成土地权益。

## (b) Must-Know Rules & Exact Figures / 必背规则与精确数字

### 1. Estate Agency Agreements for Leasing (4 of the 8 Prescribed Forms)

| Form | Type | Acts for |
|---|---|---|
| **Form 3** | Non-exclusive | Leasing — **Landlord** |
| **Form 4** | Non-exclusive | Leasing — **Tenant** |
| **Form 7** | Exclusive | Leasing — **Landlord** |
| **Form 8** | Exclusive | Leasing — **Tenant** |

- **Exclusive agreement:** one EA/RES only; **max validity 3 months**, no auto-renewal; commission still payable if a property is leased within **3 months after expiry** to a party the agent introduced during validity (post-expiry "tail").
- **Commission is fully negotiable** (NOT regulated by CEA). Rental norm ≈ **1 month's rent** from landlord for a lease ≥1 year (often ½ month for shorter terms).
- **Dual representation is an offence** — an RES cannot collect/share commission from both landlord and tenant in the same deal.

> 中文：租赁用 Form 3/4（非独家）和 Form 7/8（独家）；独家最长 3 个月，到期后 3 个月内成交介绍客仍要付佣金；佣金可议，但同一笔交易不可双边收佣（违法）。

### 2. Lease (Tenancy) Stamp Duty — 印花税 (IRAS)

| Lease term | Stamp duty formula |
|---|---|
| **≤ 4 years** | **0.4% × total rent for the whole lease period** (= 0.4% × AAR × years) |
| **> 4 years OR indefinite** | **0.4% × 4 × AAR** |

**Critical rules:**
- Computed on the **higher of contractual rent or market rent**.
- **AAR ≤ $1,000 → fully EXEMPT** (rarely arises in practice).
- Duty is **rounded DOWN to the nearest dollar**; **minimum duty = $1**.
- AAR **includes** other consideration: furniture, fittings, maintenance/service charges, advertising/promotion contributions, etc.
- **Stamping deadline: within 14 days** if document signed in Singapore; **30 days** if signed overseas.
- **Tenant pays by convention**, but **both parties are jointly liable**. Late/non-stamping → penalty + document is **inadmissible as evidence in court**.

> 中文：≤4年租约印花税 = 0.4% × 全期总租金；>4年 = 0.4% × 4 × 年均租金。年均租金≤$1,000免税；金额向下取整，最低$1。承租人惯例支付，但双方共同负责，须在签署后14天（境外30天）内缴纳，否则有罚款且不能作法庭证据。

### 3. Security & Utility Deposits — 押金

| Item | Norm |
|---|---|
| **Security deposit** | **1 month's rent per year of lease** (1 mo for 1-yr lease; 2 mo for 2-yr lease) |
| **Utility deposit** | Often a separate smaller sum |
| **Refund timing** | Returned after lease end less itemised deductions (commonly ~7–14 days after handover) |
| **Coverage** | Tenant default, unpaid rent/utilities, damage **beyond** fair wear and tear |
| **NOT deductible** | **Fair wear and tear** |

> 中文：押金惯例 = 每年租期 1 个月租金（1年租约付1个月，2年付2个月）；用于抵扣欠租、损坏，但**正常损耗不得扣**；租约结束后约 7–14 天内退还（扣除明细列支项）。

### 4. Key Lease Clauses — 关键条款

| Clause | What it does | 中文 |
|---|---|---|
| **Diplomatic clause** | Tenant may terminate early (usually after **12 months** with **2 months'** notice) if relocated out of / leaves Singapore. Standard on **≥24-month** leases. | 外交条款：通常住满12个月后、提前2个月通知即可解约（≥2年租约）。 |
| **Reimbursement / pro-rated commission clause** | If diplomatic clause exercised, tenant reimburses landlord part of the agent commission. | 偿还条款：若启用外交条款，租客须补偿房东部分佣金。 |
| **Minor repair clause** | Tenant bears minor repairs up to a cap (≈ **$150–$300 per item**); above cap = landlord. | 小修条款：每项小修在 ~$150–$300 内由租客承担，超过由房东负责。 |
| **Reinstatement clause** | Tenant restores premises to original condition at end of term. | 复原条款：到期恢复原状。 |
| **"Good and tenantable repair"** | Tenant's standard of upkeep, fair wear & tear excepted. | 维持"良好可居住状态"，正常损耗除外。 |

### 5. Landlord vs Tenant Obligations — 房东与租客义务

| Party | Obligations |
|---|---|
| **Tenant** | Pay rent & utilities; pay **lease stamp duty** (by convention); keep premises in "good and tenantable repair" (fair wear & tear excepted); minor repairs up to cap; reinstate at end; do not sublet without consent. |
| **Landlord** | **Major/structural repairs**; bear **fair wear and tear**; ensure quiet enjoyment; return deposit (less valid deductions); maintain property tax payment. |

> 中文：租客付租金/水电/印花税、维持可居住状态、承担小修；房东负责大修/结构、承担正常损耗、保障安静使用权、退还押金。

### 6. Renting Out HDB Flats — HDB 出租规则

| Item | Rule |
|---|---|
| **Who may rent out whole flat** | **SC owners only**, after fulfilling **MOP (5 yrs standard)**; HDB approval required. SPR owners CANNOT rent out the whole flat. |
| **Minimum rental period** | **6 months** |
| **Max tenancy term** | 3 yrs (SC/Malaysian tenants); 2 yrs (other non-citizens) |
| **Non-Citizen Quota (whole flat)** | **8% neighbourhood / 11% block**; applies when tenant is SPR or foreigner (non-Malaysian); **Malaysians exempt**. When quota full → only SC/Malaysian tenants. |
| **Renting out bedrooms** | 3-rm+ only (3-rm: max 1 room; 4-rm+: max 2 rooms); owner may rent before MOP **if owner still lives there**; **Non-Citizen Quota does NOT apply to bedroom rental** (owner stays with tenants). |
| **Occupancy cap** (relaxed **22 Jan 2024 – 31 Dec 2026**) | **8 persons** for 4-rm+; 6 for 3-rm; 4 for 1-/2-rm. (Reverts to 6 for 3-rm+ after 31 Dec 2026.) |
| **Prime / Plus flats** | Prime: no whole-flat rental even after MOP; Plus: restricted. |

> 中文：整套出租仅限**满 MOP（标准5年）的 SC 业主**，需 HDB 批准，最短租期 6 个月；非公民配额 8%/11%（马来西亚人免）；房间出租 3房以上可，业主同住可在 MOP 前出租，且**房间出租不受配额限制**；居住人数上限 4房+为 8 人（截至 2026年12月31日的临时放宽）。

## (c) Common Exam Traps / 易错点

1. **Lease ≤4 years uses 0.4% of TOTAL rent, NOT annual rent.** The "× 4 × AAR" cap only kicks in for leases **over 4 years / indefinite**. 易错：≤4年用全期总租金，不是年租金。
2. **AAR includes furniture/service/maintenance charges** — distractors will give "rent only." Don't forget furnished-unit add-ons.
3. **Stamp duty is on the HIGHER of contractual vs market rent.** Not the lower, not always the contractual.
4. **AAR exemption threshold is ≤ $1,000 (annual), not monthly.** A $1,000/month rent is NOT exempt (AAR = $12,000).
5. **Stamping deadline = 14 days (SG) / 30 days (overseas)** — not "before completion," not "anytime."
6. **Tenant pays by convention, but BOTH parties are jointly liable.** A question saying "only the tenant is liable" is wrong.
7. **Fair wear and tear is NOT deductible** from the security deposit — a classic distractor lets the landlord deduct it.
8. **Deposit norm = 1 month per YEAR of lease**, not a flat "1 month." 2-year lease → 2 months.
9. **Diplomatic clause can only be exercised AFTER 12 months** (not from day one) on ~24-month leases.
10. **SPR HDB owners cannot rent out the WHOLE flat** (only SC after MOP). Bedrooms differ.
11. **Non-Citizen Quota applies to whole-flat rental only**, NOT bedroom rental; **Malaysians are exempt** from the quota.
12. **Occupancy cap of 8 (for 4-rm+) is a TEMPORARY relaxation expiring 31 Dec 2026** — but for a 18 July 2026 exam it is still in force; use 8.
13. **Minimum HDB rental period = 6 months** (not 1 month, not 3 months).
14. **Exclusive leasing agreement max 3 months** with a **3-month post-expiry commission tail** — don't confuse the two "3 months."
15. **Lease creates an interest in land; a licence does NOT** — the test is **exclusive possession**.

> 中文易错总结：①≤4年用总租金；②年均租金含家具/服务费；③取价高者；④免税门槛是年$1,000（非月）；⑤14/30天；⑥双方共同负责；⑦正常损耗不可扣；⑧押金按"每年1个月"；⑨外交条款满12个月才可用；⑩SPR不能整套出租。

## (d) Worked Examples / 计算示例

### Example 1 — Standard 1-year furnished lease (≤4 years)
Rent = **$3,000/month**, 12-month lease, no separate furniture charge.
- Total rent = $3,000 × 12 = **$36,000**
- Stamp duty = 0.4% × $36,000 = **$144**

### Example 2 — 2-year lease with furniture charge (AAR includes add-ons)
Rent = $2,500/month + $200/month furniture, 24-month lease.
- Monthly consideration = $2,500 + $200 = $2,700
- Total = $2,700 × 24 = $64,800
- Stamp duty = 0.4% × $64,800 = **$259.20 → rounded down = $259**
- Security deposit (norm, 1 mo/yr) = 2 × $2,500 = **$5,000** (deposit usually on base rent)

### Example 3 — Lease OVER 4 years (use the ×4×AAR cap)
Rent = $4,000/month, **6-year** lease.
- AAR = $4,000 × 12 = $48,000
- Because term > 4 years → Stamp duty = 0.4% × 4 × AAR = 0.4% × 4 × $48,000 = **$768**
- (Note: the full-period formula would have given 0.4% × $288,000 = $1,152 — so the >4yr rule **caps** the duty lower.)

### Example 4 — Exemption check
Rent = $80/month, 12-month lease.
- AAR = $80 × 12 = $960 → **AAR ≤ $1,000 → EXEMPT**, duty = $0.
- (If rent were $90/month → AAR = $1,080 > $1,000 → duty = 0.4% × $1,080 = $4.32 → $4.)

> 中文：①$3,000×12 = $36,000 → 印花税 $144；②含家具$2,700×24 = $64,800 → $259（向下取整）；③6年租约用 0.4%×4×年租金 = $768（封顶更低）；④年均租金$960免税。

- **Lease vs licence** → exclusive possession = lease (interest in land). 独占占有 = 租约。
- **Leasing forms:** Non-exclusive **3 (landlord) / 4 (tenant)**; Exclusive **7 (landlord) / 8 (tenant)**. Exclusive max **3 months** + **3-month** post-expiry tail.
- **Stamp duty ≤4 yrs = 0.4% × total rent.** >4 yrs/indefinite = **0.4% × 4 × AAR**. 取价高者。
- **AAR includes furniture/service/maintenance.** AAR **≤ $1,000 = exempt.** Rounded **down**, min **$1**.
- **Stamp within 14 days (SG) / 30 days (overseas).** Tenant pays by convention; **both jointly liable.**

- **Diplomatic clause** = exit after **12 months**, **2 months'** notice, on ~**24-month** leases; pairs with reimbursement clause.
- **Minor repair cap** ≈ **$150–$300/item**; above → landlord. Landlord = major/structural + wear & tear.

## (a) Key Concepts / 核心概念

This is the single most calculation-heavy topic in Paper 2. Most questions are **Application-level MCQs** — you must *compute*, not just recall. The exam tests whether you can pick the correct tiered formula, apply it to the **right tax base**, and read **"higher of / except / not"** wording correctly.

| Concept | What it means | 中文 |
|---|---|---|
| **Stamp duty base** | BSD, ABSD and SSD are all charged on the **HIGHER of purchase price OR market value**. | 印花税按"成交价或市价中的较高者"计税 |
| **Progressive (tiered) vs flat** | BSD, owner-occupier property tax = **tiered marginal** (each band taxed at its own rate). ABSD, non-residential property tax, GST = **flat** (one rate on the whole amount). | 累进 vs 单一税率 |
| **Buyer-profile dependence** | **BSD is the same for everyone.** **ABSD depends on citizenship + count of residential properties owned.** | BSD人人相同；ABSD看身份和持有套数 |
| **Holding period (SSD)** | Time from **acquisition date to disposal date**. Charged on the seller. | 持有期=买入到卖出 |

| **AAR (lease)** | Average Annual Rent = total rent ÷ number of years; basis for lease stamp duty. | 年均租金 |
| **GST supply types** | Residential = **EXEMPT**; commercial/industrial = **standard-rated 9%**. | 住宅免税，商工业9% |

## (b) Must-Know Rules & Exact Figures / 必背规则与数字

### 1. BSD — Buyer's Stamp Duty (eff. 15 Feb 2023; current 2026)
Charged on **higher of price or market value**. **Same for ALL buyers** regardless of nationality/count.

| Portion of value | Residential | Non-residential |
|---|---|---|
| First $180,000 | 1% | 1% |
| Next $180,000 (→$360k) | 2% | 2% |
| Next $640,000 (→$1m) | 3% | 3% |
| Next $500,000 (→$1.5m) | 4% | 4% |
| Next $1,500,000 (→$3m) | 5% | **5% (everything above $1.5m)** |
| Above $3,000,000 | 6% | — |

**Shortcut formulas (residential):**
- $360k–$1m: `3% × price − $5,400`
- $1m–$1.5m: `4% × price − $15,400`
- $1.5m–$3m: `5% × price − $30,400`
- Above $3m: `6% × price − $60,400`

中文：BSD 累进、人人相同、按较高者计；非住宅最高 5%，住宅最高 6%。

---

### 2. ABSD — Additional Buyer's Stamp Duty (eff. 27 Apr 2023; unchanged 2026)
**Residential property only.** **Flat rate** on the *whole* higher-of value. Charged **on top of** BSD.

| Buyer profile | 1st property | 2nd | 3rd & subsequent |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Singapore Citizen (SC)** | 0% | **20%** | **30%** |
| **Singapore PR (SPR)** | **5%** | **30%** | **35%** |
| **Foreigner (FR)** | **60%** | 60% | 60% |
| **Entity / company** | **65%** | 65% | 65% |
| **Trustee (living trust)** | **65%** | 65% | 65% |
| **Housing Developer** | 35% remittable (conditions) + 5% non-remittable | — | — |

- **FTA exemption (treated as SC = 0%/20%/30%):** Nationals & PRs of **U**SA, **I**celand, **L**iechtenstein, **N**orway, **S**witzerland. *(Mnemonic: "**US + the 4 NORDIC/EFTA = treated like Citizens**".)*
- **Married-couple remission:** refund of ABSD on the 2nd property if the 1st is sold within the timeline (couple must include ≥1 SC).

中文：ABSD 只针对住宅、按身份与套数、单一税率、整额计；FTA 五类（美+冰岛+列支敦士登+挪威+瑞士）当公民看待。

| Holding period | Rate |
|---|---|
| ≤ 1 year | **16%** |
| > 1 to 2 years | **12%** |

### 4. LTV — Loan-to-Value Limits (MAS; current 2026)

| Loan | Bank loan (all conditions met) | Bank (tenure >30yr / >25yr HDB, OR loan beyond age 65) | Min cash down |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1st housing loan** | **75%** | 55% | 5% cash (@75%) |
| **2nd housing loan** | **45%** | 25% | 25% (≥25% cash) |
| **3rd+ housing loan** | **35%** | 15% | 25% (≥25% cash) |
| **HDB concessionary loan** | **75%** (cut from 80% on 20 Aug 2024) | — | No mandatory cash (CPF OK) |

**Conditions for the higher LTV:** tenure ≤30yr (≤25yr HDB) **AND** loan does not extend beyond borrower's age 65.

中文：首套最高 75%、第二套 45%、第三套起 35%；若贷款年限过长或超过 65 岁，LTV 降至 55/25/15%。HDB 优惠贷款 2024年8月起也是 75%。

---

### 5. TDSR / MSR / Stress-Test (MAS)

| Metric | Value | Applies to |
|---|---|---|
| **TDSR** | **55%** of gross monthly income (counts ALL debts) | All property loans |
| **MSR** | **30%** of gross monthly income (housing loan only) | **HDB flats & EC bought from developer ONLY** |
| **Stress-test floor — residential** | **4.0% p.a.** (or actual rate if higher) | TDSR/MSR computation |
| **Stress-test floor — non-residential** | **5.0% p.a.** | TDSR computation |
| **Loan tenure caps** | Private bank **35yr** · HDB bank loan **30yr** · HDB concessionary **25yr** | |

中文：TDSR 55%（算所有债务），MSR 30%（只算房贷，仅 HDB 与发展商 EC）。计算月供时按压力测试利率 4%（住宅）/5%（非住宅）。实际放款额取 LTV、TDSR、MSR 三者中最低。

---

### 6. Commission (CEA)
- **NOT fixed or regulated by CEA — fully negotiable**, documented in the Estate Agency Agreement (EAA).
- **Dual representation is an offence** — an RES may collect commission only from the party they represent.
- Market norms (for *distractor* recognition, not rules): residential sale ~**2%** from seller; rental ~**1 month's rent** from landlord (for ≥1-year lease).
- **SEAA Co-Broking Best Practice Guide** (eff. 1 Jul 2024): each side pays its own agent — a *guide*, **not a regulation** (no penalty).

中文：佣金不受 CEA 管制、可议；双重代理违法；SEAA 共同代理指南只是行业指引，不是法规。

---

### 7. Property Tax (IRAS; based on Annual Value, AV)

**Owner-occupier residential (eff. 1 Jan 2025) — tiered:**

| AV portion | Rate |
|---|---|
| First $12,000 | 0% |
| > $12k – $40k | 4% |
| > $40k – $50k | 6% |
| > $50k – $75k | 10% |
| > $75k – $85k | 14% |
| > $85k – $100k | 20% |
| > $100k – $140k | 26% |
| > $140k | 32% |

**Non-owner-occupier (rented/vacant) residential (eff. 1 Jan 2024):** First $30k = **12%**; >$30k–$45k = **20%**; >$45k–$60k = **28%**; >$60k = **36%**.
**Non-residential / land:** flat **10%** of AV.

中文：自住住宅累进 0–32%；出租/空置住宅 12–36%；非住宅一律 10%。注意是按 AV（年值，约等于一年市场租金），不是房价。

---

### 8. Lease / Tenancy Stamp Duty (IRAS)

| Lease term | Formula |
|---|---|
| **≤ 4 years** | **0.4% × total rent** ( = 0.4% × AAR × years ) |
| **> 4 years / indefinite** | **0.4% × 4 × AAR** (multiplier capped at 4) |

- **AAR = total rent ÷ number of years.**
- **AAR ≤ $1,000 → EXEMPT.**
- Result rounded **down** to nearest dollar; **minimum duty $1**.
- Pay within **14 days** (signed in SG) / **30 days** (signed overseas).
- **Tenant pays by convention**, but both parties are **jointly liable**.

中文：≤4 年=0.4%×总租金；>4 年=0.4%×4×年均租金（倍数封顶 4）。年均租金 ≤$1,000 免税；惯例由租客付，但双方共同负责。

---

### 9. GST (IRAS)
- **Rate = 9%** (since 1 Jan 2024; no change in 2026).
- **Residential** property sale & lease = **EXEMPT supply** (no GST).
- **Commercial & industrial** property = **standard-rated 9%**.
- **Furnished residential:** GST applies to **movable** furniture/fittings; **fixtures** are exempt with the property.

中文：GST 9%；住宅免税，商业/工业 9%；家具（动产）要 GST，固定装置随住宅免税。

## (c) Common Exam Traps / 易错点

1. **BSD ≠ buyer-dependent.** BSD is identical for SC/SPR/FR/company. Only **ABSD** varies by profile/count. *(Classic distractor swaps these.)*
2. **Tax base = HIGHER of price or market value** — never the lower, never "the price stated".
3. **ABSD is FLAT on the whole value; BSD is TIERED.** Don't apply BSD-style tiers to ABSD.
4. **ABSD FTA exemption = US + Iceland + Liechtenstein + Norway + Switzerland** (treated as SC). Easy to confuse with EU/ASEAN — they are NOT exempt.
5. **SSD holding period depends on PURCHASE date.** On/after 4 Jul 2025 → **4 years, 16/12/8/4%**. Before that → 3 years, 12/8/4%. Question will hide the buy date.
6. **MSR (30%) applies ONLY to HDB flats and EC bought from a developer** — NOT to private property and NOT to resale EC bought from the open market.
7. **Loan granted = the LOWEST of LTV, TDSR, MSR caps**, not whichever you computed first.
8. **Stress-test rate 4% (residential) / 5% (non-residential)** is used to size the loan — not the actual promo interest rate (unless actual is higher).
9. **Property tax is on AV, not purchase price.** And owner-occupier ≠ non-owner-occupier rates.
10. **Lease stamp duty multiplier caps at 4** for leases >4 years — candidates wrongly multiply by the full term (e.g. 6 years).
11. **AAR ≤ $1,000 → exempt** (commonly missed in low-rent scenarios).
12. **GST: residential is EXEMPT.** Distractors apply 9% to a residential sale.
13. **"EXCEPT / NOT / which is FALSE" inversions** — re-read the stem; these flip the correct answer.
14. **Commission is negotiable** — any option stating a "CEA-fixed rate" is wrong.

## (d) Worked Examples / 计算例题

### Example 1 — BSD (residential, $1,200,000)
```
1% × 180,000   = 1,800
2% × 180,000   = 3,600
3% × 640,000   = 19,200
4% × 200,000   = 8,000   (1,200,000 − 1,000,000 = 200,000 in the 4% band)
                ---------
BSD             = $32,600
```
Shortcut: `4% × 1,200,000 − 15,400 = 48,000 − 15,400 = $32,600`. ✅

### Example 2 — ABSD (SPR buying 2nd residential property, $1,500,000)
ABSD (SPR, 2nd) = **30% flat** on whole value.
`30% × 1,500,000 = $450,000`.
(Plus BSD `4% × 1,500,000 − 15,400 = $44,600`. **Total stamp duty = $494,600**.)
中文：ABSD 按整额单一税率，与 BSD 分开计后相加。

### Example 3 — ABSD (Foreigner, 1st residential property, $2,000,000)
Foreigner = **60% flat** on any residential purchase.
`60% × 2,000,000 = $1,200,000` ABSD.
*If the buyer were a US/Norway/Iceland/Liechtenstein/Switzerland national → treated as SC 1st property = **0% ABSD**.*

### Example 4 — SSD (bought 1 Aug 2025 for $1.4m, sold 18 months later)
Purchase on/after 4 Jul 2025 → **new 4-year schedule**. Holding period >1–2 yrs → **12%**.
`12% × 1,400,000 = $168,000`.
*Same facts but bought 1 Aug 2024 → old 3-year schedule, >1–2 yrs = 8% → `$112,000`.* (Buy date changes the answer.)

### Example 5 — LTV / cash down (1st bank loan, $800,000 property, conditions met)
Max loan = `75% × 800,000 = $600,000`.
Min cash = `5% × 800,000 = $40,000`. Balance `20% × 800,000 = $160,000` from CPF/cash.

### Example 6 — TDSR loan check
Gross monthly income $10,000; existing debts $1,500/mo.
TDSR ceiling = `55% × 10,000 = $5,500`. Available for new mortgage = `5,500 − 1,500 = $4,000/mo` (computed at the **4%** stress-test rate, not the promo rate).

### Example 7 — MSR (HDB) check
Gross income $6,000/mo, buying HDB resale.
MSR ceiling = `30% × 6,000 = $1,800/mo` for the housing loan. (TDSR 55% also applies; the **lower** binding constraint governs.)

### Example 8 — Property tax (owner-occupier, AV = $60,000)
```
First 12,000   × 0%  = 0
12k–40k (28k)  × 4%  = 1,120
40k–50k (10k)  × 6%  = 600
50k–60k (10k)  × 10% = 1,000
                       ------
Annual property tax  = $2,720
```
*Same AV but non-owner-occupied:* `12% × 30,000 + 20% × 15,000 + 28% × 15,000 = 3,600 + 3,000 + 4,200 = $10,800`. (Huge gap — a favourite comparison question.)

### Example 9 — Lease stamp duty (≤4 yr)
Rent $3,000/mo, 2-year lease. Total rent = `3,000 × 24 = 72,000`.
Duty = `0.4% × 72,000 = $288`.
*Quick check:* `$3,000 × 12 = 36,000` per year → 1-year lease would be `0.4% × 36,000 = $144`.

### Example 10 — Lease stamp duty (>4 yr, multiplier capped)
Rent $5,000/mo, **6-year** lease. AAR = `5,000 × 12 = 60,000`.
Duty = `0.4% × 4 × 60,000 = $960` (multiply by **4**, NOT 6).

### Example 11 — GST on commercial purchase
Commercial unit $2,000,000 (GST-registered seller). GST = `9% × 2,000,000 = $180,000`. *(A residential unit at the same price = $0 GST — exempt supply.)*

## 速记总结 / Quick Recall

- **BSD** — same for everyone, **tiered 1→6%** (non-resi max 5%), on **higher of price/value**. Shortcut $1m–1.5m: `4%×P − 15,400`.
- **ABSD** — residential only, **flat**, on top of BSD: SC **0/20/30** · SPR **5/30/35** · FR **60** · Entity/Trustee **65**. FTA-exempt = **US + Iceland + Liechtenstein + Norway + Switzerland** → treated as SC.
- **SSD** — bought **on/after 4 Jul 2025**: **4 yrs, 16/12/8/4%**; bought before: 3 yrs, 12/8/4%. Industrial: 15/10/5% over 3 yrs.
- **LTV** — 1st **75%** · 2nd **45%** · 3rd+ **35%**; min cash 5% (1st) / 25% (2nd+). HDB concessionary = 75%.
- **TDSR 55%** (all debts, all loans) · **MSR 30%** (HDB & developer-EC only) · stress test **4%** resi / **5%** non-resi. Loan = **lowest** binding cap.
- **Commission** — negotiable, NOT CEA-fixed; dual representation = offence.
- **Property tax** — on **AV**: owner-occupier **0→32%**, non-owner **12→36%**, non-residential flat **10%**.
- **Lease SD** — ≤4 yr: **0.4% × total rent**; >4 yr: **0.4% × 4 × AAR** (cap ×4). **AAR ≤ $1,000 = exempt**. Pay in 14 days (SG).
- **GST = 9%**; residential **EXEMPT**, commercial/industrial **9%**, furniture taxable.
- **Golden rules:** higher of price/value · BSD tiered & ABSD flat · BSD universal & ABSD profile-based · SSD by purchase date · loan = lowest cap · property tax on AV · lease cap ×4 · read NOT/EXCEPT.
